The Finance Policy Manual is the HQMC RFF policy reference for Marine Corps
pay, travel, fiscal, and internal control operations. Four volumes:
[Volume 1 Pay Policies](/citations/fpm-vol-1),
[Volume 2 Travel Policies](/citations/fpm-vol-2),
[Volume 3 Fiscal Policies](/citations/fpm-vol-3), and
[Volume 4 Internal Control](/citations/fpm-vol-4).
Volume-level citations resolve to the volume entries. Document-level
citations land here.

## Access notes

The Finance Policy Manual is published by the HQMC Finance Branch (RFF) with
distribution marked DoD community only. No public marines.mil URL exists.
Pull the working copy through the HQMC RFF portal or the servicing finance
office. This entry intentionally carries no externalUrl so citation chips
route here instead of hitting an authentication wall.
