## Scope

MCO 3150.4A, Marine Corps Diving Policy and Program Administration, signed 2015-10-22, establishes policy for the conduct and administration of Marine Corps diving programs. DoD executive agents set the policy frame per references (a) through (ae) of the order. Reference (a) assigns the Assistant Secretary of Defense for Special Operations and Low-Intensity Conflict, Department of the Navy, as the DoD Diving Proponent and assigns the Secretary of the Navy as the DoD executive agent for Joint Service Military Diving Technology and Training (MDT&T). For the S-1 community the order is the substantiation authority for diving duty pay.

## Audience

- Commanders of units with diving billets, who issue diving duty assignment orders.
- Dive lockers and training sections tracking annual re-certification.
- S-1 and IPAC Marines (0102, 0111, 0170) reporting dive pay in MCTFS.
- Reporting and supporting unit admin sections exchanging substantiation documents.

## Chapter map

Repo evidence names one chapter.

- Chapter 5 paragraphs 5 and 6. Diving duty assignment orders and dive pay substantiation, cited across MCAAT special and hazardous duty pay checks.

## Operative content

- Dive pay reports in accordance with DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapter 11 and MCO 3150.4A Chapter 5 paragraphs 5 and 6. MCAAT audits this at IPAC, reporting unit, and supporting unit levels.
- Marines participating in diving duty are assigned by competent orders. Units maintain the orders on file to substantiate payment of dive pay.
- Supporting units submit assignment orders substantiating dive pay to the reporting unit no later than 5 days after the annual re-certification, per MCTFSPRIUM 1-10100.6.
- MCTFS reporting runs through MCTFSPRIUM 9-90501 and 1-10111.

## Connection to higher policy

Reference (a) assigns diving proponency to ASD(SO/LIC) and executive agency for MDT&T to the Secretary of the Navy. DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapter 11 sets the diving duty pay entitlement the order substantiates. MCO 1000.6 Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraphs 4 through 6 govern the related assignment orders.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- MCAAT Special and Hazardous Duty Pay program, sponsor DCS M&RA. Audits dive pay substantiation and reporting.
- MARADMIN 601/24. Appears alongside the order in MCAAT dive pay assignment order checks.
- MARADMIN 366/09, signed 2009-06-16. Announced publication of the base order MCO 3150.4.
- The portal's diving duty pay page covers the entitlement mechanics, including the three-HDIP-per-month cap under DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapters 11 and 24.
