## Scope

MCO 3120.12A establishes the Marine Corps Global Force Management (GFM) and Force Synchronization framework. The order implements DoD Global Force Management Implementation Guidance (GFMIG), the Secretary of Defense Forces For (SECDEF Forces For) processes, the Global Force Management Allocation Plan (GFMAP), and Joint Staff allocation procedures at the Marine Corps level.

## Audience

MARFOR commands. MAGTF commanders. HQMC PP&O staff. Marine Corps GFM coordinators. S-3 operations officers supporting GFM tasking.

## Content scope

- Marine Corps GFM framework and tasking process.
- SECDEF Forces For and Force Allocation procedures.
- Joint Staff GFM coordination.
- Force synchronization across MARFORs.
- Sourcing recommendations and feasibility.
- Operational and ad-hoc tasking.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 3000.18B. Marine Corps Force Deployment Planning and Execution.
- MCO 3000.13B. Marine Corps Readiness Reporting.
- DoD Global Force Management Implementation Guidance.
- Joint Staff GFM procedures.

## Status

Active. HQMC PP&O sponsors. GFM and Force Synchronization Manual enclosure provides operational detail.
