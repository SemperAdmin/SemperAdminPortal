## Scope

SECNAVINST 5430.57H establishes the mission and functions of the Naval Inspector General (NAVIG / NAVINSGEN). The instruction sets NAVIG authority to inspect, investigate, and inquire into matters of Department of the Navy importance, reporting to the Secretary of the Navy as the Secretary's representative. The Marine Corps Deputy Naval Inspector General (DNIG / IGMC) is a Marine Corps General Officer.

## Coverage

- NAVIG mission, structure, and reporting authority.
- Inspection, investigation, and inquiry authorities under 10 USC 8014, 8020, 8032, 8042.
- DNIG / IGMC and DNIG / IGMC Deputy responsibilities for the Marine Corps.
- Authority to request DON military and civilian personnel for temporary assignments.
- Relationships, responsibilities, acronyms, and definitions per Enclosures (1)-(4).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- IGMC inspections and the IGMC Functional Area checklists derive authority from this instruction.
- MCAAT inspections at the Marine Corps level run under the IGMC framework set by SECNAVINST 5430.57H.

## Note on supersession

SECNAVINST 5430.57H cancels SECNAVINST 5430.57G. The 5430.57G instruction was the prior NCIS Mission and Functions instruction. The current NCIS Mission and Functions instruction is SECNAVINST 5430.107 series. Citations to NCIS authority should reference SECNAVINST 5430.107, not 5430.57H.
