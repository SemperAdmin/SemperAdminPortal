## Scope

SECNAVINST 5430.57H establishes the mission and functions of the Naval Inspector General (NAVIG / NAVINSGEN). The instruction sets NAVIG authority to inspect, investigate, and inquire into matters of Department of the Navy importance, reporting to the Secretary of the Navy as the Secretary's representative. The Marine Corps Deputy Naval Inspector General (DNIG / IGMC) is a Marine Corps General Officer. Effective 2019-12-17.

## Audience

- Commanders responding to IGMC and Commanding General's Inspection Program (CGIP) inspections. The portal commander inspections lifecycle page lists this instruction in its reference set.
- Inspectors general and inspection staff at Marine Corps commands.
- Staff building self-audit posture. The portal commander audit posture page lists this instruction beside the IGMC CoRE and CoRE+ Functional Area Checklist framework.

## Coverage

- NAVIG mission, structure, and reporting authority.
- Inspection, investigation, and inquiry authorities under 10 USC 8014, 8020, 8032, 8042.
- DNIG / IGMC and DNIG / IGMC Deputy responsibilities for the Marine Corps.
- Authority to request DON military and civilian personnel for temporary assignments.
- Relationships, responsibilities, acronyms, and definitions per Enclosures (1)-(4).

## Operative content

- The portal commander inspections lifecycle page pairs this instruction with MCO 5040.6K and 10 USC 8020, the statute designating the Inspector General of the Marine Corps as the eyes and ears of the Commandant.
- MCO 5040.6K, the IGMC Inspections Program order, lists 10 USC 8020 among its authorities. IGMC inspections run under Title 10 authority on behalf of the Commandant of the Marine Corps.
- The commander-facing inspection cycle the portal documents, CGIP standing requirement, Final Inspection Report receipt, and Corrective Action Report submission within 30 calendar days, operates inside the IG framework this instruction anchors.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- IGMC inspections and the IGMC Functional Area checklists derive authority from this instruction.
- MCAAT inspections at the Marine Corps level run under the IGMC framework set by SECNAVINST 5430.57H.
- MCO 5040.6K promulgates the IGMC Inspections Program. Its registry entry lists 10 USC 8020, SECNAVINST 5040.3B, and MCO 5430.1A as related authorities.

## Note on supersession

SECNAVINST 5430.57H cancels SECNAVINST 5430.57G. The 5430.57G instruction was the prior NCIS Mission and Functions instruction. The current NCIS Mission and Functions instruction is SECNAVINST 5430.107 series. Citations to NCIS authority should reference SECNAVINST 5430.107, not 5430.57H.
