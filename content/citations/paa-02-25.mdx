## Scope

This advisory addresses Amplifying Administrative Guidance On MARADMIN 128-25 Initial Guidance To Prioritizing Military Excellence And Readiness. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. All current PAAs are located under the reference tab of the MISSA portal. Request addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate organizations/units as applicable.
2. This advisory delineates administrative procedures for Marines to request for voluntary separation, and follow-on administrative actions once the request is submitted.
3. Request for Voluntary Separation
4. Voluntary Separation Pay
5. Waivers 5.a. Marines requesting voluntary separation may request a waiver for retention if requirements outlined in references (a) and (c) are met.
6. Administrative Absence
7. The point of contact regarding this matter is GySgt Bennamon at (703) 784-9632 or via e-mail at jonathan.bennamon@usmc.mil. MMSR-2 Enlisted separations at (703) 784-9322, MMSR-2 Officer separations at (703) 784-9324.
