## Scope

PAA 02-25 amplifies MARADMIN 128-25, Initial Guidance To Prioritizing Military Excellence And Readiness. The advisory delineates administrative procedures for Marines requesting voluntary separation and the follow-on administrative actions once the request is submitted. It covers the separation request itself, voluntary separation pay, retention waivers, and administrative absence. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.
- MMSR-2 enlisted and officer separation sections at HQMC.

## Action language

- Paragraph 1 places all current PAAs under the reference tab of the MISSA portal and requests addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate organizations/units as applicable.
- Paragraph 2 states the purpose. This advisory delineates administrative procedures for Marines to request for voluntary separation, and follow-on administrative actions once the request is submitted.
- Paragraph 3 covers the request for voluntary separation.
- Paragraph 4 covers voluntary separation pay.
- Paragraph 5.a covers waivers. Marines requesting voluntary separation are permitted to request a retention waiver when the requirements outlined in references (a) and (c) are met (paraphrased).
- Paragraph 6 covers administrative absence.
- Paragraph 7 names the points of contact. GySgt Bennamon at (703) 784-9632 or jonathan.bennamon@usmc.mil. MMSR-2 Enlisted separations at (703) 784-9322. MMSR-2 Officer separations at (703) 784-9324.
