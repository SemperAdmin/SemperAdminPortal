## Scope

MCO 1553.1 governs the Marine Corps training and education system. The current revision, MCO 1553.1C, The Marine Corps Training and Education System, was signed 2024-12-20. The archive copy on file, from the 2026-04-26 archive snapshot, is the current version, MCO 1553.1C. The opening section states the purpose.

> The Marine Corps training and education (T&E) system develops adaptive and decisive Marines imbued with a maneuver warfare philosophy and oriented on competition in dynamic naval environments. [...]
