## Scope

MCO 6320.4A, issued jointly with OPNAVINST 6320.7B on 2022-12-16, updates and reissues policy, prescribes procedures, and assigns responsibilities for the quality assurance of health care provided to the operating forces. The revision cancels MCO 6320.4 and OPNAVINST 6320.7A, deletes the professional qualifications and privileging enclosures in favor of BUMEDINST 6010.30, adds four provider types to the definitions, and adds twelve screening requirements across the medical and dental occurrence screening lists. The Secretary of the Navy holds policy oversight of the Clinical Quality Management Program within the Department of the Navy. BUMED-M3B62 is the program office of record.

## Audience

- Commanders of operating forces overseeing the quality of health care delivered to their Marines and Sailors.
- Medical and dental department staff executing quality assurance procedures.
- Appeal authorities and review authorities named in the order's enclosures.
- BUMED-M3B62 staff administering the program of record.

## Enclosure map

- Enclosure 1. Definitions.
- Enclosure 2. Operational Forces Medical Occurrence Screening List.
- Enclosure 3. Operational Forces Dental Occurrence Screening List.

## Operative content

- The order is the portal's citation of record for clinical quality assurance. The MCO P6320.13 clarification entry directs authors to cite MCO 6320.4 for clinical quality assurance topics.
- Occurrence screens for operational forces medical and dentistry carry their own enclosure.
- The commander oversight matrix lists the order among its command-program references.
