## Scope

MARADMIN 002/23 cancels six COVID-19 related MARADMINs published from 2020 through 2022. The cancellation removes the six messages from active reference and routes commanders to current force health protection guidance. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders. Discontinue reference to the six cancelled MARADMINs.
- S-1 staff and medical providers. Pull current force health protection guidance in place of the cancelled messages.

## Cancelled messages

- MARADMIN 754/20. COVID-19 Vaccine Guidance for Active and Reserve Components. The initial Marine Corps implementation of the DoD COVID-19 Vaccination Plan in support of Operation Warp Speed, covering vaccine offering, prioritization, distribution, execution, reporting, and force health protection posture. The registry entry records its source page as CANCELLED.
- MARADMIN 462/21, 533/21, 612/21, 733/21, and 464/22. COVID-19 framework updates cancelled with the base message.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD COVID-19 Vaccination Plan. MARADMIN 754/20 implemented the plan. This cancellation closes out the Marine Corps implementation messages.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 754/20. Registry entry maradmin-754-20 carries the full record.
- MARADMIN 192/20. COVID-19 Supplemental Personnel Guidance for Commanders. Portal COVID-19 record cross-referencing MARADMIN 754/20.

## Status

Released 2023-01-18 per the policy index. Originator CMC Washington DC. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force. Verification caution. The MCAAT military pay checklist cites MARADMIN 002/23 for Basic Needs Allowance recertification and reporting checks alongside DoDI 1341.15 and MCTFSPRIUM 8-81201, once paired with MARADMIN 369/25. The official subject of this message is the COVID-19 cancellations. Verify the intended Basic Needs Allowance message at the source before relying on those checklist citations.
