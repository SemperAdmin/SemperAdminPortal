## Scope

DODI 5160.70 establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and provides procedures for managing the Defense Language, Regional Expertise, and Culture (LREC) Program. The instruction reissues and cancels the 2007 LREC instruction. The instruction adds regional expertise and culture as mission-critical competencies of the LREC Program and establishes initial culture learning guidelines. The instruction operates under DoD Directives 5142.02 and 5160.41E.

## Audience

DoD Component Heads. Service language program managers. Marine Corps Intelligence Activity (MCIA) and Marine Corps language program managers. S-1 officers and IPAC pay clerks processing Foreign Language Proficiency Bonus (FLPB) transactions. Marines with language proficiency, regional expertise, or culture training requirements.

## Content scope

- Defense LREC Program management and oversight.
- Language proficiency testing framework (DLPT and OPI).
- Strategic-language designation process driving FLPB eligibility.
- Regional expertise and culture (REC) competencies.
- Component-level Service responsibilities for language programs.
- Reporting and assessment requirements.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 19 (FLPB). FLPB pay framework cross-references the LREC Program strategic-language designations.
- 37 U.S.C. 305a. Foreign Language Proficiency Bonus statutory authority.
- Marine Corps Language Strategic Plan. Marine Corps-specific language program implementation.
- Defense Language Institute Foreign Language Center (DLIFLC). DLPT testing facility.

## Status

Active. Effective 30 December 2016. The instruction is the current DoD-level LREC Program authority.
