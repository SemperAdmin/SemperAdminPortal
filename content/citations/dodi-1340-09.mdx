## Scope

DODI 1340.09 establishes DoD policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for the Hazard Pay (HzP) Program. The instruction was issued effective 26 January 2018 with Change 1 issued 2 February 2024. The instruction reissues and cancels the prior DODI 1340.09 (Hostile Fire Pay and Imminent Danger Pay, April 20, 2010), expanding the framework to a unified Hazard Pay Program covering Hostile Fire Pay (HFP), Imminent Danger Pay (IDP), and Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (HDIP) Other Than Flying. The instruction implements Sections 351 and 374 of Title 37, U.S.C.

## Audience

DoD Component Heads. Military Service pay officers. S-1 officers and IPAC pay clerks processing HFP, IDP, and HDIP transactions. Marines deploying to HFP/IDP-designated areas or performing HDIP-qualifying duties.

## Content scope

- Hostile Fire Pay (HFP) eligibility, certification, and rate framework.
- Imminent Danger Pay (IDP) eligibility, designated areas, and rate framework.
- Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (HDIP) Other Than Flying eligibility and rate framework.
- Hazard Pay Program oversight and administration.
- Pro-rata payment rules.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 10 (HFP and IDP). Pay framework.
- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 24 (HDIP Other Than Flying). Pay framework.
- 37 U.S.C. 351 (HFP and IDP statutory authority).
- 37 U.S.C. 374 (HDIP statutory authority).

## Status

Active. Change 1 issued 2 February 2024. The instruction is the current DoD-level Hazard Pay Program authority.
