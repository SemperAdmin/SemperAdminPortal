## Scope

SECNAVINST 5211.5F implements the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 USC 552a) inside the Department of the Navy. The instruction sets policy on the collection, maintenance, use, and disclosure of personally identifiable information (PII). The Marine Corps applies the policy across personnel administration, sponsorship coordination, command investigations, and records management.

## Structure

- Paragraph 1: Purpose.
- Paragraph 2: Cancellation.
- Paragraph 3: Definitions.
- Paragraph 4: Applicability.
- Paragraph 5: Policy.
- Paragraph 6: Responsibilities.
- Paragraph 7: Privacy Act.
- Paragraph 8: Records Management.
- Paragraph 9: Forms and Reports.
- Enclosure (1): References.
- Enclosure (2): Definitions.
- Enclosure (3): Responsibilities.

## Coverage

- Privacy Act Statement requirements when collecting PII (paragraph 7).
- Privacy Act Record of Disclosure tracking (paragraph 7).
- Routine uses and disclosure exceptions.
- System of Records Notice (SORN) framework.
- Records management for Privacy Act files (paragraph 8).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 1320.11H Marine Corps Sponsorship Program paragraph 5.c references SECNAVINST 5211.5F for PII handling.
- JAGINST 5800.7G Manual of the Judge Advocate General Chapter II section 0207.e references the Privacy Act framework for administrative investigations.
- MCO P1070.12K Individual Records Administration Manual incorporates Privacy Act handling.
