## Scope

MCO 1730.6 governs religious ministry in the Marine Corps. The current revision is MCO 1730.6G, signed 2024-05-28 and listed as Current on the MCPEL. The portal anchors commander religious ministry duties and chaplain-delivered resilience programming to this order. The archived text layer renders the opening line as follows, extraction imperfect.

> This Order implements reference (a). revision and should be reviewed in its entirety.

## Audience

- Commanders. Own the command religious ministry relationship and the oversight duties in the commander oversight matrix.
- Religious Ministry Teams and chaplains delivering ministry and confidential counsel.
- Marines and families using chaplain services and CREDO retreats.
- Leaders routing Marines to confidential chaplain support.

## Operative content

- The commander ensures the Religious Ministry Team has direct access to the commander, per the oversight matrix row.
- The commander provides a private, confidential workspace for the chaplain, per the same row.
- CREDO, the Chaplain Religious Enrichment Development Operation, provides regionally based religious ministry to build personal, relational, and spiritual resources for resilience, per the education services page.
- CREDO offerings include Personal Growth Retreats, Marriage Enrichment Retreats, and Family Enrichment Retreats, facilitated by trained chaplains and lay facilitators, confidential and voluntary.
