## Scope

MARADMIN 037/23 announces FY23 USMC Chief Warrant Officer Five through Chief Warrant Officer Three promotions for the December 2022 and January 2023 promotion months. The message lists Marines by name, EDIPI, and PMOS for promotion to CWO3, CWO4, and CWO5 following the FY23 CWO promotion selection board. Applicability is the Active Component CWO population.

## Audience

- CWOs named in the message. Execute the promotion sequence under the Promotion Manual.
- Career planners and unit S-1s. Process promotions through MCTFS unit diary entries.
- HQMC MMPB-10. Owns the lineal staff and promotion processing.

## Promotion announcement

- The message promotes selected CWOs to CWO3, CWO4, and CWO5 across the December 2022 and January 2023 promotion months.
- Each line item carries name, EDIPI, and PMOS.
- Promotion processing runs through HQMC Manpower Management Performance Branch (MMPB-10).

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO P1400.31D. Marine Corps Officer Promotion Manual. Governs officer and warrant officer promotion processing.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 628/23. FY26 Officer Promotion Selection Board schedule. Companion board-cycle message carrying eligibility, OMPF, and correspondence procedures.
- MCTFS. System of record for the promotion entries.
