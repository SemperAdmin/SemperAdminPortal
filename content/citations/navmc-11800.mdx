## What this form is

NAVMC 11800, Family Care Plan, revision 01-2023, electronic fillable (EF). The Family Care Plan form under MCO 1740.13, designating short-term and long-term caregivers and recording care details for dependents when the Marine is unavailable. The NAVMC forms index lists it at two pages, stock number NONSN00008728, digital fillable.

## Audience

The Marine completes and signs the form, certifying accuracy. Caregivers sign acknowledging responsibility. The unit FCP Coordinator (course code MFRFCPCOOR) counsels and reviews. The FCP Validator (course code MFRFCPVLDR) validates completeness before endorsement. The commanding officer endorses.

## Operative content per repo evidence

- Content captured. Marine identifying information, dependent information with DEERS status, short-term caregiver designation (typically 30 days or less, immediately available), long-term caregiver designation (deployment-length, sustained care), care details, logistics including powers of attorney, and the signature block.
- Trigger and clock. Single parents, dual-military couples with dependents, Marines with sole care responsibility for an adult dependent, and command-designated Marines complete an FCP within 60 days of the triggering event and recertify annually. Triggers include birth or adoption for a single Marine, divorce producing single-parent status, death of a spouse, marriage to another service member, new permanent dependent recognition, and PCS affecting caregiver arrangements.
- Consequences of noncompliance per the portal FCP page. Page 11 counseling, adverse FITREP exposure, deployment ineligibility, and administrative separation for continued noncompliance.

## Relationship to the MOL FCP module

Per MCO 1740.13D, Marine Online became the day-to-day FCP record store effective 2018-01-26, and FCPs validated before then on the NAVMC 11800 stayed valid until their next required renewal. The form remains in active revision, most recently January 2023, and the MOL FCP module remains the submission path for new and recertified plans. Marines complete the NAVMC 11800 fields inside the module, upload supporting documents, submit for endorsement, and track validation status.
