## What this form is

NAVMC 11800, Family Care Plan, revision 01-2023, electronic fillable (EF). The Family Care Plan form under MCO 1740.13, designating short-term and long-term caregivers and recording care details for dependents when the Marine is unavailable.

## Relationship to the MOL FCP module

Per MCO 1740.13D, Marine Online became the day-to-day FCP record store effective 2018-01-26, and FCPs validated before then on the NAVMC 11800 stayed valid until their next required renewal. The form remains in active revision, most recently January 2023, and the MOL FCP module remains the submission path for new and recertified plans.

## When Marines use it

Single parents, dual-military couples with dependents, and Marines with sole care responsibility for an adult dependent complete an FCP within 60 days of the triggering event and recertify annually. The FCP Coordinator and Validator manage unit-level processing through the MOL FCP module.
