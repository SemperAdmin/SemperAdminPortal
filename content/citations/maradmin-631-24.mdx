## Scope

MARADMIN 631/24 publishes updated EPME requirements for the Selected Marine Corps Reserve (SMCR). Released 2024-12-23 by CG TECOM as the companion to MARADMIN 630/24 for the Active Component and Active Reserve. The message sets the resident and non-resident course completion requirements by grade for SMCR Marines. Applicability is the SMCR.

## Audience

- SMCR enlisted Marines. Apply EPME requirements by grade.
- SMCR career planners. Track EPME completion for promotion eligibility.
- Reserve unit commanders and S-1s. Confirm PME completeness before promotion boards.

## Requirements the portal asserts

- Grade requirements. The portal's PME promotion guidance applies MARADMINs 630/24 and 631/24 together for the specific PME requirements by grade for SSgt and above.
- Board deadline. Marines must be PME complete by 2359 EST one day prior to the board convening to count as PME complete for the board.
- Sequenced PME boundary. The 270-day sequenced PME completion window from MARADMIN 222/26 applies to the Active Component and Active Reserve only. The portal records SMCR Marines as outside the shortened window.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 1553.4B. Professional Military Education (PME). Parent order for EPME requirements.
- MCO P1400.32D. Cited alongside this message in the portal's PME promotion guidance.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 630/24. Active Duty and Active Reserve EPME companion released the same day.
