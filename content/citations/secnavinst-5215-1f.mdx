## Scope

SECNAVINST 5215.1F establishes Secretary of the Navy Directives Policy. The instruction sets policy on creation, format, coordination, approval, publication, and lifecycle management of SECNAV-level directives. The Marine Corps implements through MCO 5215.1K (Marine Corps Directives Management Program). SECNAV M-5215.1 operationalizes the SECNAVINST 5215.1F policy with detailed procedures. Effective 2020-08-10.

## Audience

- Directives Control Points and directives managers at every echelon.
- S-1 officers and clerks drafting, staffing, and routing command directives under MCO 5215.1K.
- Commanders and adjutants signing and promulgating directives.

## Structure

- Paragraph 1: Purpose.
- Paragraph 2: Cancellation.
- Paragraph 3: Applicability.
- Paragraph 4: Policy.
- Paragraph 5: Responsibilities.
- Paragraph 6: Records Management.
- Enclosure (1): Directives Management Requirements and Standards.
- Enclosure (2): Directives Authority.

## Coverage

- SECNAV directive types and lifecycle (paragraph 4 Policy, Enclosure 1 Requirements and Standards).
- Directives Authority responsibilities (Enclosure 2).
- Coordination, signature, and promulgation procedures.
- Records management for directive issuances (paragraph 6).

## Operative content

- The portal admin directives management overview cites paragraphs 4-5 (Policy, Responsibilities) and Enclosures (1)-(2), dated 2020-08-10, as the DON-level rules over MCO 5215.1K Enclosure (1) Chapters 1 through 4 (directives administrative procedures, MCO format, MCBul format, Joint Directive format).
- The same page stacks the authority chain as DODI 5025.01 Sections 1, 4, and 5 at DoD level, SECNAVINST 5215.1F and SECNAV M-5215.1 at DON level, and MCO 5215.1K at Marine Corps level.
- The directives processing workflow maps to NAVMC 3500.3E T&R events 0102-GENA-1004 (Supervise command issued directive development), 0102-GENA-1005 (Supervise DCP), 0111-GENA-2007 (Maintain DCP), and 0111-GENA-2105 (Supervise DCP). The IGMC Directives Management Functional Area checklist provides the audit baseline.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 5215.1K Marine Corps Directives Management Program, carrying Admin CH-3 dated 2025-09-29, implements SECNAVINST 5215.1F inside the Marine Corps.
- SECNAV M-5215.1 DON Directives Issuance System Manual operationalizes SECNAVINST 5215.1F.
- SECNAV M-5210.2 supplies the SSIC table the directive numbering system uses.
- MCO 5210.11F covers records management across the directives lifecycle.
- DODI 5025.01 DoD Issuances Program sets the parent DoD framework.
