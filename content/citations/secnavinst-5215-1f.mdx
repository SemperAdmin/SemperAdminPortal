## Scope

SECNAVINST 5215.1F establishes Secretary of the Navy Directives Policy. The instruction sets policy on creation, format, coordination, approval, publication, and lifecycle management of SECNAV-level directives. The Marine Corps implements through MCO 5215.1K (Marine Corps Directives Management Program). SECNAV M-5215.1 operationalizes the SECNAVINST 5215.1F policy with detailed procedures.

## Structure

- Paragraph 1: Purpose.
- Paragraph 2: Cancellation.
- Paragraph 3: Applicability.
- Paragraph 4: Policy.
- Paragraph 5: Responsibilities.
- Paragraph 6: Records Management.
- Enclosure (1): Directives Management Requirements and Standards.
- Enclosure (2): Directives Authority.

## Coverage

- SECNAV directive types and lifecycle (paragraph 4 Policy, Enclosure 1 Requirements and Standards).
- Directives Authority responsibilities (Enclosure 2).
- Coordination, signature, and promulgation procedures.
- Records management for directive issuances (paragraph 6).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 5215.1K Marine Corps Directives Management Program implements SECNAVINST 5215.1F inside the Marine Corps.
- SECNAV M-5215.1 DON Directives Issuance System Manual operationalizes SECNAVINST 5215.1F.
- DODI 5025.01 DoD Issuances Program sets the parent DoD framework.

## Status

Active. Implements DODI 5025.01. Distribution Statement A.
