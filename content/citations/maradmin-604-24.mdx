## Scope

MARADMIN 604/24 updates MARADMIN 377/24 with revised FY25 class dates and registration guidance for Cornerstone, the Commandant's Combined Commandership Course. The Lejeune Leadership Institute (LLI) at Marine Corps University Quantico runs the program.

## Audience

Commanding officers and senior enlisted attending Cornerstone. Cornerstone spouses. Career planners and S-1 personnel coordinating attendance. The 8999 Programs Lead at the Marine Corps Senior Enlisted Academy handles SNCO attendance questions.

## Updated Spouses Workshop course dates

- Course 2025002. Report 16 March 2025. Convene 17 March 2025. Graduate 21 March 2025.

## Registration

- Attendees must register no later than 45 days prior to the desired course report date.
- Cutoff for 25-2 was 31 January 2025. Cutoff for 25-3 was 13 March 2025.
- Registration form lives at https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/h99fFW7rLg.
- LLI registers attendees into Marine Corps Training Information Management System (MCTIMS) after the form lands.
- Attendees who registered before this MARADMIN do not need to reregister.

## Spouses attending Cornerstone

All active component spouses attending Cornerstone, except active-duty military spouses, must sign and return DD Form 2793 (Volunteer Agreement for Appropriated Fund Activities) no later than 45 days prior to travel. LLI provides the DD Form 2793 by email after registration.

## Authority

Commanding General, Education Command and President, Marine Corps University publish the MARADMIN. The Lejeune Leadership Institute administers Cornerstone.

## Status

Active. Released by Brigadier General Matthew W. Tracy, President, Marine Corps University.
