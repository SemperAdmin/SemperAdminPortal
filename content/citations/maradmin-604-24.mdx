## Scope

MARADMIN 604/24 updates MARADMIN 377/24 with revised fiscal year 2025 class dates and registration guidance for Cornerstone, the Commandant's Combined Commandership Course. The Lejeune Leadership Institute at Marine Corps University Quantico runs the program.

## Audience

- Commanding officers and senior enlisted leaders attending Cornerstone.
- Cornerstone spouses attending the Spouses Workshop.
- Career planners and S-1 personnel coordinating attendance and registration.
- The 8999 Programs Lead at the Marine Corps Senior Enlisted Academy for SNCO attendance.

## Action items

- Updated Spouses Workshop dates for course 2025002. Report 2025-03-16, convene 2025-03-17, graduate 2025-03-21.
- Register no later than 45 days before the desired course report date. Cutoff for class 25-2, 2025-01-31. Cutoff for class 25-3, 2025-03-13.
- The LLI registers attendees into MCTIMS. Marines already registered do not reregister.
- Active-component spouses, except active-duty military spouses, sign and return DD Form 2793, the volunteer agreement for appropriated fund activities, no later than 45 days before travel.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MARADMIN 377/24. The FY25 Cornerstone class schedule this message changes.
- MCTIMS. The training system of record for registration.
