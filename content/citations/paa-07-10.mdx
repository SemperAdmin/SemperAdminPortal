## Scope

PAA 07-10 covers six topics in a single advisory. The advisory updates MCTFSPRIUM paragraph 51201 Training Events (TTC 483), applies interim changes to IRAM paragraphs 4006.3d, 5000.3, and 5002, lifts the commander signature requirement on SGVL-8285, and clarifies approval and delegation authority for Flight Deck Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (FDHDIP). PAAs 01-00 through 06-10 remain in effect.

## Audience

- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists running MCTFS unit diary entries.
- Unit training sections reporting pre-deployment training blocks.
- S-1 desks processing SGV (Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance) elections.
- Pay clerks adjudicating Flight Deck Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay.
- IRAM compliance leads.

## Content scope

- MCTFSPRIUM paragraph 51201 Training Events (TTC 483) update. New TTC codes for pre-deployment training Blocks 1A, 1B, 2, 3, and 4 under MCO 3502.6 Marine Corps Force Generation Process. New TTC codes for Suicide Prevention Program courses (SNCO, Officer, Junior Marine, NCO Refresher).
- Interim change to IRAM paragraph 4006.3d.
- Interim change to IRAM paragraph 5000.3.
- Interim change to IRAM paragraph 5002.
- SGVL-8285 procedure change. Commander's signature no longer required.
- Clarification of approval and delegation authority for FDHDIP.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 3502.6. Marine Corps Force Generation Process. Source policy for the pre-deployment training block framework.
- MCO P1070.12K (IRAM). Parent policy for the IRAM interim changes.
- OPNAVINST 7220.4L. Statutory framework on incentive pays including FDHDIP.
- MCTFSPRIUM. Source of unit diary reporting paragraphs.
