## Scope

This advisory addresses Administrative Procedure For Processing SMCR, IMA And IRR Marines Refusing COVID-19 Vaccination While Serving In A Duty Status. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. References (a) through (c) remain in effect. Request addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate commands.
2. This advisory provides clarifying guidance to administrators for administrative responsibilities and processing of Selective Marine Corps Reserve (SMCR), Individual Mobilization Augmentee (IMA), and IRR Marines (serving in a duty status) refusing the COVID-19 vaccination in accordance with reference (a).
3. Background. In accordance with references (a) through (c), all Active Duty and Reserve Marines in a drilling status or serving on orders are mandated to receive the COVID-19 vaccination.
4. Action. 4.a. IAW reference (d), Active Component (AC) Commanders are responsible for IMA Marines assigned to a billet within their command in the same manner they are responsible for the active duty Marines within their command.
