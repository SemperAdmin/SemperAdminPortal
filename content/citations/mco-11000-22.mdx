## Scope

MCO 11000.22, MARINE CORPS BACHELOR AND FAMILY HOUSING MANAGEMENT, establishes Marine Corps policy and prescribes requirements governing the management and administration of the Marine Corps Bachelor and Family Housing enterprise. The portal files it under Unaccompanied Housing because commander and admin surfaces reach it for UH duties. The order largely incorporates the prior MCO 11000.1 content, and the "MCO 11000.1" alias resolves to this entry so historical references in commander and admin pages continue to render the correct chip.

> This Order establishes Marine Corps policy and prescribes requirements governing the management and administration of the Marine Corps Bachelor and Family Housing enterprise. Recent investments in Family and Bachelor Housing infrastructure have significantly improved the quality of life for Marines and their families. [...]

Quoted from the document's opening section.

## Audience

- Commanders who establish unaccompanied housing management policies and approve BAH Without Dependents and CNAs.
- Installation housing offices under MCICOM (GF), the sponsor of both housing functional areas.
- S-1 and PAC personnel processing BAH without dependents transactions.
- IGMC inspectors working the Unaccompanied Housing and Family Housing functional area checklists.

## Content scope

Repo evidence names no chapter map for the 2018 order. Inspection cites for BAH without dependents land in paragraphs 2600.2 and 2600.3 of the older MCO P11000.22 W CH 1-6, the Marine Corps Housing Management Manual sharing the same number.

## Operative content

- Commanders establish UH management policies and approve BAH Without Dependents and CNAs, per the commander oversight matrix summarizing the IGMC functional area checklist.
- BAH without dependents requires approval by the appropriate command authority, defined by the local or installation BAH without policy. MCO P11000.22 W CH 1-6 paragraphs 2600.2 and 2600.3, with DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 26.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD 7000.14-R Financial Management Regulation Volume 7A, Chapter 26. The BAH entitlement rules behind the approval requirement.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- MCO 11000.1. The superseded unaccompanied housing order, still named in the IGMC registry as functional area 11000.1, Unaccompanied Housing, CoRE, sponsor MCICOM (GF), effective 2024-03-06.
- Family Housing functional area 11000.22. Non-CoRE, sponsor MCICOM (GF), effective 2024-03-19.
- MCO P11000.22 Marine Corps Housing Management Manual. The older manual with the same number, CH 4 announced by MARADMIN 302/04 on 2004-07-09, still cited in MCAAT milpay items.
- MARADMINs 429/11 and 015/20. Cited beside the BAH without dependents approval items.
- MCTFSPRIUM Chapter 8 Section 2 and Chapter 13 Appendix C. The reporting side of BAH transactions.
