## Scope

Volume 4 of DoD Financial Management Regulation 7000.14-R is the Accounting Policy volume. The official FMR index lists the volume title as Accounting Policy, published by the Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller). The volume sets DoD accounting policy for assets, liabilities, net position, revenue, expenses, cost accounting, and specialized asset classes.

## Audience

Accounting and comptroller personnel across DoD components. Financial managers preparing component financial statements. Fiscal staff tracking receivables, payables, and property accounting. Portal role pages do not cite Volume 4 directly. The registry holds the volume for reference resolution from inspection checklists.
