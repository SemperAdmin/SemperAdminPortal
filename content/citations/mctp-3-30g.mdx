## Scope

MCTP 3-30G (Marine Corps Tactical Publication) covers Marine Corps Manpower and Personnel Administration. The publication provides doctrinal reference for personnel administration across the MAGTF including manpower planning, personnel readiness, casualty reporting, replacement operations, and personnel support during deployments and operations.

## Audience

MAGTF commanders. S-1 officers and S-1 chiefs. G-1 staff. Personnel administration MOSs (0102, 0111, 0170). Manpower planners at MARFOR and HQMC level.

## Content scope

- Manpower planning at the MAGTF level.
- Personnel readiness within the deployment cycle.
- Casualty reporting and replacement operations.
- Personnel support during operations.
- Strength accounting at the operational level.
- Coordination across S-1, G-1, and HQMC manpower.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 3000.18B. Marine Corps Force Deployment Planning and Execution.
- MCO 3000.13B. Marine Corps Readiness Reporting.
- MCO P3500.72A. Marine Corps Ground T&R Manual.
- MCO 1300.8. Marine Corps Personnel Assignment Policy.
- NAVMC 3500.3E. T-R event mapping for personnel administration.

## Status

Active. Marine Corps doctrinal reference for personnel administration at the MAGTF level.
