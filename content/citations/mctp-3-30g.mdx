## Scope

MCTP 3-30G, Marine Corps Manpower and Personnel Administration, is the doctrinal publication for personnel administration across the MAGTF. Where the MCAP assigns responsibilities and the PRIUM formats transactions, this publication supplies the doctrine, how manpower planning, personnel readiness, casualty replacement, and personnel support fit the fight. The function codes it defines structure the portal's admin content and the T&R events admin Marines train to.

## Audience

- MAGTF commanders and G-1 staffs planning personnel support for operations.
- S-1 officers and chiefs applying the four functions to the unit mission.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 Marines, whose T&R event identifiers carry this publication's function codes.
- Manpower planners at MARFOR and HQMC.

## Chapter map

- Chapter 1. Fundamentals. Mission, principles, advocacy, and the administrative cycle framed as six lines of operation from requirement development through requirement redeployment. Table 1-1 applies the lines of operation across awards, staffing, evaluations, pay, and the other admin tasks.
- Chapter 2. Functions and Components. The four administrative functions, General Administration (GENA), Operational Administration (OPER), Manpower Administration (MPMN), and Personnel Administration (PERA), the three components, and the active and reserve component framing.

## Operative content

- The four function codes appear in NAVMC 3500.3E T&R event identifiers, so the doctrine reaches every admin training standard.
- The MAGTF S-1 supports personnel administration across all four elements, command, ground combat, aviation combat, and logistics combat.
- Deployment transactions, individual augment fills, dependency separation actions, and pre-deployment certifications process through UD/MIPS under S-1 cognizance.
- Casualty replacement restores unit personnel readiness, paired with MCO 3040.4 casualty procedures.
- Redeployment transactions process at the home station on return.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 5000.14D MCAP. The order assigning the responsibilities this publication frames doctrinally.
- MCO 3000.18B and MCO 3120.12A. Force deployment planning and global force management, the operational context.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- NAVMC 3500.3E. T&R events carrying the GENA, OPER, MPMN, and PERA codes.
- MCO 1300.8. Assignment policy the MPMN function executes.
- MCO 3000.13B. Readiness reporting fed by the personnel pillar.
- MCO 3040.4 and MCTFS PRIUM. Casualty procedures and the transaction layer.
