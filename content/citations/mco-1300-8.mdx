## Scope

MCO 1300.8 establishes Marine Corps personnel assignment policy. The order covers the full assignment lifecycle including PCS authorization, inbound and outbound check-in/check-out, accountability, monitor coordination, reserve assignments, and related personnel actions. Admin Change 2 is the current change.

## Audience

Every Marine Corps S-1 officer, S-1 chief, adjutant, IPAC outbound and inbound counselor, and HQMC Monitor. Commanders signing inbound and outbound packages. Marines executing PCS, retirement, or separation actions.

## Content scope

- Personnel assignment policy framework.
- PCS authorization and orders procedures.
- Inbound and outbound check-in / check-out procedures.
- Accountability rules and the unit roster.
- Monitor coordination for sensitive assignments.
- Reserve and Active component assignment rules.
- Special-case assignments (LIMDU, OCONUS, accompanied tour).
