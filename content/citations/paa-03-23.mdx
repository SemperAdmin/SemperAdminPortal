## Scope

This advisory addresses Amplifying Guidance For MARADMIN 326/23. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. Request addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate org/units as applicable.
2. The following topics are addressed: Submission of appendix J for enlisted personnel between 15-18 months of requested retirement date.
3. MARADMIN 326/23 notified the force that enlisted Marines are eligible to request for retirement 6-18 months from their requested retirement date. This is a change from the previous guidance in MCO 1900.16 W/CH 2 (MARCORSEPMAN) which allowed for retirement submission 4-14 months from the requested retirement date.
4. At this time, system limitations prevent the submission of retirement requests outside of 14 months via the normal administrative submission using the IPAC/EPAR. An entry by a reporting unit outside of 4-14 months will fail. This means an enlisted Marine who is requesting retirement between 15-18 months must do so directly through HQMC, MMSR at [SMB.MANPOWER.MMSR2E@USMC.MIL](mailto:SMB.MANPOWER.MMSR2E@USMC.MIL).
5. Submit retirement requests outside of normal parameters (under 6 months and over 18 months) to HQMC, MMSR-2E by AA Form endorsed by the O-5 Commander or above.
