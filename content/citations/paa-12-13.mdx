## Status

CANCELLED. This advisory notice has been cancelled. The entry remains in the citations registry for traceability of older content citing it. Do not act on the operative paragraphs below as current policy. Verify current BAH certification requirements through MCO 7220.51, the active MARADMIN on BAH administration, and the current MCTFSPRIUM.

Numbering caution. The portal entry carries the number 12-13 for the cancellation notice and lists PAA 12-13 itself as reference (a), the cancelled advisory. Two documents share the number. Read this registry entry as the cancellation notice. The original BAH certification advisory is the cancelled reference (a).

## Scope

The notice cancels the earlier PAA 12-13 requirement to annotate the Basic Individual Record (BIR) with the BAH certification statement. MCTFS again carries the BAH certification statement on the BIR, viewable when printed. All requirements of references (b) and (c) remain in effect. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Action language

- Paragraph 1. REQUEST ALL ADDRESSEES DISSEMINATE THIS MESSAGE TO SUBORDINATE COMMANDS.
- Paragraph 2. REF A IS HEREBY CANCELLED. THE REQUIREMENT TO ANNOTATE THE BASIC INDIVIDUAL RECORD (BIR) WITH THE BAH CERTIFICATION STATEMENT IS RESCINDED. THE BAH CERTIFICATION STATEMENT HAS BEEN RESTORED TO THE MARINE CORPS TOTAL FORCE SYSTEM (MCTFS) BIR AND IS VIEWABLE WHEN PRINTED. ALL REQUIREMENTS OF REF B AND C REMAIN IN EFFECT.
