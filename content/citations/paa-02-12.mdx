## Scope

PAA 02-12 provides updated reporting requirements on personnel deployed in support of Operation Enduring Freedom (OEF). Released 2012-01-20, the advisory directs G-1, S-1, ARC, and IPAC personnel to ensure appropriate entries report properly and timely. Units supporting OEF use reach-back administration to the fullest extent possible. PAAs 01-00 through 01-12 remain in effect per the advisory.

## Audience

- G-1 and S-1 staff reporting deployed personnel.
- Administration Reachback Center (ARC) personnel.
- Installation Personnel Administration Center (IPAC) personnel.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.

## Action language

- Report deploying personnel in support of OEF under the advisory's personnel reporting instructions.
- Report personnel returning from OEF under the return reporting instructions.
- Report other personnel categories under the residual reporting instructions.
- Enter the unit diary entries required for deployed personnel tracking.
- Route administration for units supporting OEF through reach-back channels to the fullest extent possible.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- The advisory updates the reporting series started by PAA 01-11, the 2011-01-14 advisory covering OND and OEF deployment reporting.
- The portal's I&I demobilization processing reference, sourced to MCRAMM Chapter 10 (MCO 1001R.1L), lists this advisory alongside PAA 01-11 and PAA 01-13 as deployment reporting authorities.
- PAA 02-21 carries the later Unit Deployment Program reporting update built on the same reach-back administration model.
