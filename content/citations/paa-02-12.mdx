## Scope

PAA 02-12 provides updated reporting requirements on personnel deployed in support of Operation Enduring Freedom (OEF). The advisory directs G-1, S-1, ARC, and IPAC personnel to ensure appropriate entries report properly and timely. Units supporting OEF use reach-back administration to the fullest extent possible. PAAs 01-00 through 01-12 remain in effect.

## Audience

- G-1 and S-1 staff reporting deployed personnel.
- Administration Reachback Center (ARC) personnel.
- Installation Personnel Administration Center (IPAC) personnel.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.

## Content scope

- Personnel reporting instructions for deploying personnel in support of OEF.
- Personnel reporting instructions for personnel returning from OEF.
- Personnel reporting instructions for other personnel.
- Unit diary entries required for deployed personnel tracking.

Released 201519Z Jan 12 by CMC Washington DC MRA MI MIF. Historical record covering the OEF deployment cycle. Superseded by later UDP and deployment reporting PAAs.
