## Scope

PAA 01-24 updates the reference governing the action to place a member on Involuntary Appellate Leave. Per Article 76a UCMJ (10 U.S.C. § 876a) and paragraph 170606 of MCO 5800.16 Volume 16, involuntary appellate leave begins on or after the date of the entry of judgment. Per Appendix 15 R.C.M. 1111 of MCM 2024, the entry of judgement replaces the convening authority's action as the means by which the trial proceedings terminate and the appellate process begins.

## Audience

- Legal admin clerks and 0170 legal specialists.
- S-1 desks processing involuntary appellate leave entries.
- Commanders ordering appellate leave under the updated framework.
- Convening authorities.

## Content scope
