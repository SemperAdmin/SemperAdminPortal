## Scope

MCO 1040.31 sets the framework for maintaining the career force of the Marine Corps. The order is the primary means for managing retention of highly qualified enlisted Marines to meet career force MOS requirements, and the foundation of the Career Planning Program. Attainment of the annual First Term and Subsequent Term Alignment Plans is a critical leadership responsibility.

## Audience

Commanders set retention targets and own the integrity of the force. Senior Enlisted Advisors mentor career planning at the unit. Career Planners execute the program daily. The order applies to every command with enlisted Marines.
