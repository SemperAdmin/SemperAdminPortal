## Scope

This advisory addresses Policy Regarding Alignment Of Pay-Related Functions. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. Request addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate org/units as applicable.
2. The purpose of this message is to notify the Administrative community of the cancellation of reference (a).
3. Background. In March 2021, reference (a) authorized the Administrative community to report certain TTCs usually reserved for the Disbursing/Finance community. After considerable review, it has been determined to reverse this authority and restrict these TTCs to the Disbursing/Finance community only.
4. Effective 1 June 2024, the below TTCs will no longer be reported by the Administrative community. TTC/SEQ 597/003 Advanced Basic Allowance for Housing TTC/SEQ 699/000 Special Payment TTC/SEQ 559/000 Liquidation of Indebtedness (LIQ)
5. Change of Pay Option Election (POE) (TTC/SEQ 698/XXX) will remain reportable by the Administrative community.
