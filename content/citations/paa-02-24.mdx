## Scope

PAA 02-24 cancels PAAN 4-21 and realigns pay-related transaction reporting between the administrative and disbursing communities. Effective 2024-06-01, the administrative community stops reporting three transaction type codes: TTC/SEQ 597/003 (Advance Basic Allowance for Housing), TTC/SEQ 699/000 (Special Payment), and TTC/SEQ 559/000 (Liquidation of Indebtedness). Change of Pay Option Election, TTC/SEQ 698/XXX, stays reportable by the administrative community. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- Disbursing and finance offices resuming sole ownership of the restricted TTCs.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Action language

- Paragraph 1 requests addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate org/units as applicable.
- Paragraph 2 states the purpose. Notify the Administrative community of the cancellation of reference (a).
- Paragraph 3 gives the background. In March 2021, reference (a) authorized the Administrative community to report certain TTCs usually reserved for the Disbursing/Finance community. After considerable review, it has been determined to reverse this authority and restrict these TTCs to the Disbursing/Finance community only.
- Paragraph 4 sets the effective date. Effective 1 June 2024, the below TTCs will no longer be reported by the Administrative community. TTC/SEQ 597/003 Advanced Basic Allowance for Housing. TTC/SEQ 699/000 Special Payment. TTC/SEQ 559/000 Liquidation of Indebtedness (LIQ).
- Paragraph 5 keeps one code with admin. Change of Pay Option Election (POE) (TTC/SEQ 698/XXX) will remain reportable by the Administrative community.

## References the advisory cites

- PAAN 4-21, reference (a), the March 2021 notice this advisory cancels. The portal registry entry for PAAN 04-21 carries a CANCELLED marker.
- Personnel Reporting Instruction User Manual (PRIUM), the MCTFS reporting authority.
- DODFMR Volume 5 (Disbursing Policy), the governing volume for the disbursing community.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

The portal's military pay processing overview anchors function alignment between unit S-1, IPAC, and disbursing on this advisory. It defines which echelon owns which pay action. Disbursing officers operate per DODFMR Volume 5 and MCO 7300.21B at the Marine Corps level, and the three restricted TTCs return to their control under this advisory.
