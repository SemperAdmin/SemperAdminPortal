## Scope

DD Form 1299, Application for Shipment and/or Storage of Personal Property, March 2022 edition, electronic fillable. The DoD-wide application a service member files with the Personal Property Shipping Office to move or store household goods, unaccompanied baggage, and professional books, papers, and equipment at government expense. Two sides: the application at items 1 through 18, and the shipment and storage agreements with the Privacy Act statement on the back. The form is CUI when filled in, category PRVCY.

## Audience

Marines executing a PCS move file the form to start a household goods shipment or storage request. The unit S-1 supports the documentation alongside the DD Form 1797 counseling record. The Personal Property Shipping Office processes the application and prepares the shipping documents.

## Purpose printed on the form

Evaluates shipment and storage requests from service members and eligible individuals and feeds preparation of the Government bill of lading and other shipping documents. Privacy Act authorities are 37 U.S.C. 406 and 5 U.S.C. 5726. Entitlements for household goods, unaccompanied baggage, and mobile home transportation come from JTR Chapter 5, referenced in the agreements on the back.

## Key items

- Routing. Preparing office, responsible origin Personal Property Shipping Office, and destination shipping office at items 3 through 5.
- Customer information. Name, DOD ID, rank or grade, agency, phone numbers, and work and personal email addresses at item 6.
- The request. Quantity estimates at item 7 for household goods, unaccompanied baggage, and PBP&E, broken out as authorized weight, estimated weight, estimated member PBP&E, and estimated military spouse PBP&E.
- Orders. Item 8 records the order type, permanent or temporary, the issuing authority, new duty assignment, date, and orders number.
- Logistics. Pickup and destination addresses with designated release and receiving agents at items 9 and 10, extra pickup and delivery stops at 11 and 12, in-transit contact and permanent mailing address at 13, desired pickup and arrival dates at 14, and the direct delivery election at 15.
- Certification. The customer certifies at item 17 having read the shipping responsibilities and storage conditions on the back. Item 18 provides the certificate in lieu of signature when regulations authorize it.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- JTR Chapter 5 sets the household goods, unaccompanied baggage, and mobile home transportation entitlements the form executes. Portal guidance anchors the workflow to JTR Chapter 5 paragraph 0570.
- DTR 4500.9-R Volume 4 governs personal property movement procedures.
- MCO 4050.6, the Marine Corps Personal Property Movement Manual, applies the framework at the service level.
- DD Form 1797, Personal Property Counseling Checklist, pairs with this form as the documentation framework for PPM moves.
- The Defense Personal Property System at Move.mil fronts most household goods transactions and replaces the paper form in many scenarios. The form remains the underlying application of record.
- The portal covers the workflow on the Household Goods and PPM page, the PCS Orders and Overview page, and the Joining a Unit check-in list.
