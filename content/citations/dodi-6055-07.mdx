## Scope

DODI 6055.07 sets DoD policy on mishap notification, investigation, reporting, and record keeping. The instruction establishes mishap class definitions, notification timelines, safety investigation procedures, and lessons-learned dissemination. The Marine Corps coordinates safety investigations alongside JAGMAN administrative investigations under JAGINST 5800.7G Chapter II.

## Coverage

- Mishap class definitions (Class A through Class E) per paragraph 3 and Enclosure 8.
- Notification timelines to higher headquarters and DoD per paragraph 6 and Enclosure 8.
- Safety investigation board structure and procedures per Enclosure 4.
- Privileged safety information protections per Enclosure 5.
- Cross-coordination with administrative and criminal investigations.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- JAGINST 5800.7G Manual of the Judge Advocate General Chapter II governs administrative investigations running alongside safety investigations.
- JAGINST 5800.7G Chapter II Appendix A-2-o (Aviation Mishaps) cross-references the DODI 6055.07 framework.
- MCO 5100.29C Marine Corps Safety Program cross-references DODI 6055.07.
