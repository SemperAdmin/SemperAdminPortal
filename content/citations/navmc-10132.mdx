## What this form is

10132, 10132 (EF). Fillable PDF on file from the 2026-05-23 forms archive snapshot. The NAVMC forms index titles it UNIT PUNISHMENT BOOK (5812), revision 2023-08-01, three pages, stock number NONSN00001279, digital fillable. Per MCO 5800.16 Volume 14, the Unit Punishment Book records nonjudicial punishment for enlisted Marines on this form. The form carries 25 numbered items. It is not used for officer NJP, which follows Volume 15 of the manual.

## Purpose printed on the form

> Information will be used by designated command personnel to record and process non-judicial punishment actions. Information will be added to the individual’s OMPF. Specific punishments may be recorded and effectuated in the individual’s MCTFS record.

Quoted from the form's Privacy Act statement.

## Audience

The NJP authority (CO, OIC, or officer signing By direction) signs findings, punishment, and final administrative action. Leaders prepare the pre-NJP package and track item completion. S-1 and IPAC run the diary entries, report the punishment, and route the OMPF copy. Legal administration inspectors check UPB compliance under the IGMC 5800.16 checklist.

## Operative content per repo evidence

- PAA 04-23 directs immediate use of the REV. 08-2023 (EF) form for NJPs and reporting of courts-martial and NJP statistical data TTCs 212-000 and 212-001, driven by FY-22 NDAA Section 549G demographic reporting.
- Signatures lock prior items in sequence. Item 16, final administrative action, locks the entire form. Portal leader pages direct holding item 16 unsigned on the unit copy while any suspended punishment stays vacatable, since vacations record in item 21 remarks before the lock.
- IGMC checks the current revision is in use with a separate binder or digital folder per calendar year, a copy provided to IPAC or the admin section for OMPF inclusion, and UPB entries consistent with unit diary entries.
- MCAAT checks NJPs report within 5 days of key supporting documents or the effective date, file to OMPF within 10 working days from the diary report date, and vacations annotate the original UPB.

## Disposition printed on the form

Distribution: E-SRB. Copies to: OMPF, Files, Member.
