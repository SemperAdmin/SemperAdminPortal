## Scope

MCO 3000.18B is the Marine Corps Force Deployment Planning and Execution (FDP&E) Manual. The order assigns responsibilities and prescribes procedures for force deployment planning, deployment execution, and redeployment across the Marine Corps. The order implements CJCSM 3122.01A (Joint Operation Planning and Execution System) and supports Joint Force Marine Corps deployments worldwide.

## Audience

MAGTF commanders. Marine Corps Forces commands (MARFORCOM, MARFORPAC, MARFORRES). MEU and MEB staffs. S-3 operations and S-4 logistics officers. S-1 officers supporting personnel deployment inputs. HQMC PP&O (PLN) deployment planning staff.

## Content scope

- Force deployment planning framework (FDP&E).
- Joint Operation Planning and Execution System (JOPES) interface.
- Deployment execution procedures.
- Time-Phased Force and Deployment Data (TPFDD) development.
- MAGTF deployment sequencing.
- Sourcing and force allocation.
- Redeployment planning.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- CJCSM 3122.01A. JOPES at the Joint level.
- MCO 3120.12A. Global Force Management and Force Synchronization.
- MCO 3000.13B. Marine Corps Readiness Reporting.
- MCO P3500.72A. Marine Corps Ground T&R Manual.
- MCTP 3-30G. Marine Corps Manpower and Personnel Administration.

## Status

Active. HQMC PP&O (PLN) sponsors the FDP&E Manual. The order operationalizes deployment planning at the Marine Corps level under the Joint OPLAN framework.
