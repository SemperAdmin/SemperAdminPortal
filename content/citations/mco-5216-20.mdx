## Scope

MCO 5216.20B is the Marine Corps supplement to SECNAV M-5216.5. The order operationalizes DON correspondence format rules at the Marine Corps level. The order covers Marine Corps-specific format adjustments, signature authority delegations, by direction signing rules, MCO directives correspondence rules, and Marine Corps message handling procedures. Every Marine Corps unit drafts and signs correspondence applying both SECNAV M-5216.5 and the MCO 5216.20B supplement.

## Audience

S-1 officers, S-1 chiefs, legal clerks, adjutants, by direction signers, and Marine Corps action officers. Directives Control Points and records managers use the order for Marine Corps-specific filing and supplement rules.

## Content scope

- Marine Corps-specific naval letter format adjustments.
- Marine Corps signature authority delegations.
- By direction signing rules with DD Form 577.
- MCO directives correspondence handling.
- Marine Corps message format supplements.
- Marine Corps-specific stationery and letterhead.
- Marine Corps SSIC supplement (cross-reference to SECNAV M-5210.2).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- SECNAV M-5216.5 Department of the Navy Correspondence Manual. The parent DON document.
- MCO 5215.1K Marine Corps Directives Management Program. Directives format cross-reference.
- SECNAV M-5210.2 Standard Subject Identification Codes. SSIC framework.
- MCO 5000.14D Marine Corps Administrative Procedures. S-1 correspondence duties.
