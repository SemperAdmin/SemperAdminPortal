## Scope

NAVMC 1720.1B carries the Marine Corps Suicide Prevention System procedures. The original prevention guidance lived in the MCO 1720 series and migrated to the NAVMC publication chain. The "MCO 1720.1" alias resolves to this entry so commander pages referencing the legacy MCO continue to render a live chip.

## What this directive is

NAVMC 1720.1B, MARINE CORPS SUICIDE PREVENTION SYSTEM PROCEDURES. Signed 2025-05-28. Source PDF on file from the 2026-04-25 archive snapshot.

## Stated purpose

> Suicide Prevention in the Marine Corps is executed in accordance with references (a) through (s) and this NAVMC. This NAVMC establishes procedures to support suicide prevention, intervention, and postvention efforts and to ensure consistency throughout the Marine Corps. This NAVMC details the activities involved in the DISTRIBUTION STATEMENT A: is unlimited.
