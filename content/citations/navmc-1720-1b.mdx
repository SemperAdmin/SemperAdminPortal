## Scope

NAVMC 1720.1B carries the Marine Corps Suicide Prevention System procedures. The original prevention guidance lived in the MCO 1720 series and migrated to the NAVMC publication chain. The "MCO 1720.1" alias resolves to this entry so commander pages referencing the legacy MCO continue to render a live chip.
