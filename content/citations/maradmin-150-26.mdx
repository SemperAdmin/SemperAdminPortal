## Scope

MARADMIN 150/26 announces the change to the Family Separation Allowance (FSA) rate from $250 to $300 per month, effective 2025-12-18. Public Law 119-60, the NDAA for FY 2026, amended Title 37 U.S.C. section 427 to raise the rate. The prior $250 rate had stood since 2002-10-01. The message aligns Marine Corps payment with current DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 27 rates. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines receiving FSA. Draw the $300 monthly rate for eligibility periods on or after 2025-12-18. No individual action required.
- Disbursing and finance officers. Ensure proper entitlement and payment of the new rate for all periods of eligibility on or after 2025-12-18.
- S-1 staff. Process DD Form 1561 substantiation and FSA starts and stops through the reporting unit.

## Rate change mechanics

- New rate. $300 per month, flat, regardless of pay grade or number of dependents.
- Effective date. 2025-12-18, the enactment date of Public Law 119-60.
- Paragraph 3.c. The Marine Corps Total Force System auto-updated all records for Marines entitled to FSA on or after 2025-12-18.
- The rate covers all three FSA types, FSA-R, FSA-S, and FSA-T, under the 30-continuous-day separation threshold in DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 27.
