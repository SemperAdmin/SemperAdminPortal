## Scope

MCRP 7-20A.5 is the Marine Corps Reference Publication on Training Data Management. It provides guidance on MCTIMS data entry standards, training record management, and training readiness reporting. Chapter 2 defines UTM roles and functions within the unit training management structure. Part of the MCRP 7-20A companion series to MCTP 7-20A Unit Training Management. The MCPEL row lists the publication as Current with the summary TRAINING DATA MANAGEMENT and a publication date of 2023-01-27.

## Audience

Commanders responsible for the unit training program per MCO 1553.3C paragraph 4.a.(2)(b).1. S-1 officers drafting UTM role designations. S-1 chiefs tracking designations against the UTM roster. Marines assigned training management roles throughout the unit.

## Note on Portal Citation Label

Portal content pages reference this publication as "MCRP 7-20A.5 Unit Training Management Roles and Functions." The authoritative MCPEL title is "Training Data Management." Both labels resolve to this entry. UTM roles and functions appear in Chapter 2 of this publication.

## Operative content as used in the portal

- Chapter 2 carries the authoritative UTM role list. Portal admin pages direct the S-1 to pull the role list and the per-role function list from chapter 2, then map roles to billets per MCO 1553.3C paragraph 4.a.(2)(b).1.
- Role consolidation. Where the unit lacks personnel for full role coverage, the commander consolidates roles into fewer designations per chapter 2.
- Designation in writing. UTM roles and evaluators are designated by signed letter, routed to the Marine's OMPF Field Folder, with the MCTIMS UTM roster entry and a Page 11 documenting training and designation.
- A new release of this publication changing role definitions is a listed trigger event for redoing unit role designations.
