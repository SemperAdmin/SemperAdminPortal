## Scope

DODI 1000.13 establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and provides procedures for issuing DoD identification cards. The instruction governs ID-card eligibility, issuance, and use across the Department, from the Common Access Card for uniformed members to dependent and retiree cards. The instruction reissues the prior DODI 1000.13 and incorporates Change 1 effective 2017-12-14.

## Audience

- RAPIDS operators at every Marine Corps and DoD ID card facility.
- S-1 and IPAC personnel coordinating dependent DEERS enrollment.
- Marines, dependents, retirees, and other eligible communities receiving cards.
- Commanders accountable for ID-card program findings under inspection.

## Operative content

- Identity verification requires two forms of ID, one a government-issued photo ID, per Enclosure 3. Issuance without it is a violation.
- CAC issuance captures biometrics, photograph, and signature. A recovered CAC destroys per the recovery procedure, a lost CAC requires a lost-CAC memorandum and a security manager review.
- The DoD ID card serves as the Geneva Convention card per DODI 1000.01, and the CAC serves as the Federal PIV credential under HSPD-12. Communities not requiring PIV receive a distinct card form.
- Dependent and survivor cards key to DEERS status through the DD Form 1172-2, and a deceased dependent's card returns to RAPIDS or destroys per the instruction.
- Reserve members receive cards matching their component status, paired with MCRAMM Chapter 5.

## Applicability

OSD, the Military Departments including the Coast Guard at all times, the Joint Chiefs of Staff and Joint Staff, the Combatant Commands, and the rest of the DoD enterprise.

## Connection to higher policy

- HSPD-12 and DODI 1000.01. The PIV mandate and the identity card framework above the instruction.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- MCO 5512.11D. The Marine Corps ID-card implementation RAPIDS sites run on.
- MCO 1751.3. Dependency determinations feeding card eligibility.
- MCO 1001R.1L MCRAMM Chapters 5 and 12. Reserve and retiree card status rules.
- MARADMIN 015/20. Record-side documentation of the same status changes.
