## Scope

MCO 7220.13 publishes policy governing the Marine Corps Administrative Analysis Team (MCAAT) mission. MCAAT sits under Deputy Commandant for Manpower and Reserve Affairs, and the program publishes admin and finance checklists and visit schedules through the M&RA manpower site. The portal's inspections surface carries a dedicated MCAAT checklist track beside the IGMC track, 32 inspection programs across the two.

## Audience

- S-1 and IPAC sections preparing for an MCAAT admin analysis visit.
- Finance and disbursing sections working the MCAAT Finance Checklist (MFAC).
- Commanders reading MCAAT results as part of their audit and inspection posture.
- Admin chiefs pulling MCAAT admin training classes from the FMF MCAAT SharePoint.

## Content scope

Repo evidence names the order's function without chapter detail. It publishes MCAAT policy, cancels the prior revision MCO 7220.13G, and flags significant changes requiring a full read of the order.

## Operative content

- The MCAAT Admin Checklist and Admin Schedule publish on the M&RA manpower lookup site, with a parallel Finance Checklist (MFAC) and Finance Schedule.
- The portal renders MCAAT admin and finance checklist programs under /inspections, spanning audit, milpay, promotions, travel, separations, and other functional sections.
- Contact points on record. SMB_ASK_MCAAT@USMC.MIL and the CAC-gated FMF MCAAT SharePoint.

## Connection to higher policy

Repo evidence names no statute or DoD issuance above this order.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- IGMC Functional Area Checklists. The companion inspection track on the portal.
- The portal's MCAAT resources registry, refreshed 2026-05-20, holds the checklist, schedule, training, and SharePoint links.
- The portal video MISSO vs AAU vs MCAAT vs IGMC separates the four assistance and inspection bodies for admin Marines.
