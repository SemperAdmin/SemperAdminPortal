## Scope

MCO 5110.4B establishes the Marine Corps Postal Affairs and Official Mail Program. The order assigns responsibilities and prescribes procedures for Marine Corps postal operations, official mail management, mail accountability, and postal inspection. The order operationalizes DoDI 4525.09 at the Marine Corps level.

## Audience

Marine Corps Postal Officers (MOS 0161). Postal NCOs and Marines assigned to MPO, FPO, or unit mail rooms. Unit commanders responsible for postal operations and official mail management. Marine Corps Installations and Logistics (LP) program managers.

## Content scope

- Marine Corps postal operations organization.
- Military Post Office (MPO) and Fleet Post Office (FPO) operations.
- Unit-level mail handling and accountability.
- Registered mail and certified mail handling.
- Official mail management.
- Postal inspection requirements.
- Mail routing and transportation coordination.
- Postal directive and form management.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoDI 4525.09 Military Postal Service. Parent DoD policy.
- USPS Domestic Mail Manual (DMM) and International Mail Manual (IMM).
- 39 U.S.C. Postal Service statutory authority.
- SECNAV M-5210.2 SSIC for postal subjects.

## Status

Active. The B revision cancels MCO 5110.4A. HQMC Installations and Logistics (LP) sponsors.
