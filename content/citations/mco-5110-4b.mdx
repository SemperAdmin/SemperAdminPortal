## Scope

MCO 5110.4B establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes guidance governing Marine Corps Military Post Offices (MPO), Unit Mailrooms (UMR), and Official Mail Centers (OMC). The Order covers postal affairs and the Marine Corps Official Mail Program. The original portal entry was filed as "MCO 5110.2" but the canonical postal-affairs MCO carries the 5110.4 series number. The "MCO 5110.2" alias preserves back-compat for any inline reference written before the renumber.

## Supersession chain

MCO 5110.4B supersedes MCO 5110.4A and MCO 5110.6C. The portal-side "MCO 5110.2" stub is also retired into this entry.
