## Scope

MCWP 4-11.1 covers Health Service Support (HSS) operations across Marine Corps forces. The publication frames HSS as the operational discipline supporting commanders with medical capability, casualty care, and force health protection. The portal originally filed this topic as a generic "MCO 6000" stub. No HQMC order carries the number. The Marine Corps doctrine for Health Service Support lives in the MCWP series. The "MCO 6000" alias preserves back-compat for any inline reference written before the renumber.

## Audience

Commanders and their staffs assessing HSS capabilities and requirements as part of mission planning. Medical personnel assigned to Marine Corps forces responsible for casualty response and force health protection. S-1 sections tracking medical readiness data feeding the commander's oversight picture.

## Operative content as used in the portal

The portal commander oversight matrix carries a Health Services row under the "MCO 6000" label resolving to this entry. The summarized commander actions in the row.

- Oversee 90 percent medical readiness.
- Establish Controlled Substances Inventory Board (CSIB) policy if the unit maintains controlled substances.

The matrix page marks its rows as summaries from the IGMC Functional Area checklists, to be confirmed against source text before any signature or policy action. Health Service Support (HSS) is an IGMC CoRE functional area, FA number 6000, sponsored by HQMC (HS), with a published checklist effective 2025-05-15 per the portal FA registry.

## Connections

- MCO 6320.4, Health Care Quality Assurance Policies for Operating Forces, issued jointly with OPNAVINST 6320.7A on 2007-08-15. The portal citation entry for the order names this publication as companion doctrine for Marine Corps HSS.
- MCRP 4-11.1 series. The MCPEL index on file lists multiservice tactics, techniques, and procedures companions, including MCRP 4-11.1F for health service support in corps and division operations areas.
- IGMC FA 6000 checklist. The inspection baseline for the Health Services functional area.

## Status

Signed 2012-12-10 per this entry's effective date. Repo evidence for this publication is limited to the oversight matrix row, the FA registry entry, and the MCO 6320.4 companion note. Caution.
