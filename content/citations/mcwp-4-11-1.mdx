## Scope

MCWP 4-11.1 covers Health Service Support (HSS) operations across Marine Corps forces. The publication frames HSS as the operational discipline supporting commanders with medical capability, casualty care, and force health protection. The portal originally filed this topic as a generic "MCO 6000" stub. No HQMC order with that number exists. The Marine Corps doctrine for Health Service Support lives in the MCWP series. The "MCO 6000" alias preserves back-compat for any inline reference written before the renumber.

## Audience

Commanders and their staffs assessing HSS capabilities and requirements as part of mission planning. Medical personnel assigned to Marine Corps forces responsible for casualty response and force health protection.

## Related Marine Corps Order

For health-care quality policies governing the operating forces, see MCO 6320.4 (Health Care Quality Assurance Policies for Operating Forces), issued jointly with OPNAVINST 6320.7A on 15 August 2007.
