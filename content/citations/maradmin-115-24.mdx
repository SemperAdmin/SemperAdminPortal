## Scope

MARADMIN 115/24 clarifies Permanent Change of Station (PCS) allowances for Marines attending Professional Military Education (PME) or a training class. The message aligns travel and household goods entitlements with the Joint Travel Regulations for school and training moves. Signed 2024-03-06. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines on PCS orders to PME or a training class. Apply the PCS allowance framework.
- Disbursing officers and Defense Travel Administrators. Process PCS vouchers under this framework.
- Reporting units. Report housing allowances tied to school moves in MCTFS.

## Allowance reporting

- Travel and household goods entitlements for the school move follow the Joint Travel Regulations.
- MCO 7220.56A Chapter 3 carries the BAH treatment for PCS to PME at locations other than the Permanent Duty Station.
- The MCAAT military pay checklist cites this message with MCTFSPRIUM 9-90301 on the item asking whether Family Separation Housing Allowance (FSH) on DD Form 2367 is reported in accordance with applicable regulations within 5 days after the effective date.

## Connection to higher policy

- Joint Travel Regulations. Parent authority for PCS travel and household goods entitlements.
- DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 26. FSH reference paired with this message on the MCAAT checklist.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO 7220.56A. BAH at Locations Other Than the PDS. Chapter 3 covers PCS for PME.
- MCTFSPRIUM 9-90301. FSH reporting mechanics cited alongside this message.

## Status

Signed 2024-03-06 per the marines.mil message index. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA MP. Active. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force. Verified against the message text on 2026-07-15. The FSH pairing is correct. Paragraph 4.c directs the IPAC or administrative section certifying personnel officer to report TTC 388/002 Family Separation Housing-B to start FSH-B for eligible Marines attending PME or training, with termination rules in paragraph 5. The MCAAT checklist citation stands.
