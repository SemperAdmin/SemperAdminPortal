## Scope

MARADMIN 092/21 updates MARADMIN 414/20, FY21 Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T) Program Details. The message refines eligibility and processing rules for the FY21 HDP-T program, which ran under the Department of the Navy three year reauthorization through 2023-09-30. Signed 2021-02-23. Applicability is active and reserve component Marines.

## Audience

- Marines on qualifying high tempo rotations. Confirm HDP-T rate and eligibility.
- Reporting units and IPACs. Report HDP under this message and the MCTFSPRIUM tables.
- Disbursing officers. Process HDP-T payments.

## HDP reporting on inspection checklists

- MCAAT admin checklists ask whether HDP is reported in accordance with applicable regulations and cite this message together with MARADMIN 619/14.
- Companion references on the same checklist items. DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 17 paragraph 1702 with Figure 17-1 and Table 17-1, and MCTFSPRIUM 9-90801, 9-90802, and 9-90803 with Table 9-8.
- Supporting units validate HDP reporting by the reporting unit against the same reference set.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 17. Hardship Duty Pay rate and entitlement framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 510/14. Authorization for Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T). Remains in effect for program rules.
- MARADMIN 619/14. HDP-T Program Details. Original eligibility, rate, and payment processing rules.
- MARADMIN 414/20. FY21 HDP-T Program Details. The message this MARADMIN updates. The marines.mil source page for 414/20 reads CANCELLED.
- MCTFSPRIUM 9-90801 through 9-90803 and Table 9-8. HDP reporting mechanics.

## Status

Signed 2021-02-23 per the marines.mil message index. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA. Applicability is active and reserve component Marines. Verification caution. The FY21 program details this message updates ride on MARADMIN 414/20, whose source page reads CANCELLED, and the DON reauthorization window closed 2023-09-30. Confirm current HDP-T program status before applying FY21 details.
