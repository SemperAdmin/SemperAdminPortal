## Scope

MARADMIN 518/21 directs the consolidation of the Defense Readiness Reporting System Marine Corps (DRRS-MC) into the Joint DRRS system. The MARADMIN supports the DoD-wide readiness reporting unification effort. The consolidation aligns Marine Corps readiness reporting with the Joint DRRS architecture per DoDD 7730.65 and CJCSI 3401.02B.

## Audience

Marine Corps unit commanders. S-3 operations officers. DRRS reporting administrators. HQMC PP&O readiness oversight staff. MARFOR readiness coordinators.

## Required action

- Transition DRRS-MC reporting to DRRS per the published timeline.
- Train designated reporting administrators on DRRS architecture.
- Update unit reporting procedures to align with DRRS.
- Coordinate with MARFOR for transition support.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 3000.13B. Marine Corps Readiness Reporting (DRRS-MC framework being consolidated).
- DoDD 7730.65. DoD Readiness Reporting System.
- CJCSI 3401.02B. Joint Force Readiness Reporting.

## Status

Active. The consolidation effort continues per the published timeline. Verify current DRRS-MC versus DRRS reporting status with HQMC PP&O.
