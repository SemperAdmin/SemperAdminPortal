## Scope

MCO 4650.39A sets policy for operation and use of the Defense Travel System across the Marine Corps. DTS is the single, paperless Travel Management System used by every Department of Defense traveler. The system provides automated transportation and lodging arrangements, travel-cost estimates, travel authorization, fund obligation, ticket issue, voucher processing for reimbursement and reconciliation, and DOD and Service reporting.

## Audience

Every Marine who travels on official orders. Approving Officials, Authorizing Officials, Certifying Officials, Reviewing Officials, and DTAs (Defense Travel Administrators) at unit, command, and HQMC level. Commanders manage routing lists and permissions to maintain separation of duties.

## Authority

Deputy Commandant, Manpower and Reserve Affairs (DC, M&RA) provides oversight for every DTS issue. The Director, Manpower Information Support Systems (MI) Division is the DTS sponsor. The Travel Management Section, M&RA (MIF), administers the program.

## Commander responsibilities

- Minimize the number of officials in the routing list. Keep routing timely. Keep enough officials to maintain document accuracy.
- Minimize functions assigned to one individual. Hold separation of duties.
- Regularly review permission levels for subordinate activities. Keep separation of duties intact.
