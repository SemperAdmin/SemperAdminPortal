## Scope

MCO 5320.12H establishes precedence levels for manning and staffing the Marine Corps Table of Organization. The order operationalizes the Authorized Strength Report (ASR) process, prioritizes the distribution of end-strength inventory against TFSMS T/O requirements, and assigns commands to precedence categories. The order is a manning prioritization framework. It is not the master Total Force Structure Process policy. The master policy sits at MCO 5311.1E.

## Audience

HQMC Deputy Commandant for Manpower and Reserve Affairs (M&RA) staff. Manpower Management Officer Assignments (MMOA) and Manpower Management Enlisted Assignments (MMEA) branch staff distributing inventory. G-1 manpower officers at MEF level coordinating precedence decisions. Unit S-1 chiefs reading the ASR to understand command precedence category against the manning gap report.

## Enclosure map

- Enclosure 1. Excepted Commands. Commands manned at higher precedence levels.
- Enclosure 2. Operating Forces Commands. Operating forces precedence rules.
- Enclosure 3. Priority Commands. Priority command precedence rules.
- Enclosure 4. Proportionate Share Commands. Proportionate share manning rules.

## Status

Active. MCO 5320.12H signed with Administrative Change 2 effective 20 May 2019. Cancels MCO 5320.12G. HQMC DC M&RA sponsors the order. The Commandant's Planning Guidance further prioritizes inventory distribution during wartime contingencies.
