## Scope

MARADMIN 145/24 solicits qualified Marines for lateral move (LATMOV) into the Marine Corps Community Services (MCCS) Primary Military Occupational Specialty 4133. The 4133 PMOS supports Marine and Family Programs, MCCS resale and recreation operations, and family readiness initiatives. The solicitation runs under the broader FY24 retention campaign framework. Signed 2024-03-21. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Active component Marines seeking a lateral move into 4133. Eligible to apply.
- Career planners and unit Career Counselors. Process LATMOV applications and pull current availability.
- HQMC MMEA-1. Adjudicates LATMOV requests.

## LATMOV process

- Marines submit through Marine Online (MOL) under the Career Planner module.
- Applications include command endorsement.
- HQMC MMEA-1 reviews applications against open boatspaces.
- The portal retention page pairs this message with the 4133 screening path. Review the message, write a 3 to 4 sentence rationale, complete an interview with the MCCS Senior Marine.
- 4133 screening requirements listed on the same portal page. GT 110, or GT 105 with CL 105, or GT 105 with EL 105. First class PFT and CFT. Volunteer senior Corporal with 1 year time in grade, or junior Sergeant with no more than 2 years time in grade and not in zone for promotion. 48 months obligated service. Secret clearance eligibility. Worldwide deployability. Interview by a MGySgt, MSgt, or GySgt in the MOS.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 1040.31. Enlisted Retention and Career Development Program. Parent retention authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- NAVMC 1200.1K. Military Occupational Specialties Manual. 4133 PMOS prerequisites.
- MARADMIN 014/25. FY25 Active Component Enlisted Marine Lateral Move Opportunities. Companion LATMOV announcement.
