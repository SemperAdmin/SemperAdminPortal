## Scope

This advisory addresses Clarification For BAH W/O Depns During Transitional PTAD. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. PAAS 01‐13 THROUGH 09‐13 REMAIN IN EFFECT.
2. ADDRESSEES ARE REQUESTED TO READDRESS THIS PAA TO SUBORDINATE ORG/UNITS.
3. THE FOLLOWING TOPIC IS ADDRESSED: A. CLARIFICATION FOR BASIC ALLOWANCE FOR HOUSING (BAH) WITHOUT DEPENDENTS ENTITLEMENT FOR MARINES AUTHORIZED TRANSITIONAL PERMISSIVE TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY (PTAD) AND POST DEPLOYMENT MOBILIZATIONS RESPITE ABSENCE (PDMRA) IN CONJUNCTION WITH LEAVE WHILE AWAITING SEPARATION (LWAS).
4. PER CHAPTER 10, U10416 OF REFERENCE A, A MEMBER ON LEAVE WHILE AWAITING SEPARATION, DISCHARGE, OR RETIREMENT AND USING ACCRUED PDMRA OR TRANSITIONAL PTAD ALONE OR IN COMBINATION WITH ACCRUED ANNUAL LEAVE IS AUTHORIZED LOCALITY BASED BAH OR OHA WHEN NOT ASSIGNED TO GOVERNMENT OWNED QUARTERS. REFER TO CHAPTER 10 TABLE U10E‐12 OF REFERENCE B FOR SPECIFICS ON WHICH BAH LOCATION RATE IS APPLICABLE GIVEN THE MEMBERS CIRCUMSTANCE (E.G. MEMBER FROM U.S. PDS OR MEMBER FROM PDS OUTSIDE THE U.S.).
5. SITUATIONS: A. MARINE RESIDING IN SINGLE TYPE GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AT CAMP LEJEUNE AND DRAWING BAH‐PARTIAL IS APPROVED FOR TEMPORARY DISABILITY RETIRED LIST (TDRL) EFFECTIVE 20121231. SNMS COMMANDING OFFICER APPROVES SNM 20 DAYS OF PTAD AND 30 DAYS OF LWAS WITH AN EFFECTIVE DATE OF DEPARTURE OF 20121112. SNM VACATES GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AND DEPARTS ON PTAD IN CONJUNCTION WITH LWAS AT 0800 20121112; HE IS ENTITLED TO BAH WITHOUT DEPENDENTS AT ZIP CODE 28542 EFFECTIVE
6. B. MARINE RESIDING IN SINGLE TYPE GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AT CAMP PENDLETON AND DRAWING BAH‐DIFF IS APPROVED FOR A DISABILITY WITH SEVERANCE PAY DISCHARGE EFFECTIVE 20121130. SNM HAS A PDMRA BALANCE OF 8.0 DAYS IN THE MCTFS. SNMS COMMANDING OFFICER APPROVES SNM 20 DAYS OF PTAD, 8 DAYS PDMRA, AND 20 DAYS LWAS WITH AN EFFECTIVE DATE OF DEPARTURE OF 20121014. SNM VACATES GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AND DEPARTS ON PTAD/PDMRA IN CONJUNCTION WITH LWAS AT 0800 20121014. SNMS ENTITLEMENT TO BAH‐DIFF TERMINATES EFFECTIVE 20121013 AND SNM IS ENTITLED TO BAH W/DEPN (DIFF) AT ZIP CODE 92055 EFFECTIVE 20121014. C. MARINE RESIDING IN SINGLE TYPE GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AT NEW RIVER AND DRAWING BAH‐PARTIAL IS APPROVED FOR FULL SEPARATION PAY EFFECTIVE
7. SNMS COMMANDING OFFICER APPROVES SNM 20 DAYS OF PTAD WITH AN EFFECTIVE DATE OF DEPARTURE OF 20121011. SNM VACATES GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AND DEPARTS ON PTAD AT 0800 20121011; HE IS ENTITLED TO BAH WITHOUT DEPENDENTS AT ZIP CODE 28545 EFFECTIVE 20121011.
8. POC/DFAS‐JFLS/CL/TEL: DSN 580‐5653/TEL: COMM (216) 522‐5653.
