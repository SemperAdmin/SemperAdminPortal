## What this volume is

MCO 5800.16 Volume 17, JUDGE ADVOCATE DIVISION AWARDS PROGRAM. One of 17 volumes of the Legal Support and Administration Manual, short title LSAM, the Marine Corps legal services authority issued by CMC (JA). Signed 10 FEB 2021. Cancels MCO 1650.62 and renames its award. Source PDF on file from the 2026-07 archive addition.

## Lead excerpt

> To establish and provide guidance on the annual Staff Judge Advocate to the Commandant (SJA to CMC) approved awards that are available to recognize 44XX Marines. Awards serve to appropriately recognize exceptionally meritorious service, outstanding achievement, and other acts or services that are above and beyond normal expectations.

Quoted from section 0101, Purpose.
