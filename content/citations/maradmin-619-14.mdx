## Scope

MARADMIN 619/14 published the Hardship Duty Pay - Tempo (HDP-T) Program Details on 2014-12-01. The message sets the original eligibility, rate, and payment processing rules referenced by successive HDP-T fiscal year MARADMINs and by the MCTFS reporting manual. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines on qualifying high tempo operational rotations. Eligible for HDP-T.
- S-1 staff and unit diary clerks. Report HDP-T transactions in MCTFS.
- Disbursing officers. Process HDP-T payments.

## Program rules the portal asserts

- Marines operationally deployed for more than 220 consecutive days become eligible for HDP-T on the 221st consecutive deployed day, per MCTFSPRIUM paragraph 90803, which directs readers to this message for specific rules and exceptions.
- HDP-T is payable at a rate of $495.00 per month.
- MCTFS reports HDP-T under TTC 125, 136, and 175. Start, stop, credit, and checkage transactions require effective dates after 2014-09-17.
- MCTFS allows HDP-T to run concurrently with HDP-L and HDP-M.
- MARADMIN 414/20 kept the guidance in MARADMIN 510/14 and this message in effect while reauthorizing HDP-T payments through 2023-09-30.
