## Scope

MARADMIN 028/24 cancels MARADMIN 602/24, Guiding Principles for the Ethical Use of Artificial Intelligence by Communication Strategy and Operations. MARADMIN 635/24 replaces the cancelled message concurrently, released the same day, 2024-12-30.

## Audience

- COMMSTRAT community. Apply the current MARADMIN 635/24 framework.
- Communication Directorate staff. Route ethical AI questions under the MARADMIN 635/24 framework.

## Cancellation details

- MARADMIN 602/24, dated 2024-12-17, set guiding principles for the ethical use of AI by the COMMSTRAT community. The registry records its source page as CANCELLED.
- MARADMIN 635/24, released 2024-12-30 with DTG R 301807Z DEC 24, replaces MARADMIN 602/24 with broader wording on the scope of permitted AI use. Release authorized by SES April Langwell, Director of Communication.
- The principles carry forward in MARADMIN 635/24. National security safeguards, transparency about AI-enhanced content, human oversight in information release, and acknowledgment of inherent AI limitations.

## Connection to higher policy

- The portal records no parent directive for this cancellation. The replacement framework in MARADMIN 635/24 governs.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 602/24. Cancelled by this message.
- MARADMIN 635/24. Current ethical AI framework for COMMSTRAT.
