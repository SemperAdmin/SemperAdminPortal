## Scope

PAA 12-12 corrects the mailing address for Copy 5 of DD Forms 214 and 215 sent to the Department of Labor. A DoD review of separation procedures found inconsistent mailing of Copy 5 to the correct address. The advisory directs all separation processors to use the Federal Claims Control Center (FCCC) address and to update desk top procedures. Effective date 2012-09-25.

## Audience

- IPAC retirement and separation processors.
- S-1 separation desks preparing DD Forms 214 and 215.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Personnel involved in separation processing of Marines.

## Content scope

- Department of Labor mailing address correction for Copy 5 of DD Forms 214 and 215.
- Federal Claims Control Center (FCCC) as the correct routing destination.
- Address: Department of Labor, Federal Claims Control Center (FCCC), PO Box 785070, Orlando, FL 32878-5070.
- Desk top procedures update requirement for all separation processing personnel.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- The Separations and Retirement Overview page cites this PAA within the MCO 1900.16 MARCORSEPMAN framework. MCO 1900.16 resolves in this registry.
- The citing page routes separation and retirement matters through the MMSR sections at HQMC. MMSR-2 owns enlisted matters, MMSR-4 disability and limited duty, MMSR-5 reservist matters, MMSR-6 retired services.
- The citing page lists incorrect document handling among common separation failure points, this advisory addresses one of those routing errors at the copy-distribution step.
- The same page lists this PAA next to PAA 14-13, both govern document routing when a Marine separates. The DD Form 214 entry resolves in this registry.
