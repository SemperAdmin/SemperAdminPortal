## Scope

PAA 12-12 corrects the mailing address for Copy 5 of DD Forms 214 and 215 sent to the Department of Labor. DoD review of separation procedures identified that Copy 5 was not consistently mailed to the correct address. The advisory directs all separation processors to use the Federal Claims Control Center (FCCC) address.

## Audience

- IPAC retirement and separation processors.
- S-1 separation desks preparing DD Forms 214 and 215.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Personnel involved in separation processing of Marines.

## Content scope

- Department of Labor mailing address correction.
- Federal Claims Control Center (FCCC) as the correct routing destination.
- Address: Department of Labor, Federal Claims Control Center (FCCC), PO Box 785070, Orlando, FL 32878-5070.
- Desk top procedures update requirement for all separation processing personnel.

## Status

Released 251849Z Sep 12 by CMC Washington DC MRA MI MIF. POC was Mr. Don Page. Verify the current FCCC address with DoL or current MARCORSEPMAN guidance before mailing, as the address is procedural and updates over time.
