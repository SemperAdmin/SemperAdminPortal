## Scope

MCO 6100.13A governs the Marine Corps Physical Fitness and Combat Fitness Tests, PFT and CFT. The MCPEL lists the current version as MCO 6100.13A W/ADMIN CH-5 under the title MARINE CORPS PHYSICAL FITNESS AND COMBAT FITNESS TESTS (PFT/CFT). Portal admin pages also cite the order as the Marine Corps Physical Fitness Program authority. The Change 5 transmittal, signed 2025-05-28, carries the subject line MARINE CORPS DIRECTIVES COMPLIANCE WITH EXECUTIVE ORDER 14168 and states.

> The subject order needs to be revised to reflect direction by the Commandant of the Marine Corps (CMC) regarding the Physical Fitness Test (PFT).

## Audience

- Marines taking the PFT and CFT.
- Unit training and operations staff reporting fitness scores and training data.
- S-1 and IPAC personnel coding fitness and Body Composition Program status in MCTFS.
- Inspectors and self-assessment teams working the MCAAT admin training checklist.

## Content scope

Physical fitness and combat fitness testing, plus the reporting and coding duties the portal ties to the order. Limited duty admin pages cite the order for the Marine Corps Physical Fitness Program and BCP status coding. The MCAAT admin training checklist cites it for training data reporting accuracy and timeliness.

## Operative content

- Units report training data, PFT, CFT, rifle, schools, and MCMAP, accurately within 5 days of the effective date, per the MCAAT admin training checklist citing MCO 6100.13A with Change 4 and Change 5, MCO 1500.59A, and MCO 3574.2M.
- Units identify Marines with missing or late training data in MCTFS, per the same checklist citing MCO 6100.13A with Change 5 and MARADMIN 474/21.
- The IPAC codes BCP status in MCTFS per MCTFSPRIUM and MCO 6100.13A when a limited duty status carries BCP implications, per the limited duty operational restrictions page.
- Authorization for partial or no PFT sits at MCO 1900.16 Chapter 8 paragraph 8106, cross-referenced with this order on the limited duty pages.

## Connection to higher policy

- Executive Order 14168. Change 5 implements compliance direction and flags a coming revision reflecting CMC direction on the PFT.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- MCO 1500.59A and MCO 3574.2M, co-cited with this order for training data reporting.
- MARADMIN 474/21, co-cited for missing or late training data identification.
- MCTFSPRIUM unit reporting codes 2-20103, 5-51202, 5-51213, 5-51301, and 5-51401 accompany the reporting requirement in the checklist.
- MCO 1900.16 Chapter 8, limited duty and partial or no PFT authorization.
- MCO 1300.8 and MCO P1070.12K Chapter 1, cross-referenced on the same limited duty surfaces.
