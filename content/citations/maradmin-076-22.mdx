## Scope

MARADMIN 076/22 publishes updated requirements for implementation of the Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance (SGLI) Online Enrollment System (SOES). The message directs commanders to confirm every Marine completes an SGLI election in SOES and defines the occasions requiring a new election. The message aligns Marine Corps SGLI handling with current Department of the Navy and Veterans Affairs guidance. Signed 2022-02-25. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines on Active Duty and in the Reserve Component. Complete the SOES election and select Confirm and Certify before exiting.
- Commanders. Verify elections at each triggering occasion and maintain compliance procedures.
- S-1 staff and IPACs. Process SGLI elections through SOES and track compliance.
- Casualty Assistance Calls Officers (CACOs) and inspectors. Reference SGLI status during casualty support and Functional Area 3040 inspections.

## Election requirements

- Paragraph 2. Commanders shall ensure all Marines without a SOES election make one and select Confirm and Certify before exiting SOES, so the system stores the beneficiary designation.
- Paragraph 2. Election occasions. Joining the unit. Review or update of the Record of Emergency Data (RED). Reenlistment. Request of the Marine. Request for accelerated benefits. Prior to deployment. Activation, mobilization, or permanent change of station, and prior to appellate leave or separation.
- Paragraph 6. Commands establish procedures to attain and maintain maximum compliance at all times. Inspections verify compliance through the Casualty 3040 Functional Area Checklist.
- Paragraph 6. New join audits do not meet the criteria for updating SOES. A new join audit updates only the RED.
