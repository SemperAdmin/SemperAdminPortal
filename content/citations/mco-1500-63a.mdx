## What this order is

MCO 1500.63A, MARINE CORPS COMMON MILITARY TRAINING REQUIREMENTS. Signed 2026-04-10. Source PDF on file from the 2026-04-28 archive snapshot.

## Stated purpose

> This Order provides policy and procedural guidance for the implementation of common military training requirements for every Marine. The requirements in this Order are directed by law, Department of War (DoW), Department of the Navy (DON), or through Headquarters Marine Corps (HQMC) as listed in enclosure (2). This Order is in accordance with references (a) through (ah).
