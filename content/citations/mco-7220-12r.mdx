## Scope

MCO 7220.12R establishes the Marine Corps Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP) Program. The order assigns responsibilities, defines eligible billets, sets the SDAP rate structure, and prescribes procedures for designating, paying, and terminating SDAP at the Marine Corps level. The order implements DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 8, DODI 1304.27, and 37 U.S.C. 307 at the Marine Corps level.

## Audience

Every Marine Corps S-1, IPAC pay clerk, and disbursing technician processing SDAP transactions. Commanders designating eligible billets and terminating SDAP for cause. Marines holding designated SDAP billets (drill instructor, recruiter, MSG, security force, etc.).

## Content scope

- SDAP-eligible billets across the Marine Corps (drill instructor, recruiter, Marine Security Guard, security forces, instructor duty, etc.).
- SDAP rate levels (SD-1 through SD-6) tied to position assessment.
- Designation and certification procedures.
- MCTFS transaction codes for SDAP.
- Termination on duty change, disciplinary action, or qualification loss.
- Audit requirements for unit SDAP payments.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 8 (Special Duty Assignment Pay). Parent DoD framework.
- DODI 1304.27 Award and Administration of Special Duty Assignment Pay. Parent DoD policy.
- 37 U.S.C. 307. Statutory authority for special duty assignment pay.

## Status

Active. Marine Corps Programs and Resources Division (RFM) sponsors the program.
