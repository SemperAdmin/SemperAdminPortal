## Scope

DODI 1400.25 establishes the DoD Civilian Personnel Management System (CPMS). The instruction sets policy and provides procedures for managing DoD civilian personnel including position management, classification, recruitment, placement, performance management, training, separations, retirement, and labor relations. The instruction operates as a multi-volume framework with volume-specific subject coverage.

## Audience

DoD civilian personnel offices and civilian personnel advisory services (CPAS). DoD supervisors of civilian employees. DoD HR specialists. Marine Corps civilian personnel liaisons. Marine Corps commanders supervising civilian Marines.

## Content scope

- Position management and classification.
- Recruitment and placement.
- Performance management and conduct.
- Training and development.
- Separations and retirement.
- Labor and employee relations.
- Equal employment opportunity.
- Pay administration cross-reference to DoDFMR Vol 8.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- DoDFMR Vol 8. Civilian Pay Policy.
- MCO 12810.1. Federal Employees Compensation Act Program.
- 5 U.S.C. (Title 5 Civil Service rules).

## Status

Active. The instruction operates as a multi-volume framework. Subject-specific volumes carry their own effective dates.
