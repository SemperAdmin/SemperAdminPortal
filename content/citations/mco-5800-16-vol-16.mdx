> The purpose of military law, as stated in the Preamble to the Manual for Courts-Martial, “is to promote justice, to assist in maintaining good order and discipline in the armed forces, to promote efficiency and effectiveness in the military establishment, and thereby to strengthen the national security of the United States.” 010302.
