## Scope

MARADMIN 559/14 publishes guidance for archiving Service Treatment Records (STRs) for United States Marine Corps active and Selected Reserve service members. The message sets the STR retention, archive, and access framework supporting Department of Veterans Affairs claims and longitudinal medical records. Date signed is 2014-10-30. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marine Corps medical units. Apply STR archive procedures.
- Reporting units and IPACs. Submit STRs for separating Marines on the required timeline.
- Departing Marines. Verify STR completeness before separation.
- Department of Veterans Affairs. Receives archived STRs for veteran claims.

## STR submission rules the portal asserts

- Reporting units submit all STRs to the Marine Corps STR Central Cell via USPS with signature confirmation.
- Submission occurs no earlier than the commencement of PTAD, PDMRA, or separation or terminal leave.
- Submission occurs no later than 5 working days past the Marine's EAS.
- The MCAAT outbound management checklist inspects this timeline for both IPAC and reporting unit audiences, citing this message alongside MARADMIN 393/20 and MCO 1900.16 paragraph 1101.
