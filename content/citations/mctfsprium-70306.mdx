## Source text

### Section 1

To report an absence of in Hands of Foreign Authorities (IHFA) that has been excused, report as follows:

```
TTC 300 000 IHFA (A) TO (B) ABS EXCUSED |
```
(A) 8-byte FROM date (YYYYMMDD)

(B) 8-byte TO date (YYYYMMDD)
