## Stated purpose

> The mental, moral, and physical resiliency of the Marine Corps’ warfighters will be of utmost importance to the Marine Corps winning battles in future conflicts. As a supporting effort to the Marine Corps Physical Fitness Program (MCPFP) in reference (a), Marine Corps Martial Arts Program (MCMAP) aims to strengthen the mental and moral resiliency of individual Marines through realistic combative training, warrior ethos studies, and physical hardening.
