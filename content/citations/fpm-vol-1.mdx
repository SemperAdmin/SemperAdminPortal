Volume 1 of the [Marine Corps Finance Policy Manual](/citations/fpm),
titled Pay Policies. Snapshot on file dated 2026-05-21.

## Scope

Military pay entitlements and the policies governing them inside MCTFS. Basic pay, allowances, special and incentive pays, deductions, debts, and the pay-event reporting the unit diary drives. The volume the app cites most, 90 citation hits across admin pages and MCAAT checklists.

## Access notes

The Finance Policy Manual is published by the HQMC Finance Branch (RFF) with
distribution marked DoD community only. No public marines.mil URL exists.
Pull the working copy through the HQMC RFF portal or the servicing finance
office. This entry intentionally carries no externalUrl so citation chips
route here instead of hitting an authentication wall.
