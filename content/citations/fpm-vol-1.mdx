## Scope

Volume 1 of the Marine Corps Finance Policy Manual, titled Pay Policies. The volume carries military pay entitlements and governing policies inside MCTFS, basic pay, allowances, special and incentive pays, deductions, debts, and pay-event reporting through the unit diary. The most-cited FPM volume across admin pages and MCAAT checklists. Snapshot on file dated 2026-05-21.

## Audience

- IPAC and unit pay clerks validating entitlement starts, stops, and corrections.
- Finance offices running the MCAAT-inspected pay processes.
- Auditors working the internal audit chapters against unit records.

## Chapter map

Loci the portal's MCAAT checklists cite.

- Chapter 1 with Appendices F and G. Pay policy foundations, cited across finance administrative management and pay processes.
- Chapters 2, 3, 5 through 9, 11 through 13, and 17. Entitlement-family chapters riding separations and pay processes checklists, paired with DoDFMR Volume 7A.
- Chapter 16. Internal audit, the backbone of the finance internal audit processes checklist, paired with FPM Volume 2 paragraph 080202 and Volume 3 Chapter 18.
- Chapter 21. Cited through separations and pay processes.
- Paragraphs 060102, 090101.d, 090106, and 090108. Milpay and outbound management checklist anchors, 170104.C(5)(c) alongside.
