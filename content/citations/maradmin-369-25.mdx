## Scope

MARADMIN 369/25 announces updated Marine Corps policy for administering the Basic Needs Allowance based on Change 3 to DoDI 1341.15. The message implements the 200 percent Federal Poverty Guideline threshold effective 12 May 2025 per Public Law 118-159 Section 621 and cancels the prior implementation messages, MARADMIN 673/22 and MARADMIN 345/23. The message text cites the cancelled base message as reference F under the number 002/23, an error in the message itself. The December 2022 implementation message is MARADMIN 673/22.

## Audience

Marines with dependents screening for BNA eligibility. S-1s assisting applications and submitting payment authorizations. O-5 commanding officers and SCMCAs holding non-delegable adjudication authority. PACs reporting TTC 694/693. Commanders maintaining monthly BNA records.

## Key provisions

- Threshold. 200 percent of the FPG, effective 2025-05-12. The threshold began at 130 percent, rose to 150 percent per MARADMIN 345/23, and sits at 200 percent under this message.
- Eligibility. Active-duty Marines with DEERS-enrolled dependents reported in MCTFS who have completed initial entry-level training. Reserve Marines qualify on active-duty orders of 180 days or more.
- GHI definition. All pays and allowances except BNA itself, regardless of taxability, plus all household member income. Federal food assistance income is excluded.
- Computation. ((2.0 x FPG for the payment year) - (prior calendar year GHI)) / 12, prorated at 1/30th for partial months. No retroactive payments before the certification date.
- Application. Through the MOL EPAR module to the O-5 commanding officer or SCMCA with LES, prior-year tax return, pay stubs, W-2s, and household income documents.
- Notification. Commands notify pre-screened Marines by page 11 within 30 days of the MOL advisory. Marines have 180 days to apply after notification or joining their first PDS.
- Termination. GHI exceeding the limit ends BNA 60 days after the date of ineligibility. Disciplinary pay reductions do not increase BNA. Dual-military couples jointly elect a single recipient.
- Reporting. PACs report TTC 694/693 per the MCTFSPRIUM. Payment occurs within 30 days of the certified application. Commanders submit monthly reports to HQMC MPO and forward page 11s and applications to the OMPF.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoDI 1341.15 CH-3. The governing BNA policy this message implements.
- Public Law 117-81 Section 601. Established the BNA, codified at 37 U.S.C. 402b.
- Public Law 118-159 Section 621. Expanded eligibility and raised the threshold to 200 percent.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MARADMIN 673/22. The December 2022 Marine Corps implementation at 130 percent, cancelled by this message.
- MARADMIN 345/23. Change 1, raised the threshold to 150 percent in July 2023, cancelled by this message.
- MCTFSPRIUM 8-81201. Reporting instructions for the BNA transaction.
- The MCAAT military pay checklist cites this message for BNA notification, adjudication, computation, reporting, and recertification checks.
