## Citation clarification

No standalone Marine Corps Order carries the bare number 11240. Motor transport policy issues under fully numbered orders in the 11240 series. Two govern the licensing and management requirements the portal cites:

- MCO 11240.106D, Non-Tactical Vehicle Management.
- MCO 11240.118A, Licensing Program for Tactical Wheeled Motor Transport Equipment.

Both source PDFs sit in the unit policy archive as of 2026-06-12.
