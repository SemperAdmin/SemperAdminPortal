## Scope

This advisory addresses Standard Flat Rate Per Diem Allowance For Georgia Deployment Program-International Security Assistance Force (Gdp-Isaf). Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. REQUEST ALL ADDRESSEES DISSEMINATE THIS PAA TO SUBORDINATE COMMANDS.
2. PURPOSE. REF (A), SUB-PARAGRAPH U4090-E, AUTHORIZES THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A REDUCED PER DIEM RATE WHICH THE MARINE CORPS USES TO ESTABLISH STANDARD FLAT RATE PER DIEM RATES FOR OCONUS DEPLOYED UNITS. THIS PAA ESTABLISHES THE STANDARD FLAT RATE PER DIEM ALLOWANCES FOR UNITS DEPLOYED AS PART OF THE GDP-ISAF.
3. BACKGROUND. MEMBERS ASSIGNED TO THE GDP-ISAF ARE ENTITLED TO PER DIEM WHEN THEY ARE IN A TEMPORARY DUTY STATUS (TAD/TDY) AWAY FROM THE PERMANENT DUTY STATION (PDS). PER DIEM MUST BE REDUCED WHEN GOVERNMENT QUARTERS AND MESSING ARE PROVIDED AT NO COST. THIS REDUCED PER DIEM RATE RESULTS IN MEMBERS BEING ENTITLED TO THE MEALS (SUBSISTENCE) PORTION OF PER DIEM AND THE APPLICABLE OCONUS INCIDENTAL EXPENSE (IE) RATE. HOWEVER, THE MEALS PORTION WILL BE AUTOMATICALLY WITHHELD/DEDUCTED FROM THE MEMBERS PER DIEM RATE REIMBURSEMENT IN ORDER TO PAY FOR GOVERNMENT PROVIDED MEALS, SO THE MARINES WILL SEE ONLY THE IE PORTION IN THEIR PAY.
4. ACTION. ON 1 MARCH 2015 THE FOLLOWING REMARK AND RATE WAS ESTABLISHED FOR THE GDP-ISAF UNITS BASED ON THEIR LOCATION AND THE CIRCUMSTANCES AT THAT LOCATION: GEORGIA DEPLOYMENT PROGRAM-INTERNATIONAL SECURITY ASSISTANCE FORCE (REPUBLIC OF GEORGIA) CODE (H) IE RATE $3.50.
5. THIS MESSAGE HAS BEEN COORDINATED WITH CMC (MPO), M&RA; (MI/MISSA), AND P&R; (RFF).
