## Scope

PAA 09-20 issues amplifying guidance on the documentation Marines and family members must carry for Air Mobility Command-Patriot Express (AMC-PE) flights to OCONUS destinations. The advisory sets the minimum count of funded travel order sets at the AMC gateway and requires appropriation data on every order so USTRANSCOM bills the Marine Corps for space-required travel. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.
- Marines and family members reporting to the AMC gateway for OCONUS PE flights.

## Action language

- Paragraph 1. This Personnel Admin Advisory (PAA) provides amplifying guidance for required documentation for Air Mobility Command-Patriot Express (AMC-PE) flights. References (a) through (c) remain in effect. All current PAAs are located under the reference tab of the MISSA portal located at: [https://eis.usmc.mil/sites/missa/default.aspx](https://eis-op.usmc.mil/sites/missa/default.aspx).
- Paragraph 2, orders. In accordance with the references, Marines and family members checking-in at the AMC gateway for Outside Continental United States (OCONUS) PE flights must bring at least four (4) complete sets of funded travel orders when reporting to the AMC gateway.
- Paragraph 3, appropriation data. All orders must contain appropriation data for USTRANSCOM to properly bill the Marine Corps for space-required travel.
- Paragraph 4, paraphrased. Confirms coordination with DC I&L and DC P&R.
