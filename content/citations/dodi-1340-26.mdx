## Scope

DODI 1340.26 establishes DoD policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for Assignment Incentive Pay (AIP) and Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP). The instruction was issued effective 25 September 2017 with Change 1 issued 11 January 2019. The instruction reissues the prior AIP instruction and incorporates and cancels the prior DODI 1304.27 (Award and Administration of Special Duty Assignment Pay, April 22, 2015), bringing AIP and SDAP under one DoD instruction.

## Audience

DoD Component Heads. Military Service personnel offices. Marine Corps Programs and Resources Division (RFM). S-1 officers and IPAC pay clerks processing AIP and SDAP transactions. Marines designated to AIP-eligible assignments and SDAP-eligible billets.

## Content scope

- Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP) eligibility, designation, and rate framework.
- Assignment Incentive Pay (AIP) eligibility, designation, and rate framework.
- Authority delegations to Component Heads for designation.
- Rate establishment and review framework.
- Reporting requirements.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 7220.12R Marine Corps SDAP Program. Marine Corps implementation.
- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 8 (SDAP). Pay framework.
- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 15 (AIP). Pay framework.
- 37 U.S.C. 307 (Special Duty Pay) and 37 U.S.C. 307a (Assignment Pay or Special Duty Pay). Statutory authority.

## Status

Active. DODI 1340.26 superseded DODI 1304.27 effective 25 September 2017. Older citations to DODI 1304.27 reference the cancelled instruction. New SDAP and AIP citations should reference DODI 1340.26.
