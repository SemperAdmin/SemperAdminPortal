## Scope

PAA 14-13 clarifies health Service Treatment Record (STR) forwarding for personnel transferred to the Temporary Disability Retired List (TDRL) under the new STR transfer procedures in reference (a). The advisory title covers STR transfer of transitioning Service Members to the Veterans Administration Records Management Center (VARMC). The clarification carves out TDRL personnel, whose STRs continue on the existing route through Manpower Management Support Branch (MMSB) to the Navy Medicine Records Activity (NMRA). Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.
- Separation and retirement processors handling records for transitioning Marines.

## Action language

- Paragraph 1. ADDRESSEES ARE ASKED TO READDRESS THIS PAA TO SUBORDINATE ORGANIZATIONS/UNITS.
- Paragraph 2. REF A OUTLINES NEW PROCEDURES FOR THE TRANSFER OF STRS.
- Paragraph 3, paraphrased to correct a source typo. The PAA clarifies STR forwarding for personnel transferred to the TDRL. The STR submission process for all TDRL personnel remains unchanged. STRs for TDRL personnel continue forward to Manpower Management Support Branch (MMSB) for further distribution to the Navy Medicine Records Activity (NMRA).

## References cited inside the PAA
