## Scope

This advisory addresses Clarification For The Proper Transfer Of Health Service Treatment Records (STRS) Of Transitioning Service Members To The Veterans Administration Records Management Center (Varmc). Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. ADDRESSEES ARE ASKED TO READDRESS THIS PAA TO SUBORDINATE ORGANIZATIONS/UNITS.
2. REF A OUTLINES NEW PROCEDURES FOR THE TRANSFER OF STRS.
3. THIS PAA CLARIFIES THE FORWARDING OF THE STR FOR PERSONNEL TRANSFERRED TO THE TEMPORARY DISABILITY RETIRED LIST (TDRL). THE PROCESS FOR THE SUMBISSION OF STRS OF ALL TDRL PERSONNEL REMAINS UNCHANGED. STRS FOR TDRL PERSONNEL WILL CONTINUE TO BE FORWARDED TO MANPOWER MANAGEMENT SUPPORT BRANCH (MMSB) FOR FURTHER DISTRIBUTION TO THE NAVY MEDICINE RECORDS ACTIVITY (NMRA).
