## Scope

MARADMIN 007/25 cancels MARADMIN 178/24, Reserve Component Telecommute Policy Update. The cancellation issues under the DoD Memorandum for Senior Pentagon Leadership, Commanders of the Combatant Commands, and Defense Agency and DoD Field Activity Directors dated 2025-01-31.

## Audience

- Reserve component leaders and unit administrators. Discontinue reference to MARADMIN 178/24.
- Reserve component Marines with telecommute arrangements. Route questions through the chain to Reserve Affairs.
- Reserve Affairs Policy Branch (RAP-1). Adjudicates questions.

## Cancellation details

- MARADMIN 178/24, Reserve Component Telecommute Policy Update, is cancelled in full.
- The DoD Memorandum dated 2025-01-31 drives the cancellation.
- The portal records no replacement telecommute policy message.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD Memorandum for Senior Pentagon Leadership, Commanders of the Combatant Commands, and Defense Agency and DoD Field Activity Directors, dated 2025-01-31.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 178/24. Reserve Component Telecommute Policy Update. Cancelled by this message.
