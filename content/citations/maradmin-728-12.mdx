## Scope

MARADMIN 728/12 updates Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (HDIP) for demolition duty. The MARADMIN supersedes MARADMIN 361/09 with updated rates and eligibility. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- EOD Technicians and personnel performing demolition duty. Eligible for HDIP. The portal identifies MOS 2305 officers and MOS 2336 enlisted Marines as the typical recipients.
- Commanders of units with EOD or demolition billets. Authorize demolition duty by competent orders.
- Disbursing officers and S-1 or IPAC Marines. Process and report HDIP under the updated rate framework.

## Substantiation requirements the portal audits

- Supporting units submit the key supporting documents substantiating demolition pay to the reporting unit monthly, no later than 5 days after the end of the month. MCAAT special and hazardous duty pay check 21 audits this against MCO 3571.2H Chapter 2 paragraph 7, MCO 1000.6 Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraph 7, MARADMIN 361/09, MARADMIN 728/12, and MCTFSPRIUM 1-10100.6 and 2-20103.
- Demolition duty pay reports in accordance with DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapter 24 paragraph 2405. The portal records the monthly rate at 150 dollars under DoDFMR Chapter 24.
- Units confirm only authorized billets and personnel receive demolition pay.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD FMR Volume 7A. HDIP rate framework. Chapter 24 paragraph 2405 carries the demolition duty entitlement.
- 37 U.S.C. Section 301. Special pay authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 361/09. Prior HDIP Demolition Duty MARADMIN. Superseded by this MARADMIN.
- MCO 3571.2H. Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) Program. Substantiation authority for demolition duty pay.
- MCTFSPRIUM 1-10100.6 and 2-20103. MCTFS reporting procedures for demolition pay.
- MCAAT Special and Hazardous Duty Pay program, sponsor DCS M&RA. Audits demolition pay substantiation.
