## Scope

MARADMIN 349/23 discontinued the utilization, offering, and execution of the 6.8 percent discounted lump sum Assignment Incentive Pay (AIP) option, effective 2023-07-01. The message simplified AIP processing and reduced administrative burden. AIP now pays monthly only for newly assigned Marines. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines in AIP-eligible assignments. Apply the simplified rate structure.
- Disbursing officers and unit pay sections. Process AIP under the new framework. Report AIP and VSI in MCTFS.
- HQMC Manpower Plans and Policy (MPO). Owns the policy.

## AIP after the discontinuation

- The 6.8 percent discounted lump sum AIP option ended for newly assigned Marines effective 2023-07-01.
- Monthly AIP pays in two installments, on the 1st and 15th of the month, up to $1,500 per month per single AIP rate with a combined cap of $3,500 per month, per the portal AIP page sourced to this message and MARADMIN 628/20.
- Paragraph 4. The lump sum Volunteer Supplemental Incentive (VSI) program is not impacted. Marines who volunteer for and receive orders to a Special Duty Assignment, Recruiters, Drill Instructors, and MSG Detachment Commanders, remain eligible for lump sum VSI based on assignment and months of commitment.
- MCAAT pay checks cite the message for reporting SDA, AIP, and VSI per DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapter 8 paragraph 080101, MCO 7220.12R paragraph 4, and MCTFSPRIUM 9-90201, 9-90202, and 9-91701.
- MCAAT supporting unit checks confirm AIP and VSI lump sum Page 11 entries file in the OMPF within 10 working days, citing MCO 1070.1 with this message.

## Connection to higher policy
