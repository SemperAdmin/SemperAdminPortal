## Scope

MARADMIN 628/20 clarifies monetary incentives for Special Duty Assignments (SDA), Type-1 (T1) screenable billets, and other enlisted assignments eligible for Assignment Incentive Pay (AIP) for Fiscal Year 2021. The MARADMIN replaces SDAP with AIP for non-SDA billets and introduces Volunteer Supplemental Incentive (VSI) for SDA volunteers. Effective 1 October 2020.

## Audience

Career planners, S-1, and IPAC personnel processing SDAP, AIP, and VSI. Commanders signing AIP and VSI agreements. Marines volunteering for or selected to SDA, T1 screenable billets, or other AIP-eligible billets.

## Three incentive types

- SDAP. Special Duty Assignment Pay. Paid monthly to Marines assigned to SDA billets. SDA billets are Recruiters, Drill Instructors, and MSG Detachment Commanders.
- AIP. Assignment Incentive Pay. Paid monthly or as a single lump sum to Marines assigned to difficult-to-fill assignments, locations, or units. Replaces SDAP for all previously SDAP-designated non-SDA billets.
- VSI. Voluntary Supplemental Incentive. Paid monthly or as a single lump sum only to Marines who volunteered for a FY21 SDA class or second-tour SDA. VSI sits on top of SDAP and AIP.

## Eligible billets

- SDA. Recruiters (8411/8421/5524), Drill Instructors (0911), MSG Detachment Commanders (8156).
- T1 screenable. MSG Watch Standers (8156), MCSF Guards (8152/8154) at KingsBay or Bangor PRP-qualified, Combat Instructors (0913).
- Other. Career Recruiters (8412/8422), Sergeants Major and First Sergeants in listed special billets, MARSOC CSO (0372) and SOCS (8071), Career Planners (4821).
- Joint. White House Military Office (WHMO), DTRA, SEA to the CJCS.

## Amount tables (selected)

- Recruiters. SDAP 150 monthly plus AIP 225 monthly or 7,400 lump sum for a 36-month tour. VSI 10,000 lump sum for a 36-month FY21 tour, or 6,700 for a 24-month second tour.
- Drill Instructors. SDAP 150 monthly plus AIP 150 monthly or 4,900 lump sum for a 36-month tour. VSI matches the recruiter VSI.
- MSG Detachment Commander. SDAP 150 monthly plus VSI 10,000 lump sum for a 36-month tour or 6,700 for a 24-month second tour.
- MCSF Guards and MSG Watch Standers. AIP 75 monthly or 2,500 lump sum for a 36-month tour.
- Combat Instructors. AIP 150 monthly or 4,900 lump sum for a 36-month tour.
- Career Recruiters. AIP 375 monthly or 12,300 lump sum for a 36-month tour.
- SMMC. AIP 450 monthly or 10,100 lump sum for a 24-month tour.

## Operative content

### Documentation

- AIP and VSI agreements are documented on NAVMC 118 (11). The form establishes the months of commitment, the assignment, and the lump sum amount.
- Agreements route via EPAR in Marine Online to the local reporting unit or PAC for MCTFS reporting.
- A copy posts to the Marine's OMPF. If posting fails, email MMRP-20 at smb.manpower.mmrp@usmc.mil.

### Recoupment

Marines who do not serve the full term repay the unearned, prorated portion of the lump sum. Recoupment does not apply when a Marine fails to complete the agreement for reasons beyond the Marine's control (death, retirement or separation for disability).

### TSP

Marines who want to contribute SDAP, AIP, or VSI to TSP make a TSP Incentive percentage election in myPay the month prior to receiving the payment. These contributions are not matched and count against the annual Elective Deferral Limit.
