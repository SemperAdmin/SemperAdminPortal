## Scope

MCO 5354.1 is the Marine Corps Prohibited Activities and Conduct (PAC) Prevention and Response Program order series. The order sets command responsibilities for preventing and responding to prohibited activities and conduct, including equal opportunity and anti-harassment policy statements, command climate assessment, and annual PAC training. The current MCPEL listing is MCO 5354.1G Admin Ch 1, published 2024-05-31. The source PDF on file from the 2026-04 archive snapshot carries a Marine Corps Directives Compliance with Executive Order 14168 cover.

## Audience

- Commanders. Own the PAC program, the policy statements, the appointments, and the climate assessment.
- Equal Opportunity Advisors (EOA) and Equal Opportunity Coordinators (EOC) appointed in writing.
- Equal Opportunity Representatives (EOR) and Command Resilience Team members.
- S-1 staff processing Page 11 entries tied to EO and PAC matters.

## Operative content

- The commander appoints the EOA and EOC in writing and ensures 85 percent completion of annual PAC training in MCTIMS, per the commander oversight matrix for the PAC functional area.
- An incoming commander re-issues the Equal Opportunity and PAC Prevention and Response Policy Statement and the Anti-Harassment and Human Relations Policy Statement under personal signature within 30 days of assuming command, per the commander turnover checklist citing MCO 5354.1F.
- The commander appoints EORs and Command Resilience Team members, per the turnover checklist citing MCO 5354.1F and MARADMIN 468/23.
- A new commander conducts a CCA with DEOCS within 90 days of assuming command, documents the corrective action POA&M, and annotates completion in the annual FITREP, per MARADMIN 468/23 paragraph 4 and MCO 5354.1F.
- The annual CCA runs under MCO 5354.1F Chapter 2 within the window of 01 August to 30 November and begins no later than 31 October, per MARADMIN 468/23.

- IGMC Functional Area 5354.1, Prohibited Activities and Conduct Prevention and Response Program (PAC). CoRE category, sponsored by M&RA (MPE), effective 2025-05-15.
