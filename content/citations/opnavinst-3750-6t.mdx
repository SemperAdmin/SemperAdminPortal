## Scope

OPNAVINST 3750.6T issues U.S. Navy policies, guidelines, procedures, and responsibilities for the Naval Aviation Safety Program. The instruction governs Marine Corps aviation safety as well, since Marine aviation operates under the Naval Aviation Safety umbrella. The original portal entry was filed as "MCO 3750.6" but no MCO with that number exists. The "MCO 3750.6" alias preserves back-compat for any inline reference written before the renumber.

## Key changes in 3750.6T

- References to the Naval Safety Center (NAVSAFECEN) replaced with Naval Safety Command (NAVSAFECOM).
- Web-Enabled Safety System (WESS) replaced with the Risk Management Information (RMI) program of record. RMI is the only authorized application for reporting and recording mishaps, hazards, and safety incidents in the DON.

## Administration

Per OPNAVINST 5215.17A, CNO N09F reviews this instruction annually around the anniversary of its issuance date.
