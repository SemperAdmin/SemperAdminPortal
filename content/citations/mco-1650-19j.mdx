## Scope

MCO 1650.19J prescribes procedures and instructions for issuing decorations, medals, and awards inside the Marine Corps. The order sets policy guidelines for submission of personal and unit award recommendations. Awards encompass all personal, unit, campaign, and service awards across Marine Corps personnel and Marine Corps organizations.

## Authority

Commandant of the Marine Corps publishes the order. Manpower Management Awards Branch (MMMA) maintains the manual. Higher policy lives in SECNAVINST 1650.1J for the DON-level framework.

## Audience

Award writers at every command level. S-1 awards clerks. Commanders signing field-approved awards. Marines submitting unit-award recommendations through their chain of command.

## Enclosures

- Encl 1. Awards Administration. Foundational administrative rules for the awards program.
- Encl 2. Administrative Procedures for Submission of Recommendations for Personal Decorations and Other Special Recognition.
- Encl 3. Procedures for Submission of Recommendation for Unit Awards.
- Encl 4. Sample Award Certificates.
- Encl 5. Procedures for Submitting Awards through MGCAPS.

## Change history

CH 1 published 15 October 2012. Administrative update. Replaced the SECNAVINST 1650.1H reference with the current 1650.1J SECNAVINST. Filed behind the promulgation page.

## Reserve applicability

Applies to the Marine Corps Reserve. Reserve commands forward field-approved awards and award recommendations through their chain to CMC (MMMA) per the rules in the order.

## Authority chain

Commanding Generals and Commanding Officers approve awards within their authority. Award recommendations exceeding that authority forward to CMC (MMMA). Commanders submit policy-change recommendations to CMC (MMMA) via the chain of command.
