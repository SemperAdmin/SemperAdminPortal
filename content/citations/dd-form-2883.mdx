## What this form is

Form 2883, Government Travel Charge Card Alternate Credit Worthiness Evaluation. Fillable PDF on file from the 2026-04-29 forms archive snapshot. The form is the self-certification path for a GTCC applicant who declines the soft-pull credit check during the application process.

## Purpose printed on the form

> of the government travel charge card, individuals who decline a credit score and complete this form will only be issued a restricted card having half the credit limits of a standard card. If you previously agreed to a credit score and were denied a card as a result, you may not obtain a card through this self certification. Individuals who decline both a credit score and completion of this form will not be issued a [...]

Quoted from the form's Privacy Act statement.

## Who prepares, signs, and files

The applicant completes and signs the form in Adobe Reader, saving it locally before signing. The applicant submits it directly to the unit Agency Program Coordinator (APC), never through CitiManager, because the form carries PII and is handled outside the card vendor's portal. The APC processes it with the rest of the application package alongside the Statement of Understanding and the Travel Card 101 training certificate.

## What the form drives

The applicant must answer every question affirmatively to qualify. An affirmative form results in a Restricted Individually Billed Account with default limits of 4,000 dollars credit, 250 dollars cash, and 100 dollars retail, against the Standard IBA defaults of 7,500 dollars credit, 250 dollars cash, and 250 dollars retail. Both cards look identical, and commanders validate temporary limit raises for mission needs. Significant adverse indicators on the form are a documented reason Citibank denies an application, and the APC works the deficiency with the Marine or escalates. Training, the SOU, and this form typically complete in a single afternoon within the 2 to 4 week application timeline.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

The governing authorities are DoDI 5154.31 Volume 4 and MCO 4600.40C. The portal covers the workflow on the Apply for a GTCC, GTCC Overview, and Standard vs Restricted IBA pages.

## Status and access

The external link opens the official form page. The GTCC application form itself changed in September 2025 with older versions rejected after 2026-01-01, so confirm current APC guidance before submitting the package.
