## Scope

MARADMIN 229/25 announces noncompetitive Corporal to Sergeant promotions for Marine Corps Reserve recruiters, signed 2025-05-15. The message mirrors the Active Component procedure in MARADMIN 103/25 with Reserve-specific scope. Promotions under the message bypass the JEPES cutting score competition. Date of rank and effective date run retroactive to 2025-03-01 for corporals already serving on recruiting duty.

## Audience

- Reserve Component corporals on recruiting duty who have not attained Sergeant. Promote noncompetitively once eligible.
- Corporals attending the Basic Recruiter Course or the Basic Prior Service Recruiter Course. Promote the first of the month after check-in at the new duty station.
- Recruiting District Commanders. Submit the endorsed roster to MMPB-11.
- Reserve career planners and unit commanders. Prepare certificates and verify readiness before warrant delivery.

## Action items

- Eligibility standard. 12 months time in grade, 36 months time in service, PME complete for the component including the Command Sponsored Corporals Course.
- Scenario one. Eligible corporals currently serving on recruiting duty promote with date of rank and effective date of 2025-03-01.
- Scenario two. Eligible corporals attending the Basic Recruiter Course or the Basic Prior Service Recruiter Course promote with date of rank and effective date of the first of the month after checking into the new duty station.
- Applicability, paragraph 8. The policy applies to the Marine Corps Reserve, Prior Service Recruiting (PSR), and Extended Active Duty (EAD) recruiters. MARADMIN 103/25 paragraph 8 excludes EAD recruiters, this message covers them.
- Process. Same framework as MARADMIN 103/25. The Recruiting District Commander submits the endorsed roster to MMPB-11. Commanders confirm the Marine is ready to assume Sergeant duties before delivering the warrant.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions. Governing promotion authority.
