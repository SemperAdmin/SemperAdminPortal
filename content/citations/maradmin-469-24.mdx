## Scope

MARADMIN 469/24 announces Fiscal Year 2025 Intended Military Occupational Specialty (IMOS) promotions for the Active Component, signed 2024-10-04. The MARADMIN allows promotion in an intended, training PMOS rather than the current PMOS for Marines transitioning between occupational fields. MARADMIN 515/25 cancels the message and carries the non-competitive IMOS framework forward. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines selected for promotion under an IMOS during FY25. The message governed their promotion procedure.
- Career planners and IPACs. Processed IMOS promotions through MCTFS under the message.
- HQMC MMPB-11. Adjudicated eligibility and issued the framework.

## Cancellation and successor framework

- The portal promotions page names this message the predecessor FY25 framework, cancelled by MARADMIN 515/25.
- MARADMIN 515/25, effective 2025-10-01 per the portal promotions page, designates eight non-competitive entry-level Sergeant IMOSs. 0211, 0372, 1751, 2336, 3044, 4133, 4821, 5821.
- MARADMIN 515/25 paragraph 2 authorizes no non-competitive IMOS promotions to Corporal. The path is Corporal to Sergeant only.
- Successor eligibility requires reenlistment into a designated IMOS, no attained Sergeant grade, TIG and TIS per MCO P1400.32D, and PME complete.
- Apply the successor message to new IMOS promotion actions. This message stays a historical reference for FY25 actions.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions. Governing promotion authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 515/25. Non-Competitive Intended Military Occupational Specialty Promotions. Successor framework, signed 2025-11-03 per the message index.
- The portal groups IMOS promotion among the non-competitive Corporal to Sergeant paths bypassing the JEPES cutting score competition.
