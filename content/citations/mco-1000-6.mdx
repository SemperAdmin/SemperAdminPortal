## Scope

MCO 1000.6 (ACTSMAN) provides guidance for personnel management across the Marine Corps. Personnel management is the planning, organizing, directing, and supervising of procurement, development, use, and administration of personnel in accordance with the references. The order is the procedural backbone for assignment, classification, and travel decisions affecting every Marine.

## Audience

Every Marine touches the ACTSMAN through assignment, MOS classification, and TAD or PCS travel events. Monitors and the Manpower Management Officer Assignment Branch (MMOA) and Enlisted Assignment Branch (MMEA) work from this manual. S-1 and IPAC personnel execute the procedural sequences in the field. Commanders implement the personnel management framework.

## Authority

Commandant of the Marine Corps publishes the order. Deputy Commandant, Manpower and Reserve Affairs (DC, M&RA) owns the program. The Manpower Information Support Systems (MIF) Section maintains the manual.

The order does not amend Navy Regulations or Navy Department General Orders. The ACTSMAN operates inside that framework.

## Report required

Field Flight Performance Board Recommendations. Report Control Symbol EXEMPT. Enclosure 1, Chapter 1, Section 2, Paragraph 11.g(2).
