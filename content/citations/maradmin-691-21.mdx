## Scope

MARADMIN 691/21 announces the Calendar Year 2022 Continuation Pay Program for Blended Retirement System (BRS) participants. The MARADMIN sets eligibility, multipliers, and processing rules for Continuation Pay paid at 12 years of service. The program window is Calendar Year 2022. Applicability is BRS-enrolled Marines.

## Audience

- BRS-enrolled Marines approaching 12 years of service. Apply for Continuation Pay.
- Disbursing officers. Process Continuation Pay under DoD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 1.
- IPAC and reporting unit Marines. File the statement of understanding and report the pay in MCTFS.
- Career planners. Track Continuation Pay eligibility.

## Processing requirements the portal audits

- Statement of understanding. NAVMC Form 11905 for Active and NAVMC Form 11908 for Reserve files in the OMPF within 10 working days from the date reported on unit diary. MCAAT military pay check 48 audits this at the IPAC against MCO 1070.1, MARADMINs 691/21 and 084/22, and MCTFSPRIUM 9-91800.
- MCTFS reporting. BRS Continuation Pay reports under TTC 450. MCAAT reporting unit check 35 audits the transaction against DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapter 66, MARADMINs 691/21 and 084/22, and MCTFSPRIUM 9-91800.
