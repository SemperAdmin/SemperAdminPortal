## Scope

DODI 1322.25 implements DoD Directive 1322.08E for the operation of voluntary education programs across the DoD. The instruction sets tuition assistance eligibility criteria and the memorandum of understanding requirement binding every educational institution in the DoD TA program. The instruction incorporated and cancelled DODI 1322.9.

## Audience

- Marines using tuition assistance toward off-duty education.
- Education services officers and installation voluntary education coordinators.
- Career planners and commanders authorizing TA.

## Operative content

- Participating institutions sign the DoD MOU and provide meaningful information, cost, financial aid, completion rates, and outcome data.
- TA caps review annually, the portal states the working rates as 250 dollars per semester hour and 4,500 dollars per fiscal year, with reservists qualifying on qualifying active duty orders. TA is a current-service benefit.
- The Joint Services Transcript documents military learning for credit evaluation.
- The DoD Postsecondary Education Complaint System receives student complaints against participating institutions.
- Military departments set service-specific eligibility criteria and management controls, coordinated through the Interservice Voluntary Education Board.

## Applicability

OSD, the Military Departments, the Joint Staff, the Combatant Commands, the DoD Inspector General, defense agencies, and field activities.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD Directive 1322.08E. The voluntary education policy the instruction implements.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine and lower policy

- MCO 1560.25. The Marine Corps Lifelong Learning Program operationalizing the instruction.
- MCO 1700.30, MCO 1700.31, and MCO 1500.55. The Marine Corps personal and professional development orders citing it.
- MCO 1001R.1L MCRAMM Chapter 12. Reserve education benefits alongside TA.
