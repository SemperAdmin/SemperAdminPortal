## Scope

DODI 1322.25 implements policy established by DoD Directive 1322.08E for the operation of voluntary education programs across the DoD. The instruction sets eligibility criteria for tuition assistance (TA) and the memorandum of understanding (MOU) requirement for every educational institution providing education through the DoD TA Program. The instruction incorporates and cancels DODI 1322.9.

## Audience

Marines participating in the Tuition Assistance Program. Installation Voluntary Education Coordinators. Career planners advising Marines on educational benefits. Commanders authorizing TA participation. The Marine Corps Lifelong Learning Program (MCO 1560.25) operationalizes this DoD instruction at the Service level.

## Key policy points

- Educational institutions providing programs through DoD TA must provide meaningful information to students about cost, financial aid, completion rates, and outcome data.
- Annual review and notification process applies to changes in the uniform semester-hour TA caps and annual TA ceilings.
- Military Departments provide service members with a Joint Services Transcript (JST).
- The DoD Postsecondary Education Complaint System provides service members, spouses, and adult family members a venue to register student complaints.
- Military Departments establish Service-specific TA eligibility criteria and management controls.
- The Interservice Voluntary Education Board governs cross-Service coordination.

## Applicability

OSD, the Military Departments, the Joint Staff, the Combatant Commands, the DoD IG, Defense Agencies, DoD Field Activities, and every DoD Component.
