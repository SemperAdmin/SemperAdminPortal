### Section 90700

Introduction to Hostile Fire Pay and Imminent Danger Pay

### Section 1

The following reporting instructions are covered in this section:

a.  IMMINENT DANGER PAY (IDP) ; and

b.  HOSTILE FIRE PAY (HFP).

### Section 2

Reporting instructions for applicable transactions are listed in the section.  Diary input authority (Active, Reserve, and Headquarters Marine Corps) and correction indicators (Delete/Add and Delete/Erroneous) for applicable transactions are listed in Paragraph 130104 (APPENDIX D - TTC REPORT).
