## Scope

MCO 7220.24P governs the Marine Corps Selective Retention Bonus (SRB) and Broken Service Selective Retention Bonus (BSSRB) Programs. The SRB is a flat-rate cash bonus paid to Marines reenlisting in eligible primary MOSs and reenlistment zones. The order supplies the standing policy and procedural framework. Dollar amounts, zone tables, kicker programs, and eligibility windows publish each fiscal year in an SRB MARADMIN riding on this order.

> To provide policy and procedural guidelines for the administration of the Marine Corps Selective Retention Bonus (SRB)/Broken Service Selective Retention Bonus (BSSRB) Program as authorized by references (a) through (l).

Quoted from the document's opening section.

## Audience

- Marines weighing reenlistment against zone, PMOS bonus, and kicker options.
- Career Counselors matching Marines to zones, bonus tables, and Statements of Understanding.
- Commanders endorsing SRB-eligible reenlistments and approving lateral move recommendations.
- S-1, Disbursing, and Finance staff supporting payment computation and recoupment actions.

## Content scope

Repo evidence names program areas rather than chapters. Eligibility by reenlistment zone, PMOS bonus tables, kicker programs, payment methodology, and recoupment. Zones run A through F by years of active military service.

## Operative content

- The SRB pays only on new reenlistment contracts. Extensions add obligated service without bonus entitlement, per the portal's extensions page sourced to this order.
- Bonus amounts key to zone, PMOS, and rank, with kickers on top, per this order and the annual SRB MARADMIN.
- Marines who fail to serve in the PMOS or skill for which the bonus paid face recoupment of the unearned portion, absent an authorized waiver or out-of-skill assignment.
- Marines who apply for a PMOS-changing or commissioning program and then reenlist in their PMOS are not normally SRB eligible and are subject to recoupment of unearned payments.
