## Scope

MARADMIN 537/23 announces the reactivation of the Command and Marine Profile application within Marine Online (MOL). The message restores leader access to the application after it was offline. The application provides leaders with consolidated Marine readiness, performance, and personnel data. Date signed is 2023-10-23. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Commanders and unit leaders. Access the Command and Marine Profile application through MOL.
- S-1 staff. Reference the application for Marine readiness and personnel data.
- HQMC Manpower Management Information Division. Owns the application and the reactivation notice.

## The application

- The Command and Marine Profile application runs inside MOL and returns to service under this message.
- The application consolidates Marine readiness, performance, and personnel data into a single leader-facing view.
- The Marine Corps Total Force System (MCTFS) feeds the application as the system of record.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- Marine Corps Total Force System (MCTFS). System of record feeding the application.
- Marine Online (MOL). Hosting platform.
- MARADMIN 412/23. Transition from Optical Digital Imaging Records Management System to the Official Military Personnel File. The portal lists this reactivation as companion modernization.

## Status

Released 2023-10-23 per the policy index date signed. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA, Manpower Management Information Division. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force. Active.
