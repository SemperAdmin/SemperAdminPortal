## Scope

MARADMIN 361/09 set Hazardous Duty Incentive Pay (HDIP) authority and rates for Marines performing demolition duty, signed 2009-06-12. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force. MARADMIN 728/12, signed 2012-12-20, supersedes the message with updated rates and eligibility.

## Audience

- EOD officers (MOS 2305) and EOD technicians (MOS 2336) performing demolition duty. The portal identifies these as the typical demolition HDIP recipients.
- Commanders of units with EOD or demolition billets. Authorize demolition duty by competent orders.
- Disbursing officers and S-1 or IPAC Marines. Report demolition pay in MCTFS and process HDIP under DoD FMR Volume 7A.
- Inspectors executing the MCAAT Special and Hazardous Duty Pay checklist.

## Demolition pay substantiation and reporting

- Report Demolition Duty Pay in accordance with DoDFMR Volume 7A Chapter 24 paragraph 2405, MCO 3571.2H Chapter 2 paragraph 7, MCO 1000.6 Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraph 7, and MCTFSPRIUM 9-90101 Table 9-5. MCAAT items 42 and 08 audit this requirement and cite this message.
- Confirm only authorized billets and personnel receive demolition pay, and prepare the key supporting documents substantiating payment. MCAAT item 07 audits this and cites this message.
- Submit substantiation documents to the reporting unit monthly, no later than 5 days after the end of the month. MCAAT item 21 audits this and cites this message with MARADMIN 728/12 and MCTFSPRIUM 1-10100.6 and 2-20103.
- The portal records the demolition duty HDIP rate at 150 dollars per month under the DoDFMR framework.
