## Scope

PAA 09-12 covers the Integrated Disability Evaluation System (IDES), the joint DoD-VA process by which DoD determines whether wounded, ill, or injured Service Members are fit for continued military service and by which DoD and VA determine appropriate benefits for Service Members separating or retiring for service-connected disability. The IDES goal is to complete the VA disability compensation delivery phase within 30 days from separation. The advisory directs that the DD Form 214 reside in the OMPF and route to VA on time to support the 30-day benefits delivery target.

## Audience

- IPAC retirement processors and DD 214 preparers.
- Wounded Warrior administration teams.
- S-1 desks tracking IDES-track separations.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.

## Content scope

- IDES framework as the joint DoD-VA disability determination process.
- 30-day benefits delivery target from date of separation.
- DD Form 214 OMPF placement and VA routing requirement.
- Coordination between DoD, VA, IPAC, and the Wounded Warrior Regiment.
