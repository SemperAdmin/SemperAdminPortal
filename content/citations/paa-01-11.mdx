## Scope

PAA 01-11 provides updated reporting requirements on personnel deployed in support of Operation New Dawn (OND) and Operation Enduring Freedom (OEF). The advisory covers reporting instructions for deploying personnel, personnel returning from OIF/OEF, other personnel, and deployment entitlement entries. Units supporting OND/OEF use reach-back administration to the fullest extent possible.

## Audience

- G-1 and S-1 staff reporting deployed personnel.
- Administrative Reachback Center (ARC) personnel.
- Installation Personnel Administrative Center (IPAC) personnel.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.

## Content scope

- Personnel reporting instructions for deploying personnel in support of OND/OEF.
- Personnel reporting instructions for personnel returning from OIF/OEF.
- Personnel reporting instructions for other personnel.
- Deployment entitlement entries.
