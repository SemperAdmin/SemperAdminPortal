## Scope

PAA 01-11 provides updated reporting requirements on personnel deployed in support of Operation New Dawn (OND) and Operation Enduring Freedom (OEF). Released 2011-01-14, the advisory covers reporting instructions for deploying personnel, personnel returning from OIF/OEF, other personnel, and deployment entitlement entries. Units supporting OND/OEF use reach-back administration to the fullest extent possible.

## Audience

- G-1 and S-1 staff reporting deployed personnel.
- Administrative Reachback Center (ARC) personnel.
- Installation Personnel Administrative Center (IPAC) personnel.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.

## Action language

- Report deploying personnel in support of OND/OEF under the advisory's personnel reporting instructions.
- Report personnel returning from OIF/OEF under the return reporting instructions.
- Report other personnel categories under the residual reporting instructions.
- Enter deployment entitlements per the advisory's entitlement entry guidance.
- Route administration for units supporting OND/OEF through reach-back channels to the fullest extent possible.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- The portal's I&I demobilization processing reference, sourced to MCRAMM Chapter 10 (MCO 1001R.1L), lists this advisory alongside PAA 02-12 and PAA 01-13 as deployment reporting authorities.
- PAA 02-12 issued the follow-on OEF reporting requirements on 2012-01-20 and kept PAAs 01-00 through 01-12 in effect.
- PAA 02-21 carries the later Unit Deployment Program reporting update built on the same reach-back administration model.
