## What this order is

MCO 5300.17A with Admin Change 2, Marine Corps Substance Abuse Program. Subject line quoted from the order: MARINE CORPS SUBSTANCE ABUSE PROGRAM, From Commandant of the Marine Corps to the Distribution List. Source document on file from the 2026-06-12 unlocked copy.

## What Admin Change 2 did

> The Department of Defense Inspector General determined in DODIG-2019-091 the Marine Corps Substance Abuse Counseling Centers (SACC) were making diagnoses and diagnostic impressions outside the scope of non-medical services. DoDIG recommended the Substance Abuse Program policy MCO 5300.17A be revised to remove diagnostic language and capabilities from the Order.

Quoted from the change transmittal. The change replaces diagnostic terms with identification language throughout.
