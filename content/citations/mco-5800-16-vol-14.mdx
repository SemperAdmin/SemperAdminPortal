## What this volume is

MCO 5800.16 Volume 14, ENLISTED NONJUDICIAL PUNISHMENT MATTERS AND PREPARATION OF THE UNIT PUNISHMENT BOOK. One of 17 volumes of the Legal Support and Administration Manual, short title LSAM, the Marine Corps legal services authority issued by CMC (JA). Original volume signed 18 MAY 2021 with no published changes. The volume prescribes the NAVMC 10132 Unit Punishment Book form for recording enlisted NJP, sets the punishment limits by grade of the imposing officer, gives the SMCR forfeiture calculation formulas, and provides the Figure 14-1 notice of intent to vacate suspended punishment.

## Lead excerpt

> NJP provides commanders with an essential and prompt means of maintaining good order and discipline and also promotes positive behavior changes in Service members without the stigma of a court-martial conviction. Although a disciplinary measure more serious than administrative corrective measures, NJP is less serious than trial by court-martial.

Quoted from section 0102 of the volume.
