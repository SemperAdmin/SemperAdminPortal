## Scope

DODI 4525.09 establishes DoD policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for the Military Postal Service (MPS). The instruction was issued 10 July 2018 with Change 3 effective 23 October 2024. The instruction cancels and consolidates DoDI 4525.7 (Military Postal Service and Related Services, 1981) and DoDI 4525.08 (DoD Official Mail Management, 2006), bringing military postal operations and official mail management under one DoD instruction.

## Audience

DoD Military Postal Service Agency (MPSA). Service postal organizations. Military Post Offices (MPOs), Army Post Offices (APOs), Fleet Post Offices (FPOs), and Diplomatic Post Offices (DPOs). Marine Corps postal officers (MOS 0161). Unit commanders supervising postal operations. Official mail managers.

## Content scope

- Military Postal Service organization and responsibilities.
- MPO, APO, FPO, DPO designation and operations.
- Mail accountability and security.
- Official mail management.
- DoD postal Inspector General oversight.
- Interagency coordination with United States Postal Service (USPS).
- Mail routing and transportation.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 5110.4B. Marine Corps Postal Affairs and Official Mail Program. Marine Corps implementation.
- USPS Domestic Mail Manual (DMM) and International Mail Manual (IMM).
- 39 U.S.C. Postal Service statutory authority.

## Status

Active. Change 3 issued 23 October 2024. The instruction is the current DoD-level military postal service authority.
