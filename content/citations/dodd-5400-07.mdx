## Scope

DODD 5400.07 establishes DoD policy and assigns responsibilities for the DoD Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Program in accordance with Section 552 of Title 5, United States Code. The directive operationalizes FOIA request processing across the DoD Components including initial denial authority, FOIA appeals, fee schedules, exemptions, public reading room requirements, and annual reporting. The directive replaced the prior regulation-form DoD 5400.7-R.

## Audience

DoD Component FOIA officers. Marine Corps S-1 officers, S-1 chiefs, and adjutants processing FOIA requests affecting unit records. Initial denial authorities. FOIA appeal authorities. Public Affairs Officers coordinating media FOIA requests. Legal staff coordinating FOIA exemption review.

## Content scope

- DoD FOIA program policy and responsibilities.
- FOIA request receipt, tracking, and routing.
- Initial denial authority designation.
- FOIA exemptions (Exemptions b(1) through b(9)).
- Privacy Act overlap with FOIA (Exemption b(6) personal privacy).
- Public interest test and segregability rules.
- Fee schedule and fee waiver criteria.
- FOIA appeals procedures.
- Annual FOIA reporting to Department of Justice.
- Coordination with the Office of Government Information Services (OGIS).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy
