## Scope

MARADMIN 515/25 announces non-competitive Intended Military Occupational Specialty (IMOS) promotions to Sergeant for the Active Component, effective 2025-10-01. The message succeeds MARADMIN 469/24, the FY25 IMOS framework, which it cancels. Corporals in one of eight designated MOSs earn promotion to Sergeant by meeting the eligibility criteria rather than competing against a JEPES cutting score. Applicability is the Active Component only.

## Audience

- Corporals holding or training into one of the eight designated MOSs. Eligible for non-competitive promotion to Sergeant.
- Career planners. Confirm the reenlistment requirement before processing.
- S-1 staff and IPACs. Process the promotion warrant through MCTFS.

## Program rules

- The eight covered MOSs are 0211, 0372, 1751, 2336, 3044, 4133, 4821, and 5821.
- There are no non-competitive IMOS promotions to Corporal, per paragraph 2. The path is Cpl to Sgt only.
- Marines disenrolled from the program are administratively reduced effective the date of disenrollment notification, per paragraph 2.d.
- The policy is not applicable to the Marine Corps Reserve, per paragraph 4.
- PME completion is required. TIG and TIS requirements follow MCO P1400.32D.
- Promotion under this path bypasses the JEPES cutting score competition.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO P1400.32D, Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions. Parent promotion authority.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 469/24. FY25 IMOS Promotions. Predecessor framework, cancelled by this message.
- MARADMIN 103/25. Active Duty Recruiter Promotion, effective 2025-03-01. Companion non-competitive Cpl-to-Sgt path.
- MARADMIN 229/25. Reserve Component Recruiter Promotion. Companion non-competitive path.
- MARADMIN 222/26. Cancels the SULI path from MARADMIN 225/23 effective 2026-10-01 and leaves this message as one of three remaining non-competitive paths.
