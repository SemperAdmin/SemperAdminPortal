## Scope

This advisory addresses Cancellation Of Paa 02-19 Guidance On Area Clearance/Dependent. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. All current PAAs are located under the reference tab of the MISSA portal. Request addressees disseminate this PAA to subordinate org/units as applicable.
2. PAA 02-19 is hereby canceled. All guidance regarding the requirement of area clearance and dependent entry approval for permanent change of station (PCS) moves to overseas is available in the Department of Defense Foreign Clearance Guide (https://www.fcg.pentagon.mil).
