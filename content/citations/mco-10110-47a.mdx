## Scope

MCO 10110.47A promulgates policy for administering Basic Allowance for Subsistence (BAS) and the meal card program for military personnel, and cancels MCO 10110.47 W/CH 1. Portal BAS pages carry the working rule. BAS is a monthly cash payment offsetting the cost of food, officers almost always draw it, and enlisted members draw it when not on a meal card.

## Audience

- PAC specialists, section SNCOs, and branch heads (0111, 0170) processing BAS, meal card, and commuted rations transactions.
- Commanders publishing the unit BAS policy and authorizing commuted rations in writing.
- S-1 and unit personnel managing meal card issue and return.
- MCAAT and audit inspectors testing milpay, reserve pay, and deployment subsistence items.

## Enclosure map

- Enclosure 1, Chapter 1. BAS and meal card program administration. Inspection items cite Chapter 1 paragraph 5, subparagraphs a through l.

## Operative content

- Commuted rations reporting follows Chapter 1 paragraphs 5.a through 5.l, documented on the NAVMC 10522 Commuted Rations Action form.
- The NAVMC 10522 is completed and filed in the OMPF within 10 working days from the date reported on the unit diary. Chapter 1 paragraphs 5.g-i and 5.l, with IRAM paragraph 5006.2.p.
- The unit BAS policy is reviewed every 3 years. Chapter 1 paragraph 5.g.
- Key supporting documents authorizing commuted rations reach the reporting unit within 5 days of authorization. Chapter 1 paragraph 5.l.
- Discount meal rate or field rations for Essential Unit Messing, field duty, and members traveling together with no or limited reimbursement during ADOS and AT report per Chapter 1 paragraph 5.b.
- Pay entitlements for Marines attached under a humanitarian status report per Chapter 1 paragraph 5.a.
- Separate rations authorization is issued in writing by the command. Members on meal cards do not draw BAS.
