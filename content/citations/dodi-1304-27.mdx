## Scope

DODI 1304.27 established DoD policy and procedures for the award and administration of Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP). The instruction was issued 10 April 2009 and reissued 22 April 2015. The instruction is HISTORICAL. DODI 1340.26 (Assignment and Special Duty Pays, effective 25 September 2017) incorporated and cancelled the 2015 version of DODI 1304.27 and combined AIP and SDAP under one DoD instruction.

## Audience

S-1 officers and IPAC pay clerks reviewing legacy SDAP records or transactions predating 25 September 2017. The 1304.27 instruction remains useful for historical context on SDAP rate establishment, position assessment methodology, and program origins.

## Status

HISTORICAL. The current DoD-level SDAP authority is DODI 1340.26 effective 25 September 2017. Cite DODI 1340.26 for active SDAP policy and procedure questions.
