## Scope

Citi Commercial Cards introduced a new confirmation step in CitiManager for Agency/Organization Program Coordinators (APCs) closing cardholder accounts. Effective 13 June 2026. The change applies to both the Individual Online Maintenance workflow and the Bulk UI workflow. Selecting an account status to close now triggers a Permanent Account Closure confirmation pop-up. The APC must check three acknowledgement boxes and click the Permanently Close Account button to complete the action.

## Audience

- APCs at Hierarchy Level 3 and Hierarchy Level 4 running CitiManager closures.
- Component Program Manager (CPM) at HQMC P&R-RFF overseeing APC execution.
- Commanders certifying the unit GTCCP for FA inspection.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists supporting the S-1 GTCCP function.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. A new Permanent Account Closure confirmation pop-up appears when an APC selects an account status to close an account in Individual Online Maintenance or Bulk UI.
2. The APC must acknowledge three statements by clicking each checkbox before the Permanently Close Account button activates.
3. Acknowledgement 1. The account cannot be reopened if moved to this status.
4. Acknowledgement 2. The impacted user or associated accounts must reapply for a new card if they require credit access in the future.
5. Acknowledgement 3. The action is permanent and cannot be reversed.
6. Exception. Only accounts closed with status Closed - Temporary Block can be reopened. Every other Closed option in the dropdown results in permanent closure and requires a new application from the cardholder.

## Connection to higher policy

- DoD GTCC Regulations Section 041106. Closure of Accounts Upon Separation. Defines when an APC closes a cardholder account on separation, retirement, or transfer.
- DoD GTCC Regulations Section 041107. Review Reports to Identify Inactive Accounts for Closure. Defines the APC review cycle that triggers closures.
- MCO 4600.40C Enclosure 2. GTCCP Management Procedures. Service-level policy for closure execution.
