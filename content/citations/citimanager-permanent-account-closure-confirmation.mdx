## What this is

A CitiManager system bulletin confirming permanent GTCC account closure behavior, effective 13 June 2026. Vendor system documentation, not a public publication.

## Audience

Agency Program Coordinators (APCs) at Hierarchy Level 3 and 4 who close accounts in CitiManager. Component Program Managers overseeing Marine Corps GTCCP execution. S-1 sections and commanders tracking IGMC GTCCP Functional Area exposure. The portal APC appointment and duties page is the primary citing surface.

## Operative content

- Effective 2026-06-13, CitiManager requires a three-checkbox Permanent Account Closure confirmation before a closure executes. The pop-up activates the Permanently Close Account button only after all three acknowledgements.
- Closed - Temporary Block is the only closure status the APC is able to reverse. Every other Closed dropdown option in CitiManager Individual Online Maintenance and Bulk UI triggers permanent closure.
- A permanent closure forces the cardholder to reapply for a new card. Portal guidance prices the mistake at a 60 to 90 day reapproval cycle and an audit finding on the next IGMC GTCCP FA inspection.
- Portal guidance to APCs. Verify the intended status before clicking. When in doubt, select Closed - Temporary Block so the account stays reversible.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- DoD GTCC Regulations Section 041106, Closure of Accounts Upon Separation. The APC closes the IBA on separation, and this bulletin governs how the closure executes in CitiManager.
- MCO 4650.39A Chapter 9. DTS-side debt clears through the DTS DMM before closure.
- MCO 4600.40C Enclosure 2. Marine Corps GTCCP execution procedures around the closure step.
- Portal cardholder pages note Citi permanently closes accounts cancelled for sustained delinquency, a separate path from APC-initiated closure.

No public URL exists. CitiManager bulletins reach Agency Program Coordinators through the Citi portal at https://home.cards.citidirect.com/ behind account authentication. APCs pull the current bulletin from the portal message center.
