## Scope

Volume 14 of DoD Financial Management Regulation 7000.14-R covers Administrative Control of Funds and Antideficiency Act Violations. The volume sets DoD policy for administrative control of appropriated funds, defines Antideficiency Act (ADA) violations, and lays out the investigation and reporting process a violation triggers. The underlying statute is the Antideficiency Act, 31 U.S.C. Sections 1341 through 1517, per the MARADMIN 350/11 registry entry.

## Audience

Fund control personnel across the Department of the Navy. Comptrollers and fiscal officers administering appropriated funds. Commanders accountable for obligations against available funds. Training coordinators tracking the Volume 14 Chapter 2 appropriations law training requirement.

## Operative content

- Volume 14 Chapter 2 requires DON fund control personnel to receive appropriations law training or refresher training at least every three years. MARADMIN 350/11 implements the requirement for the Marine Corps and divides personnel into two tiers, Tier 1 reporting to the DON and Tier 2 maintaining certificates locally.
- MARADMIN 350/11 responded to GAO-08-1063 (dated 2008-09), Improvements Needed in Antideficiency Act Controls and Investigations. The training requirement remains active per the MARADMIN registry entry.
