### Section 80402

**Discount Meal Rate** (TTC 475)

### Section 1

Enlisted members assigned to Essential Station Messing (ESM) will see their Basic Allowance for Subsistence (BAS) deducted for the current DMR daily rate.  Refer to PRIUM Table 8-1.  Report DMR as follows:

```
TTC 475 000 [A] STRT DISC MEAL RATE [B] |
- [A] DOA (Date of Action; never 00000000)
- [B] ED (Effective Date; never 00000000)
```
or

```
TTC 475 001 [A] STOP DISC MEAL RATE [B] |
- [A] DOA (Date of Action; never 00000000)
- [B] ED (Effective Date; never 00000000)
```
Note:  When reporting TO LWAS (TTC 115 030) on a member with a DMR deduction, the system will offset DMR deduction with a credit for the period of leave (TTC 115 030 will not generate a 75% DMR credit).  Therefore, TTC 475 001 STOP DMR MUST NOT be reported. EXCEPTION:  When there is Permissive Temporary Additional Duty in conjunction with LWAS and the member is not returning to the PDS.  Example:  Member departs on 10 days Permissive Temporary Additional Duty in conjunction with LWAS at 0800, 12 February 2015; TTC 475 001 STOP DMR will be reported effective 20150212 and the system will credit the 75% DMR for 20150212.

### Section 2

'BAS DISC MEAL RATE' is a deduction when the Marine is assigned to ESM.  If there is a period that the commanding officer determines the Marine should be credited the money deducted by 'BAS DISC MEAL RATE', report as follows:

```
TTC 475 002 [A] CRED DISC MEAL RATE [B] TO [C] |
- [A] DOA (Date of Action; never 00000000)
- [B] FROM DATE (From Date; never 00000000)
- [C] TO DATE (To Date; never 00000000)
```
### Section 3

If there is a period that the commanding officer determines the Marine should be checked 'BAS DISC MEAL RATE', report as follows:

```
TTC 475 003 [A] CHEK DISC MEAL RATE [B] TO [C] |
- [A] DOA (Date of Action; never 00000000)
- [B] FROM DATE (From Date; never 00000000)
- [C] TO DATE (To Date; never 00000000)
```
### Section 4

To check a Marine's DMR or Field Rats at 75 percent that was previously reported, report:

```
TTC 475 005 [A] CHEK DISC MEAL RATE 75% [B] |
- [A] DOA (Date of Action; never 00000000)
- [B] ED (Effective Date; never 00000000)
```
or

```
TTC 375 003 [A] CHEK FLD RATS RATE 75% [B] |
- [A] DOA (Date of Action; never 00000000)
- [B] ED (Effective Date; never 00000000)
```
