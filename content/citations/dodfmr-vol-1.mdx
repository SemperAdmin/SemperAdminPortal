## Scope

Volume 1 of DOD Financial Management Regulation 7000.14-R sets general financial management information, systems, and requirements for the Department of Defense. The volume frames the financial-management framework that every DOD component operates inside. It defines the CFO authority, federal accounting standards hierarchy, FFMIA compliance posture, the standard financial information structure, the financial-records retention rules, and the Advana enterprise data repository.

## Audience

Marine Corps comptrollers, resource managers, and S-8 personnel. Manpower Strategy Branch (M&RA MX) and audit-readiness leads. IPAC personnel applying the Vol 1 Ch 9 retention rules to MILPAY Key Supporting Documents. Commanders accountable for financial-statement audit compliance.

## Why this matters for the Marine Corps

The MARADMIN 601/24 directive to upload Key Supporting Documents to UD/MIPS, and MAA 1-20 (MARADMIN 015/20) requiring KSDs in the OMPF Field Folder, both cite Vol 1 Chapter 9 retention rules. The 10-year retention floor for pay-supporting documents traces directly to Vol 1 Chapter 9 Figure 9-1.

## Chapter map

- Chapter 1. Chief Financial Officer of the Department of Defense. Defines the CFO authority. June 2024.
- Chapter 2. Federal Accounting Standards Hierarchy. The framework for federal accounting authoritative literature. September 2022.
- Chapter 3. Federal Financial Management Improvement Act Compliance. FFMIA posture and DOD reporting. January 2025.
- Chapter 4. Standard Financial Information Structure. The DOD-wide financial data model. June 2023.
- Chapter 6. Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) Financial Management Awards Program. September 2024.
- Chapter 7. Department of Defense Standard Chart of Accounts. The unified COA used across components. March 2024.
- Chapter 9. Financial Records Retention. Defines retention periods for financial records including MILPAY KSDs. March 2024.
- Chapter 10. Advana - Common Enterprise Data Repository for the Department of Defense. The enterprise data platform for financial information. June 2023.
- Appendix A. Object Classification. The DOD object class system. March 2023.
