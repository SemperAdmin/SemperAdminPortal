## Scope

MARADMIN 150/16 sets the quarterly Page 11 counseling requirement for Marines NOT RECOMMENDED for promotion to Corporal and Sergeant. The counseling recurs each quarter while the Marine holds the NOT RECOMMENDED status, and the cadence tracks with the promotion cycle now managed under the Junior Enlisted Performance Evaluation System (JEPES). Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Commanding officers. Conduct quarterly Page 11 counseling for every Marine held NOT RECOMMENDED for promotion to Corporal or Sergeant.
- S-1 staff. Track Page 11 entries and match each NOT RECOMMENDED entry with its counseling record.

## Page 11 requirement

- Units complete required Page 11 entries for negative promotion recommendations under MCO P1400.32D paragraphs 2101, 2500.4, and 2500.5.
- MCO P1070.12K w/CH 1 paragraph 4006.2.f sets the Page 11 entry standard.
- The MCAAT admin promotions checklist cites this message together with MARADMIN 153/21, 559/23, and 185/25 for the negative promotion recommendation entry requirement.
- MARADMIN 559/23 confirms all NOT RECOMMENDED entries continue to require a Page 11 under MCO P1400.32D w/CH2.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO P1400.32D. Marine Corps Promotion Manual, Volume 2, Enlisted Promotions. Parent order for promotion recommendations.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1070.12K (IRAM). Marine Corps Individual Records Administration Manual. Page 11 framework.
- MARADMIN 153/21. Clarification to the promotion recommendation process and remedial promotions under JEPES.
- MARADMIN 559/23. Junior Enlisted Remedial Promotions Automation. Carries the Page 11 requirement forward.
- MARADMIN 185/25. Cancellation of obsolete promotion MARADMINs and upcoming changes to MCO P1400.32D.
