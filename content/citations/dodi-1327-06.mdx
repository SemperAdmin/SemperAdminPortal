## Scope

DODI 1327.06 establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for military leave, liberty, and administrative absences across the Department of Defense. The instruction provides uniformity among the Military Services under Section 704 of Title 10, U.S.C. It also provides respite from the work environment, time for personal business and emergencies, and rules that maximize use of accrued leave to minimize loss and reduce unused-accrual cost.

## Audience

Service-level leave-policy owners. Marine Corps Manpower Plans and Policy (MPO). Commanders and leave approvers across DoD. S-1 personnel implementing leave categories defined by the parent DoD policy.

## Authority chain

USD(P&R) publishes the instruction. ASD(M&RA) and DASD(MPP) carry the program at the OSD level. Secretaries of the Military Departments and the Commandant of the Coast Guard implement at the Service level. The Joint Chiefs of Staff Chairman coordinates joint impacts.

## Relationship to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 1050.3J. Regulations for Leave, Liberty, and Administrative Absence. The Marine Corps-level implementation of this DoD instruction.
- MARADMIN 625/24. Advance notification of forthcoming changes to MCO 1050.3J. Cites DoDI 1327.06 along with NDAA legislative authority for parental leave changes.

## Reissue

The 7 August 2025 version reissues and cancels the original DoDI 1327.06 dated 16 June 2009 (as amended). It incorporates and cancels predecessor documents per Paragraph 1.3.
