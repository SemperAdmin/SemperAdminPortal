## Scope

The Commanders Readiness Handbook is the Marine Corps quick-reference guide for unit commanders on readiness reporting requirements. The handbook covers Marine Corps readiness reporting structure, statutory responsibilities, force readiness ratings (P, S, R, T, C), command readiness reporting cadence, report content, commander narratives, and assessment frameworks. The handbook complements MCO 3000.13B and DRRS reporting procedures.

## Audience

Marine Corps unit commanders. S-3 operations officers. DRRS reporting administrators. New commanders receiving the initial readiness brief.

## Content scope

- Marine Corps Readiness overview.
- Statutory responsibilities and reporting requirements.
- Force readiness rating definitions (P-1 through P-4, S, R, T, C).
- Command readiness reporting cadence and types.
- Report content overview.
- Commander narrative and assessment guidance.
- DRRS and DRRS-MC interface (prior to consolidation per MARADMIN 518-21).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 3000.13B. Marine Corps Readiness Reporting.
- MARADMIN 518-21. DRRS-MC Consolidation to DRRS.
- DoDD 7730.65. DoD Readiness Reporting System.
