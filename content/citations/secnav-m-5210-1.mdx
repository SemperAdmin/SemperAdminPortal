## Scope

SECNAV M-5210.1 provides the guidelines and procedures for the Department of the Navy Records Management Program. The manual sets policies and procedures for life-cycle management of DON records, including creation, maintenance, use, and disposition. The manual implements SECNAVINST 5210.8D and operates under SECNAVINST 5430.7N.

## Audience

Records officers and records-management leads across the DON. S-1 and IPAC personnel maintaining unit records. Commanders accountable for records of subordinate Marines. Every DON activity that creates, receives, or maintains records.

## Manual structure

Part III carries the disposition schedules listed by Standard Subject Identification Code (SSIC). The schedules incorporate National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) records disposition policies and guidelines. The current revision made major changes to the disposition schedules in Part III.

## Authority

Department of the Navy Chief Information Officer publishes the manual. The Office of the Chief Information Officer maintains it. The manual replaces SECNAV Manual M-5210, the December 2005 predecessor.

## Connection to other Marine Corps records policies

- MCO 5210.11F. Marine Corps Records Management Program. Implements the DON framework at the Marine Corps level.
- SECNAVINST 5210.8D. DON Records Management Program. Sets the parent policy.
- MARADMIN 601/24. Directs UD/MIPS as the central document repository for MILPAY Key Supporting Documents.
- MARADMIN 015/20 (MAA 1-20). Directs OMPF Field Folder use for pay-supporting documents.

## Status

Active. Revised November 2007. NARA approval covers the disposition schedules. Retention floors set in this manual flow through DODFMR Vol 1 Chapter 9 for financial records.
