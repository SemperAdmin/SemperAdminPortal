## Scope

PAA 06-13 addresses a recurring error on the DD Form 2656 (Data for Payment of Retired Personnel) that affected wounded warriors being medically retired. IPACs were missing the "No Eligible Dependents" block in Section IX, Block 26.g, which left the form blank and triggered automatic SBP enrollment at the full amount. The advisory directs IPACs to mark Block 26.g for single Marines being retired to prevent unintended full SBP enrollment.

## Audience

- IPAC retirement processors and the IPAC pay clerks completing DD Form 2656.
- S-1 chiefs auditing retirement packages.
- Wounded Warrior administration teams.
- Single Marines undergoing medical retirement.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.

## Content scope

- DD Form 2656 Section IX, Block 26.g completion requirement for single Marines without eligible dependents.
- Automatic SBP enrollment trigger when Block 26.g is left blank.
- DFAS auto-enrollment in the full SBP amount when no DD 2656 is received.
- Specific impact on Wounded Warriors being medically retired.

## Connection to higher policy

- DD Form 2656 Data for Payment of Retired Personnel.
- Survivor Benefit Plan (SBP) statutes under 10 U.S.C. Chapter 73.
- DFAS retirement pay processing rules.

## Status

Released 2013 by CMC Washington DC MRA MI. Historical record but the underlying rule applies whenever a single Marine without dependents executes the DD Form 2656 today. POC at MI was CWO5 David W. Howell.
