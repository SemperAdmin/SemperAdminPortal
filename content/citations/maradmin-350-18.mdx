## Scope

MARADMIN 350/18 establishes the Marine Corps SkillBridge Employment Training Program, signed 2018-06-25. The program allows transitioning Service Members to receive private-sector training, education, and apprenticeships during the last 180 days of military service. Units manage participation as Permissive Temporary Additional Duty (PTAD). Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines within 180 days of EAS. Eligible for SkillBridge.
- Career planners. Process SkillBridge applications.
- Unit commanders and S-1s. Manage SkillBridge PTAD.
- HQMC M&RA Marine and Family Programs. Owns the program.

## Inspection footprint

- MCAAT Outbound Management items 11 and 16 ask whether the unit manages SkillBridge PTAD properly. Both items cite this message alongside DoDI 1322.29, MCO 1050.3J, MARADMIN 280/24, and NAVMC 1700.2.
- Inspectors audit SkillBridge PTAD management at the unit level under these references.
