## Source text

### Section 1

Certain items in officer and enlisted records can be corrected only by the Servicing Manpower Information Systems Support Office.  Discrepancies noted in these items will be submitted via the Trouble Tickets module in MOL.  Reporting units must ensure that the correspondence to their Servicing Manpower Information Systems Support Office includes the following information: a.  Information in MCTFS as shown on the BIR or BTR. b.  Information contained in the reporting unit's records and/or the Marine's Service Record.

c.  When applicable, if the correct information is different from that contained in the Service Record, provide additional information to assist in research.
