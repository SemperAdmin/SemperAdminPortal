## Scope

MARADMIN 388/25 publishes Fiscal Year 2026 class dates for Cornerstone, the Commandant's Combined Commandership Course. The message is the FY26 edition of the annual Cornerstone scheduling series and succeeds MARADMIN 377/24, the FY25 message signed 2024-08-17. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Board-selected commanders, command senior enlisted leaders, and spouses. Required attendance.
- Marine Corps University, Lejeune Leadership Institute. Hosts the course at Quantico VA.
- Slating and turnover staffs. Confirm the incoming commander attended before slating.

## Course requirement

- The message publishes the FY26 class schedule.
- The portal's commander turnover checklists list Cornerstone attendance as a required pre-change-of-command action under the annual Cornerstone MARADMIN, with this message as the FY26 authority.
- Outgoing commanders confirm the incoming commanding officer has attended before slating.
- Aviation commanders attend Cornerstone like ground commanders. Cornerstone is general command preparation. The MAWTS-1 Aviation Commander's Course is the aviation-specific pair. Both are required.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 377/24. FY25 Cornerstone class dates. Predecessor in the annual series.
- CMC White Letter 3-17 and the commander turnover framework. The portal pairs Cornerstone with the Status of Command Brief and turnover checklist requirements in the pre-command preparation sequence.
