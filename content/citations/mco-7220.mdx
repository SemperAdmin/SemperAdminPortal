## Citation clarification

No standalone Marine Corps Order carries the bare number 7220 or P7220. Authors have cited the series number in error. Military pay and allowance policy issues under fully numbered orders in the 7220 series, MCO 7220.52F Foreign Language Proficiency Bonus and MCO 7220.24P Selective Retention Bonus among them, riding on DoD 7000.14-R Volume 7A.
