## Scope

MARADMIN 412/23 announces the transition from the Optical Digital Imaging Records Management System (ODI-RMS) to the OMPF Records Management Application (O-RMA) for storage and maintenance of the Official Military Personnel File (OMPF), signed 2023-08-22. The MARADMIN consolidates Marine Corps record-keeping into the modernized OMPF platform accessed through Marine Online. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- Marines accessing personal records. Pull the OMPF through MOL.
- S-1 staff and unit administrators. Apply O-RMA submission procedures and maintain unit access.
- HQMC MMPB-22. Owns the OMPF transition.
- Inspectors executing the MCAAT Systems Management checklist.

## O-RMA access requirement

- MCAAT Systems Management items 04, 05, and 14 ask whether the unit has and maintains access to the OMPF Records Management Application (O-RMA) to validate and update each Marine's official record. Every item cites this message with MCO 1070.1.
- Units treat O-RMA as the platform of record for OMPF storage and maintenance following the ODI-RMS retirement.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 1070.1. Official Military Personnel File. Order receiving the transition.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1070.12K. Marine Corps Individual Records Administration Manual (IRAM).
- MARADMIN 537/23. Reactivation of the Command and Marine Profile Application, signed 2023-10-23. Companion records modernization.
