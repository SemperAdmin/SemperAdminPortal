## Scope

MCO 8023.3 provides policy and procedural guidance for the management and implementation of the Marine Corps Personnel Qualification and Certification (QUAL/CERT) Program for Class V Ammunition and Explosives (A&E). The program governs which Marines are qualified and certified to perform Class V A&E duties.

> This Order provides policy and procedural guidance for the management and implementation of the Marine Corps Personnel Qualification and Certification (QUAL/CERT) Program for Class V Ammunition and Explosives (A&E). This Order is a complete revision and should be reviewed in its entirety.

Quoted from the document's opening section.

## Audience

- Marines performing Class V A&E duties under the QUAL/CERT Program.
- Ammunition and ordnance staff managing the qualification and certification program.
- Commanders, whose change-of-command turnover pairs this order with AA&E accountability actions.
- Units maintaining TAMIS ammunition plans.

## Content scope

Repo evidence names the program without chapter detail. Qualification and certification of personnel for Class V ammunition and explosives duties. The 2025 revision is a complete revision, review it in its entirety per its own opening.

## Operative content

- Outgoing commanders reconcile a 100 percent serialized AA&E inventory by serial number, lot, and DODIC and provide TAMIS ammunition plan status before relief. The turnover checklist cites this order beside MCO 5530.14A and MCO 5100.29C Volume 8 for the action.
