## What this order is

MCO 1020.34H, Marine Corps Uniform Regulations, signed 2018-05-01. The cover reads MARINE CORPS UNIFORM REGULATIONS over PCN 10200150000 with Distribution Statement A. ## Scope

The order governs wear of the uniform across the Marine Corps: authorized articles, grooming standards, insignia, awards placement, and civilian attire rules. Cited across the portal's awards pages for precedence and device wear.
