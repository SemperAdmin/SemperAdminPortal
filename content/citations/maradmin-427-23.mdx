## Scope

MARADMIN 427/23 updates Unit Punishment Book policy for officer Nonjudicial Punishment. When an officer receives NJP, the UPB entry, the acknowledgment of the opportunity to submit matters, and any response provide to the local personnel administration center for inclusion into the officer's Official Military Personnel File (OMPF).

## Audience

Commanding officers, S-1 officers, S-1 chiefs, legal clerks, and IPAC personnel processing officer NJP.

## Required action

- Officer NJP UPB entry routes to IPAC for OMPF inclusion.
- Officer acknowledgment of the opportunity to submit matters routes to IPAC.
- Officer response (if submitted) routes to IPAC.
- IPAC includes the package in the officer's OMPF.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 5800.16 Volume 14 paragraph 011105. Officer UPB completion follows the standard NJP UPB rules.
- MCO P1070.12K paragraph 4007. OMPF adverse material rules.
- MCO 5800.16 Volume 15. Officer Misconduct framework.
