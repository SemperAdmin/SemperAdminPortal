## Scope

SECNAV M-5210.2 is the Department of the Navy Standard Subject Identification Codes (SSIC) Manual. The manual provides the standardized subject-classification system that identifies every DON correspondence, directive, and record. Every Marine Corps directive, every piece of official correspondence, and every records file carries an SSIC drawn from this manual.

## Coverage

- SSIC numbering structure across DON-wide subject groups.
- Specific SSICs for personnel, administration, legal, communications, supply, transportation, intelligence, medical, security, training, and other functional areas.
- Cross-reference index for locating SSIC by subject keyword.
- Application rules for directives (per MCO 5215.1K and SECNAV M-5215.1) and records management (per MCO 5210.11F and SECNAV M-5210.1).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 5215.1K Marine Corps Directives Management Program references SECNAV M-5210.2 for SSIC assignment on every directive.
- MCO 5210.11F Marine Corps Records Management Program references SECNAV M-5210.2 for records classification.
- SECNAV M-5215.1 DON Directives Issuance System Manual references SECNAV M-5210.2 for directive SSIC assignment.
- SECNAV M-5210.1 DON Records Management Manual references SECNAV M-5210.2 for records SSIC assignment.

## Status

Active. The SSIC table updates periodically through change transmittals. Distribution Statement A.
