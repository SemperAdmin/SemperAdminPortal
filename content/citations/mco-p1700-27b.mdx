## Scope

MCO P1700.27B is the policy manual for Marine Corps Community Services (MCCS). The manual issues policies governing the management, operation, and administration of MCCS programs across the Marine Corps. It implements DoD and other higher-authority directives where appropriate. Specific operational policies for MCCS programs and support functions are published in separate directives.

## Audience

MCCS Directors at every Marine Corps installation. MCCS-affiliated program managers. Marine and Family Programs personnel. Commanders whose installations host MCCS facilities. Marines and family members using MCCS programs including fitness, recreation, family support, and Marine Corps Exchange operations.

## Authority

Commandant of the Marine Corps publishes the manual. Deputy Commandant, Manpower and Reserve Affairs (DC, M&RA (MR)) receives recommended changes. Applicable to the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Coverage

The manual operationalizes the MCCS program inside the framework set by:

- DOD 7000.14-R Volume 13. Nonappropriated Funds Policy and Procedures.
- DOD Instruction 1015.10. Programs for Military Morale, Welfare, and Recreation (MWR).
- DOD Instruction 1015.15. Procedures for Establishment, Management, and Control of Nonappropriated Fund Instrumentalities (NAFI).
- DOD Instruction 1015.13. Procedures for Implementing Public-Private Ventures (PPVs) for MWR and Armed Services Exchange Category C Revenue-Generating Activities.
- SECNAVINST 5720.44B. DoN Public Affairs Policy and Regulations.
- DOD 5500.7-R. Joint Ethics Regulation (JER).
- DoD Directive 7050.1. Defense Hotline Program.
- DoD Directive 1332.35. Transition Assistance for Military Personnel.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 1560.25. Marine Corps Lifelong Learning Program. MCCS Directors operate the LL program at installation level.
- MCO 1754.6C. Marine Corps Family Team Building Program. MCCS-coordinated family support.
- MCO P12000.11A. Civilian personnel programs that intersect with MCCS staffing.
- MCO P5800.16. LSAM. Legal administrative procedures for MCCS-related issues.
