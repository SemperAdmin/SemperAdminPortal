## Scope

MCO 10110.47 is the base revision of the Basic Allowance for Subsistence (BAS) and Meal Card Program order. MCO 10110.47A, signed 2013-09-25, cancels MCO 10110.47 W/CH 1 and carries the program today. The substantive breakdown lives at /citations/mco-10110-47a. Portal admin pages citing the number without the revision letter resolve here, read the A entry for the enclosure map, operative rules, and status.
