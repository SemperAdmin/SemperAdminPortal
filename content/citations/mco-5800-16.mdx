## Scope

The Marine Corps Manual for Legal Administration consolidates legal administrative procedures into a multi-volume order. Volumes cover claims, complaints, courts-martial, investigations, military justice, and administrative separations.

## Authority

Headquarters Marine Corps Judge Advocate Division publishes and maintains the LSAM. Each volume tracks its own change history under the parent MCO. The volume map below identifies the high-traffic surfaces.

## Volume map

- Volumes 1 to 4. Claims, investigations, freedom of information.
- Volume 7. Article 138 complaints.
- Volume 8. Administrative separations and discharge characterizations.
- Volumes 12 to 14. Military justice and courts-martial procedure.
- Volumes 15 to 17. Specialized legal topics including SAPR, victims rights, and post-trial review.
