## Scope

MARADMIN 191/25 announces the Fiscal Year 2026 Selective Retention Bonus (SRB) Program and the FY26 Broken Service SRB (BSSRB) Program. Marines with an End of Current Contract (ECC) between 2025-10-01 and 2026-09-30 receive program coverage, matching the FY26 Retention Cohort defined in MARADMIN 585/24. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines with an FY26 ECC. Eligible for SRB consideration under the published PMOS tables.
- Career planners. Apply PMOS bonus tables and kicker options. Route reenlistment requests through TFRS.
- HQMC. Owns the bonus tables and per-PMOS guidance.

## Program structure

- FY26 SRB. Bonus amounts set by PMOS, zone, and rank tables published with the message.
- Kicker options. Career planners apply the kicker options listed in the tables.
- FY26 BSSRB. Covers prior service Marines with a break in service between regular component contracts.
- Program framework, zones, obligated service floors, and the career cap sit in MCO 7220.24P.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 7220.24P. Selective Retention Bonus Program. Parent order.
- DODI 1304.31. Enlisted Bonus Program. DoD-level authority cited by the portal's SRB page.
- DOD FMR Volume 7A Chapter 9. Bonus payment framework.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 585/24. FY26 Enlisted Retention Campaign. Same ECC cohort.
- MARADMIN 527/25. Change 1 to the FY26 Enlisted Retention Campaign, released 2025-11-03. Supersedes conflicting guidance in this message, MCO 1040.31, and MARADMIN 585/24.
- MARADMIN 023/26. FY27 SRB Program, released 2026-01-22. Successive year message. Suspends the BSSRB Program for FY27.
- MARADMIN 365/25. FY26 Active Component LATMOV Opportunities. Companion lateral move guidance.
- Total Force Retention System (TFRS). System of record for retention requests.
