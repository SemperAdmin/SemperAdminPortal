## What this form is

Form 2367, Individual Overseas Housing Allowance (OHA) Report. Fillable PDF on file from the 2026-04-29 forms archive snapshot. A Marine requesting OHA, with or without dependents, submits this report. The same form documents Family Separation Housing Allowance (FSH) per the MCAAT military pay checklist.

## Purpose printed on the form

> , SELECT THE APPROPRIATE BOX FOR EACH CATEGORY OF INDIVIDUAL OCCUPYING YOUR RESIDENCE. FOR EACH CATEGORY YOU SELECT, ENTER THE NUMBER REQUESTED IN THE BOX AT RIGHT, THEN RECORD THE TOTAL IN THE BOX AT THE BOTTOM. (NOTE: Do not count dependents unless covered by category c.) a. MYSELF

Quoted from the form's Privacy Act statement.

## Who prepares, signs, and files

MCTFSPRIUM 8-0207 lays out completion of the original and one copy. The member completes parts A and B and signs and dates block 10, adding a statement there when all non-command-sponsored dependents reside in the vicinity of the PDS. The housing officer or appropriate official completes and signs block 11. The commanding officer or designated representative approves the application in block 12. After approval the unit files the original in its correspondence files per SECNAV M-5210.1, retains only the most current form, and returns the duplicate copy to the Marine. Anytime information on the current report changes, the member completes a new report.

## Inspection and reporting standards

The MCAAT military pay checklist holds units to filing the OHA DD Form 2367 in the OMPF within 10 working days from the date reported on unit diary, with a parallel item measured from the effective date. FSH documents on the same form carry the same 10-working-day OMPF standard, and a separate item asks whether FSH is reported within 5 days after the effective date per applicable regulations, citing MCTFSPRIUM 9-90301. MARADMIN 115/24 backs the FSH reporting item.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

OHA reimburses the actual rent paid up to the published ceiling, certified by the member and supported by a lease or rental agreement, as the portal's BAH page covers. MARADMIN 015/20 lists the form among required pay substantiation documents. The portal covers OHA mechanics on the BAH page and the audit items in the MCAAT admin military pay checklist.
