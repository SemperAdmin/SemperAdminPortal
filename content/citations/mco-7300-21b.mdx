## Scope

MCO 7300.21B is the Marine Corps Financial Management Standard Operating Procedure Manual. The order assigns responsibilities and prescribes procedures for financial management across the Marine Corps including disbursing operations, pay administration coordination, audit posture, and financial records management. The order operationalizes DoD-level financial management policy at the Marine Corps level.

## Audience

Comptroller and disbursing officers. S-1 officers and S-1 chiefs coordinating pay actions with disbursing. Marine Corps unit financial management staff. IPAC pay clerks. HQMC Programs and Resources Division (RFB) financial management program managers.

## Content scope

- Financial management organizational responsibilities.
- Disbursing operations procedures.
- Pay administration coordination.
- Audit posture and internal controls.
- Financial records management.
- Reporting requirements.
- Marine Corps-specific implementation of DoDFMR Vol 5 (Disbursing Policy) and DoDFMR Vol 7A (Military Pay Policy).

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- DoDFMR Vol 5. Disbursing Policy.
- DoDFMR Vol 7A. Military Pay Policy.
- MCO 5000.14D. Marine Corps Administrative Procedures.
- MCTFSPRIUM. Personnel reporting instructions for MCTFS pay transactions.

## Status

Active. HQMC Programs and Resources Division (RFB) sponsors the order.
