## What this order is

MCO 7220.56, ENTITLEMENT TO BASIC ALLOWANCE FOR HOUSING (BAH) AT LOCATIONS OTHER. Signed 2015-08-31. Source PDF on file from the 2026-04-28 archive snapshot. The archive copy is the current version, MCO 7220.56A.

## Stated purpose

> The authority delegated from the Secretary of the Navy to the Marine Corps to provide policy and instructions for the administration of the entitlement to BAH at locations other than the PDS is set forth in this Order. Reference (a) governs BAH and addresses circumstances that do not normally require Secretarial Process approval for the payment of BAH.

Quoted from the document's opening section.
