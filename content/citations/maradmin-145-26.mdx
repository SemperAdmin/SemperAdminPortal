## Scope

MARADMIN 145/26 announces the Academic Year 2026-2028 National Intelligence University (NIU) Reserve Component Masters Degree Programs. NIU at Bethesda MD offers accredited masters programs in intelligence-related fields tailored to Reserve Component members. The message opens the application cycle for the AY 2026-2028 programs. Applicability is the Reserve Component.

## Audience

- Reserve Component Marines seeking an NIU masters degree in an intelligence-related field. Eligible to apply.
- HQMC Reserve Affairs (RA). Adjudicates applications.
- Career planners and S-1 staff supporting the Reserve Component. Track applications and IDT status.

## Action items

- Pull NIU program requirements from https://ni-u.edu.
- Submit the application through Reserve Affairs CMT.
- Confirm IDT eligibility for participation.

## Board timeline

MARADMIN 224/26, the CY2026 SNCO selection board schedule, lists the FY26 NIU Reserve Component Masters Degree Programs board with an application window of 2026-11 and a convene date set by this message. The portal's SNCO selection boards page carries the same timeline.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 224/26. CY2026 SNCO Selection Boards. Lists this message as the governing solicitation and sets the 2026-11 application window.
- National Intelligence University. Degree-granting institution at Bethesda MD.

## Status

Signed 2026-03-31. Originator CMC Washington DC MRA RA. Applicability is the Reserve Component. Active for the Academic Year 2026-2028 application cycle.
