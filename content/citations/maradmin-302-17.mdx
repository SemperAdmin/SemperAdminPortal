## Scope

MARADMIN 302/17 announced advance notification of changes to MCO 7220.12R, the Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP) Program. The message gave the force advance notice of a restructuring of SDAP rates and the authorization framework ahead of the formal order revision. Applicability is the Active Component.

## Audience

- Marines on special duty assignment (Recruiter, Drill Instructor, Marine Security Guard, Combat Instructor). Apply current SDAP rates.
- Career planners and disbursing officers. Apply the rate framework.

## Announced changes

- Advance notice of a change to MCO 7220.12R before the formal revision published.
- Restructured SDAP rates and the authorization framework.
- Successive rate messages carry the current tables. MARADMIN 438/19 set FY21 special duty assignment and screenable billet incentives. MARADMIN 628/20 set FY21 SDAP, Assignment Incentive Pay, and Volunteer Supplemental Incentive rates. MARADMIN 181/26 opened the FY28 special duty campaign.
- The portal's pay pages for SDAP, Assignment Incentive Pay, and the Volunteer Supplemental Incentive list this message as a source.

## Connection to higher policy

- MCO 7220.12R. Special Duty Assignment Pay Program. Parent policy the message amends in advance.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MARADMIN 628/20. FY21 SDAP, AIP, and VSI rates. Later rate framework.
- MARADMIN 438/19. FY21 Special Duty Assignments and Screenable Billets Incentives.
- MARADMIN 181/26. FY28 HQMC Special Duty campaign.
