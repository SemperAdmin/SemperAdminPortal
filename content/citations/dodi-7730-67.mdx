## Scope

DODI 7730.67 establishes DoD policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for the Aviation Incentive Pays and Bonus Program. The instruction reissues and cancels the prior 2011 Aviator Incentive Pay and Bonus Program instruction. The instruction also cancels DODI 7730.57 (Aviation Incentive Pay and Continuation Bonus Program). The instruction implements Sections 101, 204, 205, 206, 334, 353, 371, 373, and 374 of Title 37 U.S.C. and DoDFMR 7000.14-R Volume 7A.

## Audience

DoD Component Heads. Service aviation program managers. HQMC Aviation (AVN) Branch. S-1 officers and IPAC pay clerks processing Aviation Incentive Pay (AvIP) and Aviation Bonus (AvB) transactions. Marine Corps aviators eligible for AvIP and AvB.

## Content scope

- Aviation Incentive Pay (AvIP) eligibility and rate framework.
- Aviation Bonus (AvB) contract eligibility and rate framework.
- Operational flying gate requirements for continued AvIP.
- Career aviator pay ladder by years of aviation service.
- Reporting and oversight requirements.

## Operative content

Portal usage evidenced in the repo.

- The Marine-facing Aviation Bonus page, built on DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 20 (December 2025), cites paragraph 2.1.b of this instruction as the ASD M&RA authority to update AvB amounts.
- Per Vol 7A Chapter 20 paragraph 4.3 as cited on the Aviation Bonus page, aviation pays and bonuses paid under this instruction add to other pay and allowances the officer earns, except where the limitations and restrictions in paragraph 3.1.b of this instruction apply.
- The Marine-facing aviation incentive pays page, built on Vol 7A Chapter 22 (March 2024), cites the instruction beside Title 37 U.S.C. sections 334(a), 351(a)(2), and 353. Marine Corps officer AvIP rates sit in Chapter 22 Table 22-10, with current rates published by DTMO.

## Status

Active. Effective 20 October 2016. The instruction is the current DoD-level Aviation Incentive Pays and Bonus authority. Caution. One admin sub-page labels DODI 7730.67 as "Special and Incentive Pay Programs". The official subject is Aviation Incentive Pays and Bonus Program. Use the official subject when citing.
