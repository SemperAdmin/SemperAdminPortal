## Scope

DODI 7730.67 establishes DoD policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for the Aviation Incentive Pays and Bonus Program. The instruction reissues and cancels the prior 2011 Aviator Incentive Pay and Bonus Program instruction. The instruction also cancels DODI 7730.57 (Aviation Incentive Pay and Continuation Bonus Program). The instruction implements Sections 101, 204, 205, 206, 334, 353, 371, 373, and 374 of Title 37 U.S.C. and DoDFMR 7000.14-R Volume 7A.

## Audience

DoD Component Heads. Service aviation program managers. HQMC Aviation (AVN) Branch. S-1 officers and IPAC pay clerks processing Aviation Incentive Pay (AvIP) and Aviation Bonus (AvB) transactions. Marine Corps aviators eligible for AvIP and AvB.

## Content scope

- Aviation Incentive Pay (AvIP) eligibility and rate framework.
- Aviation Bonus (AvB) contract eligibility and rate framework.
- Operational flying gate requirements for continued AvIP.
- Career aviator pay ladder by years of aviation service.
- Reporting and oversight requirements.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 20 (Aviation Bonus). Pay framework.
- DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 22 (Aviation Incentive Pays). Pay framework.
- 37 U.S.C. 334 (Aviation Bonus). Statutory authority.
- 37 U.S.C. 353 (Aviation Career Incentive Pay). Statutory authority.
- Annual Aviation Bonus MARADMINs. Marine Corps-specific contract terms.

## Status

Active. Effective 20 October 2016. The instruction is the current DoD-level Aviation Incentive Pays and Bonus authority.
