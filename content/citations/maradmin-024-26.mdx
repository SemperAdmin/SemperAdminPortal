## Scope

MARADMIN 024/26 announces the Calendar Year 2026 U.S. Marine Corps Officer Non-Statutory Selection Board and Panel schedule. The message sets convening dates for officer slating panels including Service Academy assignments, CMC Fellowships, fellowship programs, and other non-statutory boards. Individual MARADMINs publish solicitation criteria for each board.

## Audience

- Marines eligible for CY26 officer non-statutory boards. File applications by the published deadlines.
- HQMC Manpower Management Officer Assignments (MMOA). Owns the schedule.
- S-1 staff. Track OMPF and MBS posture for board-eligible officers.

## Action items

- Pull the individual solicitation MARADMIN for each board before applying.
- Confirm OMPF accuracy 12 months before the convene date.
- Confirm obligated service requirements through TFRS.

## Schedule entries the portal carries

- AY27 Marine Corps Personnel (Officer and Enlisted) Assigned to Service Academies Slating. Application window 2026-05. Convenes per this message. The CY2026 SNCO board schedule in MARADMIN 224/26 cross-references the panel.
