## Scope

MCO 5214.2G establishes the Marine Corps Information Requirements (Reports) Management Program. The order assigns responsibilities, sets policy, and prescribes procedures for managing recurring and one-time information collections across the Marine Corps. Information collections include forms, requests for information, data calls, surveys, and electronic reporting systems. The order operationalizes SECNAVINST 5210.16 at the Marine Corps level.

## Audience

Every Marine Corps commander, Command Reports Manager, and staff section processing information collections. Adjutants, S-1 chiefs, and AR (Administration and Resource Management) program managers run the daily program. Higher headquarters program managers coordinate Marine Corps-wide reporting requirements.

## Chapter map (high-level)

- Chapter 1. General Information. Purpose, applicability, and responsibilities (paragraph 3a covers the Reports Management Program scope and the 3-year review).
- Chapter 2. Information Requirement Procedures. Case file establishment and life cycle (paragraph 7a Appendix A).
- Chapter 3. Reports Inventory. Inventory of valid and cancelled reporting requirements (paragraph 1).
- Chapter 5. Periodic Review. 3-year review cycle for all existing reports (paragraph 1, paragraph 2d).
- Chapter 6. Public Information Collections. OMB approval rules for collections from 10 or more members of the public (paragraph 1d).
