**70508 – COURTS-MARTIAL/NONJUDICIAL PUNISHMENT STATISTICAL DATA (TTC 212) **

1. Maintaining Courts-martial and Nonjudicial Punishment statistical data. In addition to reporting the sentence of a Marine convicted by a Courts-martial or when a Marine is awarded Nonjudicial Punishment under Article 15, UCMJ, the unit also must report Courts-martial/Nonjudicial Punishment statistical information. The Date of Action is the date the Courts-martial or Nonjudicial Punishment is adjudged. After processing, this information will be posted to the Marine's CM-NJP-STAT-212-REMARK where it will be retained in MCTFS.

NOTE 1:  When reporting the Convening Authority or Commanding Officer EDIPI, do not leave blank nor report zeros.  The CA-CO EDIPI must be on the MCTFS MASTER FILE.

NOTE 2:  When reporting the punitive articles refer to MCTFS Table 18 (PUNITIVE ARTICLES TABLE), a minimum of one article must be reported.  When reporting multiple articles, do not skip any prompts for example ART1 86, ART2 (blank), ART3 111.

NOTE 3:  When reporting the permissible punishments refer to MCTFS Table 19 (PERMISSIBLE PUNISHMENT TABLE), a minimum of one punishment must be reported.  When reporting multiple punishments, do not skip any prompts for example PUN1 (blank), PUN2 (blank), PUN3 N01, PUN4 N07.

NOTE 4: When reporting the punishment code, bytes 1-3 refer to MCTFS Table 19. Byte-1 of the punishment code indicates if the punishment is associated with a 'C' Courts-martial or 'N' Non Judicial Punishment. For byte 4, enter ‘Y’ (yes) if the member was punished or ‘N’ (no) to indicate the punishment was suspended. For example: ‘C04N’ = REDUCTION IN PAY GRADE WAS SUSPENDED (PUNISHMENT ON HOLD), ‘C04Y’ = REDUCTION IN PAY GRADE NOT SUSPENDED (PUNISHMENT STARTED/ENACTED).

NOTE 5:  In order to facilitate the deletion of this sequence report Type Transaction Code 029.  DEL AS ERR/DEL ADD not capable.
