## Scope

SECNAVINST 5210.16 establishes the Department of the Navy Reports Management Program. The instruction sets policy, assigns responsibilities, and provides procedures for managing DON-wide information collections. The instruction implements the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. Chapter 35) and 5 CFR Part 1320 at the Department of the Navy level. The instruction sets the framework MCO 5214.2G implements at the Marine Corps level.

## Audience

Department of the Navy reports program managers. Marine Corps Command Reports Managers. DON staff sections originating information collections. OMB liaisons for public-facing collections.

## Key paragraphs

- Paragraph 5 a-d. Reports Management Program policy framework.
- Paragraph 7d-f. Redundancy review rules requiring use of higher-level forms (NAVMC, DD, SF) when an approved form exists for the information being collected.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- MCO 5214.2G Marine Corps Information Requirements (Reports) Management Program. Marine Corps implementation.
- 5 CFR Part 1320 Controlling Paperwork Burdens on the Public. Federal regulation implementing Paperwork Reduction Act.
- SECNAV M-5210.1. DON Records Management Manual cross-reference.
- 44 U.S.C. Chapter 35. Paperwork Reduction Act statutory authority.
