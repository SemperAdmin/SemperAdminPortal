## Scope

MARADMIN 301/02 published Change 1 to MCO P1070.12K, the Marine Corps Individual Records Administration Manual (IRAM). The change replaced NAVMC 118(12) (SRB Page 12) with NAVMC 10132 (Unit Punishment Book) and modified UPB procedures. The MARADMIN remains historically relevant as the source of the NAVMC 10132 transition under IRAM. Applicability is the Marine Corps Total Force.

## Audience

- S-1 staff and adjutants. Apply the NAVMC 10132 as the Unit Punishment Book.
- Commanding officers. Maintain the UPB under updated IRAM procedures.

## Connection to Marine Corps doctrine

- MCO P1070.12K w/CH 1. Marine Corps Individual Records Administration Manual (IRAM). The order receiving this Change 1.
- NAVMC 10132. Unit Punishment Book. The form replacing NAVMC 118(12).
- MARADMIN 661/19. Manpower Audit Advisory 1-19. Companion compensating control message touching NAVMC 10922.
