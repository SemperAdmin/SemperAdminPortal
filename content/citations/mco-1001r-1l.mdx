## Scope

The Marine Corps Reserve Administrative Management Manual (MCRAMM) governs administration of the Marine Corps Reserve. Chapters cover personnel administration, drill, active duty for training, mobilization, and reserve component pay procedures.

## Audience

I-and-I staff, Reserve Affairs S-1, drill leads at reserve units, and reservists managing their own admin actions. The order is the day-to-day playbook for SMCR unit administration.

## Status

The current change (CH-2) supersedes earlier amendments. Review the change history before relying on a stand-alone chapter. The order remains the active reference for reserve admin until Headquarters publishes a successor.
