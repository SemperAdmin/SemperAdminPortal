## Scope

SECNAV M-5215.1 is the Department of the Navy Directives Issuance System Manual. The manual operationalizes SECNAVINST 5215.1F by providing the detailed format, structure, coordination, and lifecycle procedures for SECNAV and Navy directives. The Marine Corps directives management program (MCO 5215.1K) aligns with SECNAV M-5215.1 for cross-DON consistency.

## Coverage

- Detailed format specifications for SECNAVINST, SECNAVNOTE, OPNAVINST, OPNAVNOTE.
- Subject Standard Identification Code (SSIC) application using SECNAV M-5210.2.
- Coordination, signature, and promulgation procedures.
- Sample formats and templates.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy

- SECNAVINST 5215.1F establishes the policy that SECNAV M-5215.1 operationalizes.
- MCO 5215.1K Marine Corps Directives Management Program parallels SECNAV M-5215.1 inside the Marine Corps.
- SECNAV M-5210.2 Standard Subject Identification Code Manual supplies the SSIC table both manuals reference.
