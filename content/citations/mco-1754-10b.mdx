## Scope

MCO 1754.10B sets the Marine Corps Information, Referral and Relocation (IR&R) Program. The order operationalizes inbound relocation services for Marines and family members across installations, including information, referral, and relocation coordination. The Marine Corps Sponsorship Program (MCO 1320.11H) coordinates with IR&R Program Managers per paragraph 4.b.(9).

## Coverage

- IR&R Program Manager appointment and responsibilities.
- Inbound relocation services for Marines and families.
- Installation-level coordination with the Marine Corps Sponsorship Program.
- Cross-reference to Family Advocacy (MCO 1754.9B) and Marine Corps Community Services (MCO P1700.27B).
- Privacy Act handling per paragraph 5.b.

## Structure

- Paragraph 1: Situation.
- Paragraph 2: Cancellation.
- Paragraph 3: Mission.
- Paragraph 4: Execution (Commander's Intent and Concept of Operations, Subordinate Element Missions).
- Paragraph 5: Administration and Logistics (Recommendations, Privacy Act, Records Management).
- Paragraph 6: Command and Signal.
- Enclosure (1): Information, Referral and Relocation Program Requirements.
- Enclosure (2): Glossary of Terms and Definitions.
- Enclosure (3): Glossary of Acronyms and Abbreviations.

## Connection to Marine Corps policy
