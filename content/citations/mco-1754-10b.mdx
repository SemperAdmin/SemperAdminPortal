## Scope

MCO 1754.10B sets the Marine Corps Information, Referral and Relocation (IR&R) Program. The order operationalizes inbound relocation services for Marines and family members across installations, including information, referral, and relocation coordination. Paragraphs 4-5 and Enclosure (1) carry the execution rules installations follow. Signed 2022-02-02 by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs.

## Audience

- IR&R Installation Program Managers running relocation assistance and resource referral.
- S-1 sponsorship coordinators handing inbound Marines off to the installation IR&R office.
- Commanders who own inbound integration and family readiness outcomes.
- Marines and families arriving at a new installation seeking housing, school, employment, child care, and medical care information.

## Operative content

- Paragraphs 4-5 and Enclosure (1) operationalize the IR&R Program at installations, per the portal's sponsorship IR&R handoff page.
- The Marine Corps Sponsorship Program (MCO 1320.11H) coordinates with IR&R Installation Program Managers per MCO 1320.11H paragraph 4.b.(9). The IR&R Installation Program Manager provides relocation assistance, family advocacy referral, special-needs coordination, and the installation inbound brief covering housing, schools, employment, child care, and medical care.
- IR&R serves as the entry-point program for new arrivals and ongoing information needs under the Unit, Personal and Family Readiness Program umbrella.
- IR&R outputs include the installation handoff record, family advocacy or special-needs referrals, inbound brief attendance, and follow-up actions logged on the unit sponsorship roster.
- Privacy Act handling follows paragraph 5.b. Records management sits in paragraph 5.
