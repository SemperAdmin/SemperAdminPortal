## What this form is

NAVMC 11432, Marine Corps Martial Arts Program Instructor's Course Record, revision 04-2020, electronic fillable (EF). Records completion of the MCMAP instructor course. This form is a martial arts training record, not an administrative processing form. Portal pages previously cited this number in error for the Family Care Plan, which is NAVMC 11800. The NAVMC forms index lists the full title MARINE CORPS MARTIAL ARTS PROGRAM INSTRUCTOR'S COURSE RECORD, two pages, stock number NONSN00005247, digital fillable.

## Audience

MCMAP instructor course students and the Martial Arts Instructors and Martial Arts Instructor Trainers running the course. Unit training sections holding course records. S-1 and reporting clerks entering resulting belt and qualification data into MCTFS.

## Operative content per repo evidence

Repo evidence for this form is limited to the forms index row and the reference-links map. The surrounding program facts on portal pages attach to the MCMAP program, not to this form directly.

- MCO 1500.59A governs MCMAP as a supporting effort to the Marine Corps Physical Fitness Program.
- Units report training data, including MCMAP belt qualifications, accurately within 5 days of the effective date per the MCAAT admin and training checklist.
- MCMAP belt level counts 12.5 percent of the JEPES score inside the Warfighting pillar.
- MCMAP is an IGMC CoRE functional area, FA number 1500.59, sponsored by TECOM (HPB).

## Connections

- MCO 1500.59A. Marine Corps Martial Arts Program order.
- NAVMC 11800. Family Care Plan, the form this number was once mis-cited as.
- MCTFS. System of record for the belt and qualification entries downstream of instructor course completion.
