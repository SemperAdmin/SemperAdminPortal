### Section 60300

INTRODUCTION TO ATTACHMENTS

### Section 1

The following reporting instructions are covered in this section:

a.  ATTACHED PERSONNEL;

b.  ATTACHED TO THE SMCR/MTU;

c.  ATTACHED STATUS, COMMENCEMENT;

d.  ATTACHMENT OF RESERVISTS, COMMENCEMENT;

e.  FLEET ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (FAP);

f.  OCCURRENCES DURING ATTACHED STATUS;

g.  TERMINATION OF ATTACHED STATUS; and

h.  TEMPORARY ADDITIONAL DUTY (TAD).

2. Reporting instructions for applicable transactions are listed in the section.  Diary input authority (Active, Reserve, and Headquarters Marine Corps) and correction indicators (Delete/Add and Delete/Erroneous) for applicable transactions are listed in paragraph 130104 (APPENDIX D - TTC REPORT).
