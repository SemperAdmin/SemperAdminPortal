## Scope

This advisory addresses Authorization Of Digital Signatures On The DD Form 214. Issued by Headquarters Marine Corps Manpower and Reserve Affairs, Manpower Information Branch (MIF) and distributed through the MISSA portal for execution by S-1, IPAC, and commanders across the force.

## Audience

- S-1 desks and IPAC pay clerks executing the actions in this advisory.
- 0102, 0111, and 0170 admin specialists.
- Commanders signing affected actions.

## Operative paragraphs from the source

1. REQUEST ADDRESSEES DISSEMINATE THIS MESSAGE TO SUBORDINATE ELEMENTS.
2. THIS ADVISORY IS TO NOTIFY ADDRESSEES OF THE AUTHORIZATION TO AFFIX DIGITAL SIGNATURES ON THE DD FORM 214. THIS AUTHORIZATION APPLIES TO BOTH THE MEMBER'S SIGNATURE IN BLOCK 21A AND THE OFFICIAL AUTHORIZED TO SIGN BLOCK 22B OF THE DD FORM 214.
3. REF A IS UNDER REVIEW AND THIS AUTHORIZATION WILL BE EXPLICITLY ADDRESSED IN THE NEXT REVISION.
4. UNTIL SYSTEM MODIFICATION CAN BE MADE TO DOCUMENT TRACKING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM, AFFIXING DIGITAL SIGNATURES MAY BE ACCOMPLISHED UTILIZING ADOBE ACROBAT DC (PRO) BY ADDING THE DIGITAL SIGNATURE AND DATE BLOCKS. IT IS NOT MANDATORY AT THIS TIME TO AFFIX DIGITAL SIGNATURE. IT IS RECOMMENDED TO CHANGE THE PROPERTIES OF THE DATE FIELD TO FONT TIMES NEW ROMAN 12 AND CREATE A CUSTOM DATE FORMAT OF YYYYMMDD.
5. THIS PAA HAS BEEN COORDINATED WITH M&RA; (MMSR).
6. EFFECTIVE PAAS ARE LOCATED UNDER THE REFERENCE TAB OF THE MISSA PORTAL LOCATED AT HTTPS:(SLASH)(SLASH)EIS.USMC.MIL/SITES/MISSA/SITEPAGES/PAA.ASPX
