## Day 90 to day 60

- Confirm TAP completion certificate is in your record.
- Review SGLI and TSP elections.
- Schedule final medical and dental.

## Day 60 to day 30

- Submit final DD-214 review.
- Coordinate household goods shipment.
- Out-process administrative sections.

## Day 30 to day 0

- Final pay audit.
- Return all issued gear.
- Receive DD-214 and gold ID card if applicable.
