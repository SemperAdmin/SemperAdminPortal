## Steps

1. Pull current NAVMC 11000<Citation source="NAVMC 11000" /> from the personal record.
2. Update beneficiary, dependent, and notification fields.
3. Sign and date in the proper signature blocks.
4. Submit through the unit S-1 within 5 working days of any change.
5. Confirm the updated form posts in your record after 10 working days.
