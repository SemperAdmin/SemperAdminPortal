## Pre-board records check

- Review fitness reports and any missing periods.
- Confirm awards posted with citations.
- Validate education and training entries.

## Photo and uniforms

- Schedule official photo no later than 60 days before the board.
- Verify uniform measurements per current ALMAR guidance.

## Final brief

- Brief the convening authority of any record discrepancies.
- Document corrective actions in your record.
