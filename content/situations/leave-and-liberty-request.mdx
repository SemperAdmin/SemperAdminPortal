## Submit and route

1. Generate the leave request through the unit system at least 30 days out for ordinary leave.
2. Route through the chain of command per local SOP.
3. Confirm the granting official and transmit the approved copy.

## Track and close

- Monitor the leave clock.
- Submit the chargeable extension before expiration if needed.
- Close out at the end-of-leave check-in within 24 hours of return.
