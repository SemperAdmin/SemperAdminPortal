## Steps

1. Complete EFM screening at the gaining command's family advocacy office.
2. Apply for no-fee passport at the issuing PSD.
3. Schedule pre-departure brief with the OCONUS coordinator.
4. Confirm shipment dates for unaccompanied baggage and household goods.
5. Update Record of Emergency Data with the new APO or FPO.
