## Categories of Training

Section leaders track training across four categories.

- MOS proficiency. Skill progression per the MOS Roadmap.
- Annual mandatory training. PFT, CFT, rifle qual, swim qual, SAPR, INFOSEC.
- Required PME. MarineNet, MCI, distance learning per rank.
- Deployment-specific training. Theater-specific requirements when deployment is on the calendar.

Each category has different cadence and different tracking systems.

## Quarterly MOS Proficiency Audit

For each Marine, pull MOS Roadmap progress.

- Required certifications at the current rank.
- T&R event completions.
- Specific qualifications (instructor codes, NMOS, ASI).
- Gap analysis against the next rank's requirements.

A Marine missing certifications for the current rank is a Marine the section is letting drift.

| Swim qualification | Annual | Combat water survival record |
| SAPR | Annual | TWMS or equivalent |
| INFOSEC | Annual | TWMS or equivalent |
| Suicide prevention | Annual | TWMS |
| Other unit-specific | Per the unit | Unit training tracker |

Section calendar drives scheduling.

## PME and Distance Learning

Per rank requirements.

- L/Cpl through Cpl. Junior MCI courses, MarineNet basics.
- Sgt. NCO PME pre-board.
- SSgt. SNCO course pre-board.
- GySgt and above. Senior PME schools.
- Officer. JPME and resident school cycles.

Track each Marine's PME completion against the next promotion zone. A Marine in the zone with incomplete PME is locked out of promotion.

## Deployment-Specific Training

When deployment lands on the calendar.

- Theater orientation training.
- Cultural awareness.
- Counter-threat training.
- Specific weapons or equipment training.
- Medical training (TCCC, TC3).
- Communications training.

Schedule deployment-specific training as soon as the deployment alert lands. Late scheduling cascades into late deployment-readiness.

## Coordinating With S-3

Unit-level training events run through S-3.

- Confirm training calendar visibility.
- Coordinate Marine attendance against billet coverage.
- Track who attended what, with the post-event documentation.
- Reconcile training records to the section's tracker.

S-3 owns the unit calendar. You own who from the section attends.

## Common Training Tracking Failures

- Section believes annual training is complete because the Marine attended a brief, but record entry never happened.
- MOS Roadmap progress never tracked at the section level.
- PME deferred Marine after Marine, then board season hits with multiple Marines locked out.
- Deployment-specific training scheduled too late, cascades.
- S-3 calendar not visible to the section.
