## The Four Cadences

| Cadence | Trigger | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| Quarterly | Every 90 days | MCTFS audit per Marine |
| Annual | Once per fiscal year | Full OMPF audit per Marine |
| Trigger-based | Specific event | MCTFS and OMPF check on the affected Marine |
| Pre-board deep | 6 months pre-board | Comprehensive audit per board candidate |

Each cadence has a different depth and scope. The four together cover the section.

## The Quarterly MCTFS Audit

Schedule on a fixed quarter mark. Same week each quarter.

- Pull each Marine's MCTFS data.
- Cross-check the standard fields.
- Document discrepancies.
- Route corrections.

Quarterly cadence catches errors before they compound over a year.

## The Annual OMPF Audit

Schedule on a fixed month each year. Common choices: October (start of fiscal year), January (calendar year), or section anniversary.

- Each Marine pulls their own OMPF.
- Section leader cross-checks.
- Discrepancies flagged for correction.

Annual depth catches the entries that quarterly MCTFS does not surface.

## Trigger-Based Audits

Specific events trigger immediate audits regardless of cadence.

- Marine joins the section. Inbound records cleanup within 90 days.
- Marine PCSes out. Outbound cleanup before sign-out.
- Marine returns from deployment. Within 60 days of return.
- Marine in pre-board zone. 6 months out.
- Marine in transition window. Pre-EAS cleanup.
- Life event triggering MCTFS update. Verify the update landed.
- FitRep approved. Verify OMPF entry within 60 days.
- Award approved. Verify OMPF entry within 60 days.

Document the trigger and the audit completion in the leader notebook.

## Pre-Board Deep Audit

For Marines in the promotion zone, a 6-month pre-board audit covers.

- Every FitRep present and current.
- Every award entered.
- Every Page 11 (commendatory and adverse) present.
- PME completion documented.
- Specific qualifications documented.
- Citation language verified.

The deep audit takes hours per Marine. Schedule it.

## The Section Records Calendar

Build a single calendar covering all four cadences.

- Quarterly MCTFS dates.
- Annual OMPF date.
- Standing trigger response process.
- Pre-board deep audit dates per zone Marine.

Print it. Hand it to the company-level leader. Keep it visible.

## Findings Tracking

Maintain a section findings log.

| Marine | Audit Type | Date | Finding | Submitted | Closed |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

Closed findings demonstrate the audit cadence is working. Open findings drive the next routing push.

## When the Cadence Catches Patterns

Repeated findings of the same type across Marines surface a structural issue.

- Multiple Marines missing the same FitRep type. S-1 routing problem.
- Multiple Marines missing the same unit award. Original submission gap.
- Multiple BAH errors in the same housing area. PAC system issue.

Bring patterns to S-1 for systemic fix rather than running individual corrections forever.

## Common Cadence Failures

- Calendar built but never followed.
- Audits run but findings never routed.
- Findings routed but never re-verified.
- Pattern recognition never applied.
- New section leader inherits the calendar but does not refresh.

## Where to Go Next

- MCTFS review for the quarterly audit detail.
- OMPF audit walkthrough for the annual audit detail.
- Awards records cleanup for trigger-based award audits.
