## The 10-Day Window Matters

Per MCO 1751.3<Citation source="MCO 1751.3" />, Marines have 10 working days from a family life event to submit NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> with supporting documents. Per MCO 5000.14D<Citation source="MCO 5000.14D" /> Enclosure 3, you and the commander own the accuracy of your Marines' records.

When the 10-day window slips.

- BAH delayed or unprocessed
- Dependent ID cards delayed
- DEERS enrollment gaps blocking medical care
- TRICARE coverage delays for new dependents
- SGLI beneficiary may not update timely
- $63M dependency audit category exposure

Your section's life event responsiveness is a direct measure of your leadership.

## Five Common Life Events

Coach Marines on these.

### Marriage

Required documents.

- Original or certified copy of marriage certificate
- Spouse SSN
- Spouse photo ID
- Completed NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> with both signatures and witness

Coaching points.

- Get certified marriage cert from issuing county within first week
- Submit NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> within 10 days
- Schedule RAPIDS appointment for spouse ID within 30 days
- Verify BAH rate change on next LES
- Update SGLI beneficiary if changing

### New Baby

Required documents.

- Original or certified copy of birth certificate
- Child SSN application receipt (apply at hospital)
- Completed NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" />

Coaching points.

- Apply for child SSN at the hospital before discharge
- Submit NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> with SSN application receipt if SSN pending
- Schedule RAPIDS for DEERS enrollment when SSN arrives
- Coordinate parental leave with command
- Update SGLI beneficiary if applicable

### Divorce

Required documents.

- Final divorce decree (not the petition)
- Custody order if children involved
- Completed NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" />

Coaching points.

- Submit within 10 days of decree
- Update SGLI beneficiary immediately
- Update RED beneficiary contacts
- Verify BAH rate change appropriately
- Verify former spouse loses ID and DEERS access

### Death of Dependent

Required documents.

- Certified death certificate
- Completed NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" />
- SGLI beneficiary update if dependent was beneficiary

Coaching points.

- Coordinate with command on bereavement leave
- Submit NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> within 10 days
- Update SGLI immediately
- Update RED via S-1
- Coordinate with Casualty Assistance if needed

### Custody Changes

Required documents.

- Court order or modified custody decree
- Prior custody documentation
- Completed NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" />

Coaching points.

- Get certified copy of court order
- Submit NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> within 10 days
- Verify dependent status changes process correctly
- Verify BAH rate adjusts if applicable

## Performance Steps

### Step 1 - Identify Life Events

How you find out.

- Marine tells you directly
- Marine requests leave for the event
- Marine seems distracted or stressed (pay attention)
- Marine asks for paperwork help

When you identify a life event, immediately brief on the 10-day window.

### Step 2 - Brief Marine on Requirements

For each event type.

- Required documents (originals, not photocopies)
- Where to obtain certified copies
- 10-day timeline expectations
- Consequences of late submission

### Step 3 - Verify Supporting Documents

Before Marine submits to S-1.

- Original or certified copy (look for embossed seal or color stamp)
- Documents in member's name (verify spelling matches)
- Documents have proper dates
- All required attachments present

### Step 4 - Verify NAVMC 10922 Completed

Walk the Marine through the form.

- All blocks completed
- Member signature present
- Spouse signature for marriage events
- Witness signature where required

### Step 5 - Route Marine to S-1

Take Marine personally to S-1 if possible.

- Introduces Marine to S-1 admin
- Ensures package is delivered
- Marine sees the routing
- You track from delivery point

### Step 6 - Track to Closure

After S-1 receives.

- 10-day target for PAC processing
- Verify on Marine's next LES
- Verify dependents in DEERS
- Coordinate any follow-up

## Common Section-Level Issues

### Marine Forgets to Tell You

Marine got married 6 months ago, just now mentioning.

- BAH may be wrong this whole time
- Dependent ID never issued
- Late submission still acceptable but explain delay
- Counseling on prompt notification

### Marine Brings Photocopies

Marine doesn't understand certified vs photocopy requirement.

- Marriage certificates require original or certified
- Sending Marine to county for certified copy
- Brief on quality of supporting documents

### Marine Uncertain About SGLI

Member confused about beneficiary changes.

- Brief on SGLI beneficiary considerations
- Coordinate with S-1 for SGLV-8286 update
- Discuss spouse vs child vs parent beneficiary options

### Custody or Divorce Complications

Marine in middle of legal proceedings.

- Wait for final decree before NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" />
- Coordinate with command if questions
- Specific procedures for complex situations

## Coaching Junior Marines

Junior enlisted often have first major life events during early service.

### First Marriage

Most common life event for junior Marines.

- Brief well in advance if Marine engaged
- Coordinate with finance on BAH change
- Brief spouse on military spouse resources
- Connect with Family Readiness

### First Child

Significant life adjustment.

- Brief well in advance during pregnancy
- Coordinate parental leave
- Brief on TRICARE for newborn
- Connect with new parent support

### First Divorce

Difficult situation requiring sensitivity.

- Respect privacy of personal matter
- Focus on documentation requirements
- Connect with chaplain or counseling if needed
- Coordinate with S-1 on financial transitions

## Related Roles

Life event coaching connects through the chain.

- **Marines** submit NAVMC 10922<Citation source="NAVMC 10922" /> within 10 working days per MCO 5000.14D<Citation source="MCO 5000.14D" /> Encl 2.
- **Admin** at PAC processes the package within 10 working days of receipt.
- **Commanders** sign endorsements and review dependency disputes per MARADMIN 661/19<Citation source="MARADMIN 661/19" />.

You drive the timeline. Without your push, Marines often miss the window.
