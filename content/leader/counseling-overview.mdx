## The Four Terms You Owe Marines

Per MCO 1500.61<Citation source="MCO 1500.61" /> paragraph 4.c.(1), four leader actions develop a Marine. They overlap. They are not interchangeable.

- Teaching. You impart knowledge to a Marine without your level of experience. Continuing action.
- Coaching. You encourage and demand output. Forward looking. Daily action.
- Counseling. You deliver feedback on performance. Backward looking with forward implications. Scheduled and event driven.
- Mentoring. You build a voluntary, longer relationship outside the immediate chain when commonality exists.

<Callout variant="info" title="Coaching is not counseling">
Coaching pulls the Marine forward. Counseling reviews what has happened and resets the targets. Mixing the two waters down both. Run them as separate exchanges.
</Callout>

## The Eight Mandatory Occasions

MCO 1500.61<Citation source="MCO 1500.61" /> paragraph 4.c.(2) sets the floor.<Citation source="MCO 1500.61 4.c.(2)" /> You owe a counseling session at each of these triggers:

1. New senior or junior relationship is established.
2. A FitRep is issued.
3. A JEPES evaluation is rendered (the system replaced Pro and Con marks for the junior enlisted tier).
4. Marine becomes eligible for promotion.
5. Marine joins a new unit.
6. Marine executes a PCS.
7. Marine is assigned to Force Preservation.
8. Marine takes a major change in billet responsibility.

The list is the minimum. Stack additional sessions on milestones. Birth of a child, first home purchase, selection to a school, completion of a deployment.

## The Six Functional Areas

Per MCO 1500.61<Citation source="MCO 1500.61" /> paragraph 4.a.(2)(d), six areas frame Marine development.<Citation source="MCO 1500.61 4.a.(2)(d)" /> Touch each at least once during the initial counseling session.

Fidelity, Fighter, Fitness, Family, Finances, Future. The dedicated leaf in this branch builds out the prompts and resources for each.

## What Counseling Is Not

Performance evaluation is past tense. Counseling references the past to redirect future performance. The senior who counsels is not always the senior who writes the FitRep.<Citation source="NAVMC 2795 1004" />

Personal counseling sits inside the conversation, but the program is performance counseling.<Citation source="NAVMC 2795 1004.4" /> Refer matters outside your authority to chaplain, behavioral health, or legal.
