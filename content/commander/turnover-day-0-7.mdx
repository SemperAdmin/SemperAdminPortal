## What Day 0-7 Means

The first seven days are the highest-density action period of your command. The 72-hour CMC notification, the fiscal appointment sweep, and access provisioning all happen in this window. Slipping any of them creates fiscal exposure, system access gaps, or notification deficiencies visible to HQMC.

[ NEW V4.0 ] MARADMIN 165/07<Citation source="MARADMIN 165/07" /> explicit rule: First GO in reporting chain sends message to CMC (MRA MM and PP&O) within 72 hours of CoC. Lists outgoing CO, incoming CO, and next anticipated CoC. Applies any time XO assumes command for any reason.

The "by direction" letter rule traps every new CO who treats their letters as inherited. Predecessor letters lose force at CoC. New letters under your signature are required for "by direction" authority to function.

## System Access Provisioning

This is a multi-system action. Each system has a different point of contact and a different request mechanism. Sequence matters.

| System | Action | Authority |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| MOL / MISSO | Submit trouble ticket to servicing MISSO with Assumption of Command letter and Manager Code (in your BIR). MISSO grants: MOL Commander Role, Report Commander Code, CLA Access, Marine Profile. Access through manpower.usmc.mil. | MISSO; manpower.usmc.mil |
| Command Profile | Submit request to smb.mra.webmaster@usmc.mil. Separate from MOL Commander Role granted by MISSO. Different system, different POC, separate action. | smb.mra.webmaster@usmc.mil; manpower.usmc.mil |
| Manpower Information Portal | Access full app directory at https://www2.manpower.usmc.mil/apps-home/ | MIP: www2.manpower.usmc.mil/apps-home |
| iAPS | S-1 submits request to HQMC MMPB-31 (MMMA mailbox) with outgoing CO name, incoming CO name, Assumption of Command letter, and UIC. Authority does NOT transfer automatically. | HQMC MMPB-31; iAPS Help, manpower.usmc.mil |
| CROSS | Ensure CDRM (E-6/GS-9 or higher) is appointed in writing and updated with incoming CO's name. NAVMC 10030<Citation source="NAVMC 10030" /> due within 90 days - initiate now. | MCBul 5210 (29 Aug 2025); MCO 5210.11F<Citation source="MCO 5210.11F" /> |
| GCSS-MC | Confirm User Role Appointment Letter signed and Supply AIS Administrator has updated permissions. Verify UUAM assignments. | MCO 4400.201<Citation source="MCO 4400.201" /> Vol. 1 |
| DTS | Confirm DTS Authorizing Official appointment via DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" />. Review routing lists and pending de-obligation tasks after assumption. | DoD FMR Vol. 9<Citation source="DoD FMR Vol. 9" />; JTR |
| GTCC | Confirm role as unit Component Program Manager. Review delinquency report and APC appointment. | DoDI 5154.31<Citation source="DoDI 5154.31" /> Vol. 4; IGMC FAC #10 |

The MOL Commander Role and Command Profile are routinely confused. They are different. MISSO grants MOL access. The smb.mra.webmaster mailbox grants Command Profile. Both required, both separate actions.

The OSTC jurisdiction confirmation is the single most important Day 0-7 legal action. Imposing NJP or referring to court-martial on a covered offense without confirming OSTC has not exercised authority creates immediate procedural failure. Verify before any disciplinary action in the first week.

## Day 0-7 Sequence

A workable sequence for the first week:

1. **Day 0.** Read orders, take command, sign DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" /> sweep within hours.
2. **Day 0-1.** First GO notification to CMC initiated by reporting chain. CIRRAS and FPC review of high-risk Marines.
3. **Day 1-2.** MISSO trouble ticket submitted. Command Profile request submitted. iAPS request from S-1 to MMPB-31.
4. **Day 2-3.** Re-issue by-direction delegation letters. Change combinations. Appoint Legal Officer in writing.
5. **Day 3-5.** UPB custody taken. LSSS/T liaison meeting. Pretrial confinement IRO timeline review.
6. **Day 5-7.** GCSS-MC, DTS, and GTCC role confirmation. CROSS CDRM appointment confirmed. Initiate NAVMC 10030<Citation source="NAVMC 10030" />.

This sequence is illustrative. Local conditions and case loads adjust the order. The constraint is the 72-hour CMC notification and the requirement that DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" /> sweep occur essentially at assumption.

## Common Failures on Day 0-7

1. **DD Form 577<Citation source="DD Form 577" /> sweep deferred past Day 1.** The longer the delay, the longer fiscal authority is ambiguous. Sign within hours of assumption.
2. **iAPS treated as automatic.** S-1 forgets that authority does not transfer. Awards stall in the system. Submit the MMPB-31 request immediately.
3. **OSTC jurisdiction not confirmed before first NJP.** Incoming CO acts on a case the LSSS/T flag would have stopped. Confirm before action.
4. **By-direction letters carried over.** Staff continues using predecessor delegations. The first time someone signs "by direction" without a current letter, the action is voidable.
5. **Combinations not changed.** Predecessor or departed custodian retained access to a security container. SECNAV M-5510.36<Citation source="SECNAV M-5510.36" /> violation.
