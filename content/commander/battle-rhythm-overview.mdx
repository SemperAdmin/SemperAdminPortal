## How to Use This Page

This page is the lookup index for recurring command obligations. Three layers: monthly, quarterly, annual. Each row points to the deeper page with the procedure, the authority, and the common failures.

Bookmark this page. Most CO portal traffic at month 6 of command is on this page.

## Non-Delegable Items

Four obligations under MCO 1752.5C and MCO 1500.60A are explicitly non-delegable. Track them on your personal calendar, not the unit calendar.

| Obligation | Cadence | Authority |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| CMG attendance | Monthly | MCO 1752.5C, DoDI 6495.02 |
| 72-hour victim case-status update after each CMG | Monthly | MCO 1752.5C |
| FPC chair | Monthly | MCO 1500.60A Para 4.b.(3)(b) |
| MfR when CO unavailable for FPC, retained 3 years | As needed | MCO 1500.60A Para 4.b.(3)(l) |

XO substitution on these items is not authorized. The MCO treats habitual XO chairing as a finding.

## Event-Driven Items

Some obligations are triggered by events, not by the calendar. Documented here because COs habitually forget them.

| Trigger | Action | Authority |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| XO assumes command for any reason | First GO in reporting chain notifies CMC within 72 hours | MARADMIN 165/07 |
| Marine joins unit with LOW risk in MCTFS | Auto-assign ELEVATED for 90 days | MCO 1500.60A Para 4.b.(3)(g)(3) |
| DoDI 6490.08 notification received from provider | Update CIRRAS, brief next FPC | DoDI 6490.08, MCO 1500.60A |
| SAPR Unrestricted Report comes in | Open SAPR Unrestricted Report playbook | MCO 1752.5C |

| Marine receives NJP | Page 11 entry directed, UPB processed | MCO P1070.12, MCO 5800.16 Vol. 14 |

## Calendar Hooks Worth Building In

The Suicide Prevention guide and Mental Readiness Playbook V3 identify periods of elevated risk. Build awareness into the calendar.

- **Pre-deployment work-up:** Bi-weekly FPC recommended.
- **Post-deployment 90 days:** Sustained elevated leadership attention to reintegration.
- **Holiday season (Thanksgiving to New Year):** Historically elevated risk. Increase touchpoint frequency.
- **Tax season (Jan to Apr):** Financial stressors peak.
- **Promotion announcements:** Failure-to-promote is a documented critical stressor.
- **End-of-fiscal-year drawdown:** Fiscal-law training currency, FLIPL closures, budget-execution status.
- **PCS season:** Elevated transition risk for joiners and leavers. Sponsor program discipline tightens.

## What Goes Wrong in Battle Rhythm

The most-missed items, ranked by frequency.

1. **72-hour victim case-status update.** Easy to forget. Non-delegable. The MCO penalty is the highest among recurring items.
2. **Habitual XO chair on FPC.** No MfR in the file. Pattern visible across multiple months.
3. **Quarterly CMR reconciliations slip past quarter-end.** Treated as a flexible deadline.
4. **GTCC delinquency report unread.** APC handles it. CO never reviews. IGMC FAC #10 finding waiting.
5. **DEOCS started in November.** 31 October begin date missed. Annual FITREP annotation suffers.
6. **Non-core CMT still annual.** MARADMIN 199/26 changed to biennial. Unit calendar not updated.
7. **MfR for unavailable FPC not retained 3 years.** Filed and lost. The MCO requires the 3-year retention period.
8. **Capstone changes not pushed to ARD Records via ETMS2.** Quarterly tasker missed. CROSS records out of sync.
