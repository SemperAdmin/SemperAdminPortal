## Role and responsibility

Installation Marine and Family Services (MF) coordinates with the Victim and Witness Assistance Program per MCO 5800.16<Citation source="MCO 5800.16" /> Chapter 4 paragraph 040907. MF establishes a process to inform crime victims and witnesses about VWAP rights, services, and assistance when they receive counseling, treatment, or advice per paragraph 040907(A). MF ensures victims and witnesses understand the rights afforded under the law and receive a completed DD Form 2701 if not already provided per paragraph 040907(B). MF appoints a representative to the local Victim and Witness Assistance Council per paragraph 040907(C). The Installation Victim Witness Liaison Officer (IVWLO) chairs the Council and coordinates the installation VWAP directory per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(6).

Source documents flow from the MF counseling encounter, the victim or witness contact record, the DD Form 2701 already provided by law enforcement or not provided, the installation VWAP directory, the MF resource referral record, the MF Council representative appointment letter, and the MF training record. Outputs include the established information process, the DD Form 2701 distributed at counseling, the resource referral record, the appointed MF Council representative, the Council meeting participation, and the audit trail.

Per paragraph 040907(A), MF establishes a process informing crime victims and witnesses about VWAP rights when receiving counseling, treatment, or advice. Per paragraph 040907(B), MF ensures victims and witnesses understand the rights afforded under the law and provides a completed DD Form 2701 if not previously issued. Per paragraph 040907(C), MF appoints by name and in writing a representative to the local Victim and Witness Assistance Council.

## Trigger events

- New crime victim or witness arrives at MF for counseling, treatment, or advice.
- DD Form 2701 not previously provided requires MF issuance.
- Quarterly installation Council meeting cycle requires MF representative participation.
- Annual VWAP training cycle for MF personnel.
- New MF supervisor or program lead triggers Council representative refresh.
- Installation VWAP directory update cycle.
- IGMC VWAP FA inspection covers Marine and Family Services (Section 07.0).

## Processing workflow

1. Appoint the MF Council representative. Per MCO 5800.16<Citation source="MCO 5800.16" /> Chapter 4 paragraph 040907(C), MF appoints by name and in writing a representative to the local Victim and Witness Assistance Council.
2. Establish the information process. Per paragraph 040907(A), MF establishes a process to inform crime victims and witnesses about VWAP rights, services, and assistance when they receive counseling, treatment, or advice.
3. Ensure understanding of rights. Per paragraph 040907(B), MF ensures victims and witnesses in a case understand the rights afforded under the law.
4. Provide DD Form 2701. Per paragraph 040907(B), MF provides a completed DD Form 2701 if not previously provided by law enforcement.
5. Coordinate with the installation VWAP directory. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(6), the installation VWAP directory carries the MF resource list. MF coordinates content with the IVWLO.
6. Refer victims to resource and compensation programs. MF refers victims to military and civilian programs, services, and crime victim compensation funds available per the installation VWAP directory.
7. Participate in the installation Council. Per paragraph 040907(C), the MF representative participates in the local Victim and Witness Assistance Council chaired by the IVWLO.
8. Train MF personnel. MF personnel complete training on VWAP rights and DD Form 2701 distribution. The MF supervisor maintains training records.
9. Maintain records of victim resource referrals. MF documents victim referrals to community resources and compensation programs.

## Systems of record and forms

### Systems

- MF Counseling Records System. Holds the victim and witness counseling encounters.
- Installation VWAP Directory. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(6).
- Victim and Witness Assistance Council Records. Holds MF representative participation.

### Forms

- DD Form 2701 Initial Information for Victims and Witnesses. Per paragraph 040907(B).
- MF Council Representative Appointment Letter. Per paragraph 040907(C).
- MF VWAP Information Process Document. Per paragraph 040907(A).
- Resource Referral Record. Local format.

## Common pitfalls

- MF Council representative appointment missing. Per paragraph 040907(C), the representative appoints by name and in writing.
- MF information process not established. Per paragraph 040907(A), the process is required for counseling, treatment, or advice encounters.
- DD Form 2701 not provided when not previously issued. Per paragraph 040907(B), MF provides if not already provided by law enforcement.
- Rights understanding skipped. Per paragraph 040907(B), MF ensures victims and witnesses understand the rights.
- Installation VWAP directory missing MF resources. The directory carries the MF resource list per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(6).
- MF Council participation missed. Per paragraph 040907(C), the appointed representative participates.

## Decision points

- DD Form 2701 issuance versus reference. Per paragraph 040907(B), if law enforcement has already provided the form, MF references the prior issuance. Otherwise, MF issues.
- MF representative seniority. The MF supervisor selects a representative with appropriate experience and authority for Council participation.
- Resource referral selection. MF selects from the installation VWAP directory and from MF program offerings (Family Advocacy, Counseling Services, Sexual Assault Prevention and Response, etc.).
