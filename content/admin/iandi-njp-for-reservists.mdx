## Reserve NJP Overview

Per MCRAMM Chapter 11, NJP for Reserve members follows UCMJ Article 15 procedures with specific Reserve considerations. Commanders impose NJP for minor offenses during qualifying duty status.

This page focuses on Reserve-specific aspects. The general NJP procedure is covered in the Commander tree at /commander/discipline-and-records/njp-routing.
