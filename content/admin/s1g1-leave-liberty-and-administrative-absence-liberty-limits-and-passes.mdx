## Role and responsibility

The commander sets unit liberty regulations and liberty limits. The S-1 chief publishes the unit liberty SOP. The S-1 clerk processes liberty pass issuance for travel outside liberty limits. The chain of command authorizes special liberty per local policy. The S-1 officer signs by direction on liberty restriction Page 11 entries when delegated.

Source documents flow from MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 regulations, unit liberty SOP, and the unit holiday schedule. Liberty actions land as liberty pass records and Page 11 entries where command-imposed restrictions apply. Outputs include the unit liberty SOP, the liberty pass file, the holiday schedule, and the absence over liberty case records.

Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 1, liberty is authorized absence from a duty station for short periods of relaxation. Liberty is not chargeable to leave. Per paragraph 2, liberty limits apply. Per paragraph 5, absence over liberty constitutes a UA condition under specific circumstances.

## Trigger events

- Marine requests liberty pass for travel outside liberty limits per paragraph 3.
- Public holiday opens per the command holiday schedule per paragraph 4.
- Special liberty granted as recognition or for specific event.
- Marine returns past liberty expiration triggering absence over liberty review per paragraph 5.
- Command issues liberty restriction order requiring Page 11 documentation.
- New command policy changes liberty limits or pass procedures.
- Inbound brief includes liberty policy briefing.

## Processing workflow

1. Publish unit liberty regulations. The S-1 chief drafts the unit liberty SOP per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraphs 1 through 5 and command guidance. The commander signs.
2. Issue liberty passes. For travel outside the liberty limit per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 3, issue a liberty pass with destination, duration, and contact information.
3. Track public holiday liberty. Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 4, public holidays open per the command holiday schedule.
4. Process special liberty requests. Per command policy, route special liberty for recognition or specific events.
5. Coordinate liberty restrictions. Where the chain imposes restriction (50-mile liberty, installation liberty, base liberty), document per MCO P1070.12K<Citation source="MCO P1070.12K" /> Chapter 6 Page 11 entry.
6. Process absence over liberty. Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 5, absence over liberty becomes UA at the threshold defined by the rules. Route to UA processing.
7. Brief Marines at inbound. Include liberty limits, pass procedures, and holiday schedule in the inbound brief.
8. Update liberty policy. On command direction change or installation change, refresh the unit liberty SOP.
9. Audit liberty pass file. Run a fixed-cycle audit on issued passes.
10. Document restrictions in MCTFS. Where command-imposed restriction affects pay or duty status, cut the appropriate transaction.

## Systems of record and forms

### Systems

- Unit liberty pass file. Local register of issued liberty passes.
- MCTFS. Holds command-imposed restriction transactions where applicable.
- MOL. Surfaces liberty policy and pass procedures to the Marine.
- OMPF Field Folder. Holds Page 11 entries documenting restrictions.

### Forms

- Liberty Pass. Local format documenting destination, duration, and contact.
- Unit Liberty SOP. Local SOP per MCO 1050.3J Chapter 3.
- NAVMC 118 (11) Administrative Remarks. Page 11 documenting liberty restrictions.
- Unit Holiday Schedule. Per command policy and paragraph 4.

## Common pitfalls

- Liberty pass issued without destination or contact information. The pass cannot support accountability check. Capture full destination and contact at issuance.
- Liberty limit policy not briefed at inbound. The Marine departs without knowing limits. Include in the inbound brief.
- Public holiday liberty extended without commander approval. Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 4, holiday liberty runs per the schedule. Extensions need authorization.
- Absence over liberty handled informally. Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 5, absence over liberty triggers UA procedures at the defined threshold.
- Liberty restriction Page 11 omitted. Per MCO P1070.12K<Citation source="MCO P1070.12K" /> Chapter 6, command-imposed restrictions document in Page 11.

## Decision points

- Liberty pass requirement. The Marine travels outside the liberty limit. Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 3, a liberty pass is issued. The S-1 clerk runs issuance.
- Restriction scope. The chain imposes 50-mile liberty versus installation liberty versus base liberty. The commander calls and the S-1 officer documents.
- Absence over liberty threshold to UA. Per MCO 1050.3J<Citation source="MCO 1050.3J" /> Chapter 3 paragraph 5, the threshold drives UA routing. The S-1 chief routes per the threshold.
