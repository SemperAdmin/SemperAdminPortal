## Role and responsibility

The IPAC outbound counselor runs the outbound process per MCO 1300.8<Citation source="MCO 1300.8" /> covering PCS, separation, and retirement outbound actions. The S-1 chief tracks the outbound Marine through check-out, the Outbound Interview, and the records handover. The S-1 officer signs by direction on the MCTFS detach, the OMPF Field Folder routing, and the Page 11 documentation. The unit commander signs separation and retirement orders per MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" />. The disbursing technician runs the final pay closeout and the debt clearance. The GTCC APC and the DTS ODTA coordinate account closure on separation per MCO 4600.40C<Citation source="MCO 4600.40C" /> and MCO 4650.39A<Citation source="MCO 4650.39A" />.

Source documents flow from the PCS orders, the separation order per MCO 1900.16, the retirement order per MCO 1900.16 Chapter 2 or 7, the unit check-out sheet, the Outbound Interview record, the final pay closeout package, the OMPF Field Folder, and the GTCC/DTS account status. Outbound outputs include the executed MCTFS detach with KSD upload, the cleared EDFR after detach, the routed OMPF Field Folder to the gaining unit, the DD Form 214 on separation per MCO 1900.16 Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraphs 1200-1205, the final LES, the cleared debt, the closed GTCC and DTS accounts on separation, the Page 11 per MCO P1070.12K<Citation source="MCO P1070.12K" /> Chapter 6, and the audit trail.

Per MCO 1300.8 and MCTFSPRIUM<Citation source="MCTFSPRIUM" />, outbound check-out runs through the unit S-1 and the IPAC. Per MCO 1900.16 Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraphs 1200-1205, the DD Form 214 prepares on separation. Per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 3, the OMPF Field Folder routes to the gaining unit on PCS or to HQMC OMPF on separation.

## Sub-pages in this category

- Final Pay Closeout and Debt Clearance. Disbursing coordination for the final LES, debt clearance per DoDFMR Vol 7A, and out-of-service debt processing per MCO 4650.39A Chapter 9 for travel debt.
- MCTFS Detach and Routing. The UD-MIPS detach transaction, the KSD upload per MCTFSPRIUM, the diary certification, and the EDFR clearance.
- OMPF and SRB Handover. The OMPF Field Folder routing to the gaining unit per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 3 on PCS or to HQMC OMPF on separation.
- Outbound Interview and Counseling. The Outbound Interview process for PCS and separation, the career counselor handoff, and the retention or transition counseling.
- Separation and Retirement Handoff. Coordination with the separation-retirement category for separation processing, DD Form 214, transition benefits, and OMPF closeout per MCO 1900.16.
- Unit Check-Out Procedures. The unit check-out sheet, the staff section routing, the gear and clothing return, and the chain of command farewell.

## Trigger events

- PCS orders cut on outbound Marine.
- Separation orders cut per MCO 1900.16.
- Retirement orders cut per MCO 1900.16 Chapter 2 or 7.
- Marine 90 days from EAS triggering separation processing.
- Outbound Interview scheduled.
- Final pay closeout window opens.
- GTCC and DTS account closure on separation.
- OMPF Field Folder routing on detach.

## Processing workflow

1. Receive outbound orders. The IPAC outbound counselor receives PCS orders, separation order, or retirement order.
2. Run the unit check-out. Per MCO 1300.8<Citation source="MCO 1300.8" />, the Marine routes through staff sections (S-1, S-2, S-3, S-4, Medical, Legal, Career Planner) and signs off completion.
3. Conduct the Outbound Interview. The Outbound Interview covers career counseling, transition counseling on separation, retention counseling on continued service, and unit feedback.
4. Coordinate disbursing for final pay closeout and debt clearance. The disbursing technician runs the final LES audit, processes Advance Pay repayment, clears debt, and processes the Travel and Transportation Reform Act of 1998 actions per DoDFMR Vol 7A Chapter 32.
5. Cut the MCTFS detach. Per MCTFSPRIUM<Citation source="MCTFSPRIUM" />, the IPAC cuts the detach transaction through UD-MIPS with the KSD upload.
6. Route the OMPF Field Folder. Per MCO P1070.12K<Citation source="MCO P1070.12K" /> Chapter 3, the folder routes to the gaining unit on PCS or to HQMC OMPF on separation.
7. Process DD Form 214 on separation or retirement. Per MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" /> Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraphs 1200-1205 and Appendix B, the IPAC prepares the DD Form 214 in MOL DTMS.
8. Close GTCC and DTS accounts. Per MCO 4600.40C<Citation source="MCO 4600.40C" /> paragraph 041106 and MCO 4650.39A<Citation source="MCO 4650.39A" /> paragraph 4.b.(6)(d), close on separation.
9. Cut the Page 11. Per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 6, cut a Page 11 documenting the outbound action.
10. Brief the outbound Marine. The S-1 briefs the Marine on records access (DPRIS, milConnect, NPRC) and OMPF retrieval after separation.

## Systems of record and forms

### Systems

- Marine Corps Total Force System (MCTFS). Holds detach transaction and final pay record.
- UD-MIPS. Cuts detach transactions per Appendix E.
- OMPF Field Folder. Routes per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 3.
- MOL Document Tracking and Management System (DTMS). Prepares DD Form 214 per MCO 1900.16.
- DFAS. Processes final pay and debt.
- GTCC vendor portal. Per MCO 4600.40C for account closure.
- DTS profile. Per MCO 4650.39A for profile closure.
- MOL. Surfaces orders and LES.

### Forms

- Unit Check-Out Sheet. Local format documenting staff section sign-offs.
- Outbound Interview Record. Local format documenting the interview.
- KSD Packet. Per MCTFSPRIUM codes for detach.
- DD Form 214 / 214WS / 214C / 215. Certificate of Release or Discharge per MCO 1900.16 Appendix B.
- Final Pay Closeout Package. Per DoDFMR Vol 7A.
- OMPF Field Folder Routing Memorandum. Per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 3.
- NAVMC 118 (11) Administrative Remarks. Page 11 per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 6.

## Common pitfalls

- MCTFS detach cut without KSD upload. Per MCTFSPRIUM<Citation source="MCTFSPRIUM" />, every detach carries the KSD packet.
- Final pay closeout skipped resulting in indebtedness. Per DoDFMR Vol 7A, the closeout clears advances and allowances.
- GTCC account left open on separation. Per MCO 4600.40C<Citation source="MCO 4600.40C" /> paragraph 041106, the APC closes the account.
- DTS profile not closed on separation. Per MCO 4650.39A<Citation source="MCO 4650.39A" /> paragraph 4.b.(6)(d), the ODTA closes the profile after debt clearance.
- OMPF Field Folder not routed. Per MCO P1070.12K<Citation source="MCO P1070.12K" /> Chapter 3, the folder routes to the gaining unit or HQMC OMPF.
- DD Form 214 not prepared on separation. Per MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" /> Chapter 1 Section 2 paragraphs 1200-1205, the DD Form 214 prepares in MOL DTMS.
- Outbound Interview skipped. The interview supports career counseling and transition.
- Page 11 documenting outbound omitted. Per MCO P1070.12K Chapter 6, every action records.

## Decision points

- PCS versus separation versus retirement. The order type drives the path. PCS routes to MCO 1300.8 check-out. Separation routes to MCO 1900.16 Chapter 6 (enlisted) or Chapter 4 (officer). Retirement routes to MCO 1900.16 Chapter 7 (enlisted) or Chapter 2 (officer).
- OMPF Field Folder destination. PCS routes the folder to the gaining unit. Separation routes the folder to HQMC OMPF for the master record.
- Final pay timing. The final pay processes on detach for PCS or on separation date for separation.
- GTCC and DTS account closure timing. Per MCO 4600.40C and MCO 4650.39A, closures run after final debt clearance.

## Authority

This category sits under MCO 1300.8<Citation source="MCO 1300.8" /> and MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" /> for PCS and separation/retirement actions. MCO P1070.12K<Citation source="MCO P1070.12K" /> Chapter 3 (OMPF Field Folder) and Chapter 6 (Page 11) cover records. MCO 4600.40C<Citation source="MCO 4600.40C" /> and MCO 4650.39A<Citation source="MCO 4650.39A" /> cover GTCC and DTS closure. MCTFSPRIUM<Citation source="MCTFSPRIUM" /> covers MCTFS detach. DoDFMR Vol 7A covers final pay. The processing workflow maps to NAVMC 3500.3E T-R event 0170-PERA-2105.
