## What SDAP Is

Per 37 USC 307<Citation source="37 USC 307" /> and MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" />, Special Duty Assignment Pay (SDAP) compensates Marines in extremely demanding special duty assignments. The pay recognizes the additional rigor, time commitment, and responsibility of these assignments beyond standard duty.

SDAP is significant pay. It is also audit-critical because the assignment's specific duration drives precise eligibility windows.

## SDA Categories

The Marine Corps designates specific assignments for SDAP. Common categories.

### Drill Instructor (DI) Duty

Drill Instructors at Marine Corps Recruit Depots (Parris Island and San Diego).

- Highest SDAP level (Level 6)
- Substantial monthly amount
- Specific qualification requirements (Drill Instructor School graduation)
- 3-year typical tour

### Recruiter Duty

Marine Corps Recruiters in field recruiting stations.

- High SDAP level (Level 5 or higher depending on assignment)
- Significant monthly amount
- Recruiter School graduation required
- Specific recruiting station assignment

### Marine Security Guard (MSG) Duty

Marines guarding U.S. embassies worldwide.

- High SDAP level
- Embassy duty requirements
- MSG School graduation required

### Marine Corps Combat Instructor

Combat instructors at School of Infantry and Combat Marksmanship Schools.

- SDAP level varies
- Specific instructor qualification

### Other Designated Special Duties

- Specific staff positions
- Specific high-demand billets
- Per current MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" /> designations

## SDAP Levels

Per MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" />, SDAP has specific rate levels.

| Level | Approximate Monthly Rate | Typical Assignments |
|-------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Level 1 | Lowest tier | Specific designated billets |
| Level 2 | Lower tier | Specific designated billets |
| Level 3 | Mid tier | Specific designated billets |
| Level 4 | Mid-high tier | Some recruiter assignments |
| Level 5 | Higher tier | Most recruiter assignments |
| Level 6 | Highest tier | DI, MSG, and similar high-demand |

Specific rates set in DOD FMR with annual adjustments. Always reference current rates.

## Eligibility Requirements

Per MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" />, eligibility requires.

- Active duty status
- Assigned to designated SDAP billet
- Qualified for the specific duty (school graduation, screening, etc.)
- Specific service requirements per duty type
- Member's record qualifies for the specific duty

## Performance Steps for SDAP Processing

### Step 1 - Verify Assignment

Pull member's orders.

- Verify assignment to designated SDAP billet
- Verify billet identification code matches MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" /> designation
- Verify reporting date

### Step 2 - Verify Qualification

Confirm member meets duty-specific qualifications.

- DI: Drill Instructor School graduation
- Recruiter: Recruiter School graduation
- MSG: Marine Security Guard School graduation
- Other: Specific qualification per current MCO

### Step 3 - Determine SDAP Level

Per the specific assignment.

- Identify SDAP level from MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" /> listings
- Apply current rate from DOD FMR

### Step 4 - Process UD/MIPS Transaction

Apply SDAP via UD/MIPS.

- Specific SDAP transaction code per MCTFSCODESMAN
- Effective date of assignment (typically date member reports to billet)
- Level identifier in transaction

### Step 5 - Verify EDFR

Confirm processing within 5 business days.

- SDAP posts at correct level
- Member sees SDAP on next LES

### Step 6 - Track Assignment End

For each SDAP recipient.

- Note typical tour length
- Track approaching tour end
- Process SDAP stop at member's transfer or end of tour

## When SDAP Stops

SDAP stops when.

- Member transfers from SDA billet
- Member is removed from SDA duty (disciplinary, training failure)
- Member's tour ends
- Member separates or retires

The end of duty is precise. SDAP must stop on the actual transfer date, not later.

## Common SDAP Errors

These hit during PAC processing.

1. **SDAP continues after transfer.** Member transferred from SDA billet but SDAP not stopped. Critical overpayment. Stop retroactive to transfer date.
2. **Wrong SDAP level applied.** Recruiter at Level 4 instead of Level 5. Update to correct level.
3. **SDAP not started at assignment.** Member reported to DI billet but SDAP never started. Process retroactive start.
4. **Member removed from duty but SDAP continues.** Member removed from DI duty for disciplinary reasons but SDAP continues. Stop retroactive to removal.
5. **Multiple SDAP rates simultaneously.** Some rates may not stack. Verify per MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" />.

## Pay Stacking

SDAP generally pays in addition to other allowances and special pays.

- BAH applies independently
- BAS applies independently
- HDIP applies if member also qualified
- HFP/IDP applies if combat zone
- Each pay is separate

## Audit Implications

SDAP is audit-significant given the amounts involved.

- DI overpayment of SDAP creates significant audit findings
- Wrong level over time creates compounding error
- Failure to stop SDAP at transfer creates major overpayment
- Member without qualification drawing SDAP creates findings

PAC monthly verification of SDAP supports unit audit posture.

## Documentation Required

For SDAP actions, PAC needs.

- Assignment orders to SDA billet
- School completion documentation
- Annual screening (where applicable)
- Tour end documentation

## Quality Control

Before submitting SDAP transactions.

- Verify assignment matches SDA designation
- Verify level per current MCO 7220.12<Citation source="MCO 7220.12" />
- Verify rate per current DOD FMR
- Verify effective date matches assignment

## Coordination Touchpoints

SDAP processing touches.

- **MCRD (DI duty)**: For drill instructor assignments
- **Marine Corps Recruiting Command**: For recruiter assignments
- **MSG Battalion**: For embassy duty
- **HQMC Manpower**: For policy and assignment questions

## Tour End Coordination

When SDA tour ends.

- Member transfers to follow-on assignment
- SDAP stops at transfer date
- New assignment may have different SDAP level
- Or may have no SDAP

PAC coordinates timely processing to prevent overpayment.

## Related Roles

SDAP affects Marines in special duty assignments.

- **Marines** in SDA billets verify SDAP on LES and report transitions.
- **Leaders** at SDA units track member tour completion and qualification.
- **Commanders** ensure unit SDAP processing accurate during transitions.
