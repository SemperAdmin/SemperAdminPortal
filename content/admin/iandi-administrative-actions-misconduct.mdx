## Administrative Actions Overview

Per MCRAMM Chapter 11, administrative actions provide non-judicial responses to Reserve member misconduct. Used when.

- UCMJ does not apply (offense outside duty status)
- UCMJ applies but administrative action more appropriate
- Documenting misconduct without formal proceedings
- Specific other situations

## Administrative Separation for Reserve

Per MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" /> and MCRAMM Chapter 11.

### When Administrative Separation

For specific severe situations.

- Serious misconduct without court-martial
- Pattern of misconduct
- Specific qualifying conditions

### Administrative Separation Process

Per MCO 1900.16<Citation source="MCO 1900.16" /> as amended by MARADMIN 267/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 267/26" />.

- Notification per paragraph 6303.3 with the DODI 1332.14<Citation source="DODI 1332.14" /> paragraph 5.11 right-to-statement language
- Member's rights, including the SA unrestricted-report GO/FO review right inside the one-year window
- Commander decision
- Separation Authority decision

<Callout variant="warning" title="MARADMIN 267/26 notification language, effective 9 Jun 2026">
  Every involuntary admin separation notification packet must include the new paragraph 5.11 language. MARADMIN 267/26 amends paragraph 6303.3.a, plus Figures 6-2, 6-2A, 6-3, and 6-3A of MCO 1900.16. If the respondent made an unrestricted report of sexual assault and is recommended for involuntary separation within one year of final disposition of the SA case, the respondent may submit a written request to the first General Officer or Flag Officer in the Separation Authority's chain of command to review the circumstances of and grounds for the involuntary separation before the Separation Authority approves the final separation action. Timely submission halts separation until the GO or FO concurs with the grounds. Missing the deadline routes appellate consideration to BCNR. Notification packets that omit the new language create a BCNR remand risk and an IGMC 1900.16 inspection finding.<Citation source="MARADMIN 267/26" />
</Callout>
