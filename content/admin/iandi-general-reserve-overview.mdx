## What Chapter 5 Covers

Per MCRAMM Chapter 5, general reserve information addresses topics common across reserve components. The chapter provides foundational information for I&I admin operations.

Topics include.

- Identification card programs
- Travel reimbursement
- Allowances and special pays administration
- Authorities and approvals
- Communications and reporting
- Administrative procedures

## Key Topics in Chapter 5

### Identification Cards

Reserve members and dependents receive specific identification cards based on status. Different cards for.

- Drilling SMCR members and dependents
- IRR members and dependents
- Standby Reserve members
- Retired Reserve members
- Active Reserve members

ID card processing covered separately.

### Travel Reimbursement

Reserve members traveling to drill or AT receive travel reimbursement under specific conditions.

- IDT travel reimbursement programs
- Travel reimbursement covered separately

### Allowances and Special Pays

Reserve members are eligible for many of the same allowances as active duty under specific conditions.

- BAH during active duty status
- BAS during active duty status
- Special pays during qualifying duty
- Drill pay for inactive duty

Specific allowances covered in pay sections.

### Authorities and Approvals

Reserve administration involves specific authorities for various actions.

- Convening authorities for various proceedings
- Approval authorities for various actions
- Specific delegated authorities

Covered separately.

### Communications and Reporting

Specific reporting requirements for reserve administration.

- Periodic reports
- Statistical reports
- Specific data submissions

Covered separately.

## Why I&I Admin Cares About Chapter 5

Chapter 5 provides operational foundation for daily I&I work.

- Identification questions arise daily
- Travel reimbursement supports member retention
- Allowances and pays touch every member
- Authorities determine who can do what
- Reporting drives unit and command operations

Knowing Chapter 5 makes I&I admin operations efficient.
