## Why Career Counseling Matters

Per MCRAMM Chapter 7 and MCO 1040.31<Citation source="MCO 1040.31" />, career counseling supports SELRES member retention, progression, and informed decision-making.

Career counseling addresses.

- Promotion timing and requirements
- Education benefits awareness
- Career school opportunities
- Continuation vs separation decisions
- MOS development
- Reserve career path
- Transition to other components

## Career Planner Role

Per MCO 1040.31<Citation source="MCO 1040.31" />, units have Career Planners.

### Career Planner Authority

- Specific role within unit
- Authorized career counseling
- Coordinate education and benefit programs
- Support member career decisions

### Career Planner Tools

- Access to MCTFS for member data
- Knowledge of current SRIP and incentives
- Education benefit knowledge
- Coordination with HQMC career programs

## Career Counseling Cycles

Per MCRAMM and IRAM.

### Initial Counseling

For new SELRES members.

- Welcome and orientation
- Career path overview
- Initial counseling documentation
- Page 11 entry

### Periodic Counseling

Throughout career.

- Annual counseling typical
- Promotion preparation counseling
- Pre-separation counseling
- Specific event counseling

### Pre-Separation Counseling

For members approaching obligation end.

- Continuation decisions
- Reenlistment options
- Component transitions
- Separation processing

### Pre-Mobilization Counseling

For members alerted for mobilization.

- Mobilization preparation
- Family considerations
- Specific mobilization information

## Counseling Topics

Career counseling covers various topics.

### Promotion Timing

- Member's current grade and time in grade
- Eligibility timeline for next promotion
- Composite score factors
- SNCO selection board preparation

### Education Benefits

- MGIB-SR vs Post-9/11 GI Bill
- Tuition Assistance during active duty
- Loan Repayment Programs
- Specific education planning

### Career Schools

- NCO Academy, Career Course, Advanced Course
- Officer career schools
- Specific MOS schools
- Scheduling considerations

### Bonuses and Incentives

- Current SRIP eligibility
- Reenlistment bonuses
- Lateral move bonuses
- Specific incentive programs

### MOS Development

- MOS-specific career path
- Lateral move opportunities
- MOS changes
- Specific specialty programs

### Component Transitions

- Active to Reserve transition
- Reserve to Active transition
- SELRES to IRR transition
- Specific transition counseling

### Retirement Planning

- Retirement eligibility
- Required retirement points
- TSP and benefit planning
- Pre-retirement counseling

## Performance Steps for Career Counseling

### Step 1 - Schedule Counseling

Per cycle requirements.

- Annual counseling appointment
- Specific event-driven counseling
- Member-requested counseling

### Step 2 - Prepare for Counseling

Pull member's records.

- Current MCTFS data
- Master Brief Sheet
- Recent fitness reports or Pro/Con marks
- Training and qualification status
- Career planning history

### Step 3 - Conduct Counseling

In counseling session.

- Review member's current status
- Discuss career options
- Identify member's goals
- Provide specific guidance
- Document decisions

### Step 4 - Document Counseling

Per IRAM.

- Page 11 entry documenting counseling
- Specific topics covered
- Member's election or decisions
- Follow-up actions

### Step 5 - Follow-Up Actions

After counseling.

- Schedule any required actions
- Coordinate specific programs
- Connect member with resources
- Track follow-through

## Special Counseling Situations

### Re-enlistment Decision Point

Member approaching reenlistment.

- Eligibility verification
- Bonus options
- Career path implications
- Member's decision documented

### Promotion Failure Counseling

Member not selected for promotion.

- Counseling on reasons
- Specific improvement areas
- Future board preparation
- Member's options

### Separation Counseling

Member separating from SELRES.

- Specific separation procedures
- Benefits transition
- VA and TRICARE coordination
- Final pay and records

### Career Capstone Counseling

For senior members.

- Specific capstone events
- Final career decisions
- Retirement planning
- Mentor and mentee discussions

## Common Career Counseling Issues

### Counseling Skipped

Member not counseled per cycle.

- Schedule promptly
- Catch up on missed cycles
- Document late counseling

### Member Unaware of Options

Member doesn't know career options available.

- Comprehensive briefing
- Provide written materials
- Direct to career planner

### Documentation Gaps

Counseling conducted but not documented.

- Page 11 entry required per IRAM
- Document retroactively if missed
- Specific filing per IRAM

### Member's Career Decisions Reversed

Member changes career decisions.

- Document changes
- Coordinate any required actions
- Specific procedures per change

## Documentation Required for Career Counseling

For each counseling session.

- Page 11 entry per IRAM
- Member acknowledgment signature
- Witness signature
- Specific topics covered
- Decisions documented

All filed in OMPF via UD/MIPS document storage.

## Coordination Touchpoints

Career counseling touches.

- **Career Planner**: For specific career counseling
- **Education Office**: For education benefit counseling
- **HQMC**: For specific career programs
- **MCRSC**: For component transition support

## Quality Control on Career Counseling

Common errors.

1. **Counseling cycles skipped.** Members fall through gaps.
2. **Page 11 documentation missing.** Counseling conducted but not documented.
3. **Member's options not fully briefed.** Comprehensive briefing supports informed decisions.
4. **Follow-up not tracked.** Counseling decisions require follow-through.

## Related Roles

Career counseling affects every SELRES member.

- **Marines** receive career counseling and make informed decisions.
- **Leaders** at units conduct counseling sessions.
- **Commanders** at I&I oversee unit career counseling program.
