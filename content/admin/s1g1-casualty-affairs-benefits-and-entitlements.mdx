## Role and responsibility

The CACO coordinates benefits and entitlements delivery to the PNOK and qualifying beneficiaries. The S-1 officer signs by direction on benefits routing. The IPAC pay clerk processes pay-side benefits including Death Gratuity, SGLI, and dependency continuation. HQMC Casualty Section provides national-level coordination for SBP and VA-routed benefits per MCO 3040.4<Citation source="MCO 3040.4" /> Chapter 7.

Source documents flow from the certified PCR, the SGLV 8286 SGLI election, the SGLV 8283 Death Gratuity claim, the DD Form 93 emergency data, and the MCTFS dependency record. Benefits actions land as the Death Gratuity payment, the SGLI claim disbursement, the dependency continuation, and the SBP enrollment. Outputs include the benefits delivered, the case file documentation, and the long-term benefits roster for the survivor.

Per MCO 3040.4<Citation source="MCO 3040.4" /> Chapter 7, benefits include the immediate Death Gratuity, the SGLI insurance disbursement, dependency-based pay continuation, and long-term survivor programs. Per DODFMR Vol 7A<Citation source="DODFMR Vol 7A" /> Chapter 36 paragraph 2.0, Death Gratuity processes through immediate payment channels. Per DODFMR Vol 7A Chapter 47, SGLI processes per the insurance contract.

## Trigger events

- Confirmed casualty triggers benefits activation.
- CACO begins Benefits and Entitlements Visit per Chapter 4.
- SGLI claim submitted by beneficiary.
- Death Gratuity claim submitted by beneficiary.
- Dependency change identified through DEERS or DD Form 93.
- SBP election processed per the survivor's preference.
- Tricare continuation processed for surviving dependents.
- VA DIC routing initiated.

## Processing workflow

1. Identify applicable benefits. Per MCO 3040.4<Citation source="MCO 3040.4" /> Chapter 7 and the casualty category, identify each applicable benefit.
2. Coordinate Benefits and Entitlements Visit. Per MCO 3040.4<Citation source="MCO 3040.4" /> Chapter 4 Section 6 paragraph 2, the CACO conducts a Benefits and Entitlements Visit with the family.
3. Process Death Gratuity. Per DODFMR Vol 7A<Citation source="DODFMR Vol 7A" /> Chapter 36 paragraph 2.0 (Death Gratuity), process the immediate payment to designated beneficiaries.
4. Process SGLI claim. Per DODFMR Vol 7A<Citation source="DODFMR Vol 7A" /> Chapter 47, process the SGLI insurance claim per SGLV 8286 election. The beneficiary submits SGLV 8283 to claim.
5. Coordinate dependency continuation. Process pay-side continuation for surviving dependents per the survivor's eligibility.
6. Process SBP when applicable. The survivor elects SBP per the order rules. HQMC Casualty Section coordinates routing.
7. Process VA DIC routing. Coordinate VA Dependency and Indemnity Compensation enrollment with the survivor per the order rules.
8. Coordinate Tricare continuation. Process Tricare continuation for surviving dependents per the order rules.
9. Document benefits delivered. Capture every benefit, beneficiary, and disbursement in the casualty case file.
10. Brief command. The S-1 officer briefs the commander on benefits posture and outstanding actions.

## Systems of record and forms

### Systems

- MCTFS. Holds pay-side benefits state and final pay.
- DCIPS. Tracks benefits delivery at national level.
- DFAS. Processes Death Gratuity and SGLI claims.
- VA. Receives VA DIC routing and processes survivor benefits.
- DEERS. Holds dependency state for continuation processing.

### Forms

- SGLV 8283 Claim for Death Gratuity. Per DODFMR Vol 7A Chapter 36.
- SGLV 8286 SGLI Election. Source for SGLI beneficiary identification per DODFMR Vol 7A Chapter 47.
- DD Form 93 Record of Emergency Data. Identifies beneficiaries.
- SBP Election Form. Per the survivor's preference.
- VA Form 21-534 Application for Dependency and Indemnity Compensation. For VA DIC routing.

## Common pitfalls

- Death Gratuity delayed. Per DODFMR Vol 7A<Citation source="DODFMR Vol 7A" /> Chapter 36 paragraph 2.0, Death Gratuity is an immediate payment. Process within the timeline per the chapter.
- SGLV 8286 stale. The beneficiary identification is wrong. Refresh SGLV 8286 annually and at every life event.
- Dependency continuation not coordinated with DEERS. Pay state diverges from dependency state. Coordinate at benefits visit.
- SBP election not captured. The survivor benefits lag. Capture SBP election at the Benefits and Entitlements Visit per Chapter 4.
- VA DIC routing not initiated. The survivor loses VA benefit window. Initiate at benefits visit.

## Decision points

- Death Gratuity beneficiary determination. Per DODFMR Vol 7A<Citation source="DODFMR Vol 7A" /> Chapter 36 paragraph 2.4 (Beneficiaries), the beneficiary chain follows DoD rules with member designation per SGLV 8286.
- SGLI beneficiary determination. Per DODFMR Vol 7A<Citation source="DODFMR Vol 7A" /> Chapter 47, the SGLI beneficiary is per SGLV 8286 election.
- SBP versus VA DIC selection. The survivor elects per personal circumstance. The CACO advises on options per Chapter 4.
