## Annual Muster Authority

Per 10 USC 10206<Citation source="10 USC 10206" /> and MCRAMM Chapter 8, IRR members participate in annual muster duty. The statutory authority requires.

- Periodic verification of member status
- Address and contact information updates
- Readiness data updates
- Specific reporting requirements

## Muster Purposes

The annual muster supports several functions.

### Member Accountability

Verify the member is alive, reachable, and identifiable.

### Mobilization Availability

Update member's availability for potential recall.

- Current address for orders
- Current contact information
- Family situation and dependents
- Specific availability factors

### Readiness Data

Update member's readiness profile.

- Current employment status
- Current health status
- Specific readiness factors

### Membership Points

Members completing muster earn 15 membership points per anniversary year.

- Contributes to retirement point total
- Specific procedure for muster duty pay (in some cases)

## Muster Process

Per MCRAMM Chapter 8.

### Step 1 - Notification

MCRSC sends notification.

- Standard muster notification letter
- Specific deadline for response
- Specific actions required

### Step 2 - Member Response

Member completes muster actions.

- Verify contact information
- Update readiness data
- Submit specific muster forms
- Acknowledge receipt and compliance

### Step 3 - Documentation

MCRSC documents the muster.

- Member's compliance
- Updated information
- Membership points awarded

### Step 4 - Follow-Up for Non-Response

For members not responding.

- Additional contact attempts
- Specific procedures per non-response
- Possible consequences for continued non-response

## Performance Steps for I&I Admin

I&I admin rarely directly handles IRR muster. MCRSC primarily handles.

### When IRR Muster Question Arises

For specific situations.

- Refer member to MCRSC
- Provide MCRSC contact information
- Verify member's IRR status

### When Local IRR Member Needs Help

For local member with muster question.

- Direct to MCRSC for muster processing
- Specific local support if needed
- Coordinate with MCRSC if necessary

## Muster Duty Pay

Per DOD FMR Volume 7A<Citation source="DOD FMR Volume 7A" />.

### Standard Muster Duty (MD) Pay

Members performing muster duty receive specific pay.

- Single muster duty pay rate
- Specific procedures for processing

### When MD Pay Applies

- Specific muster duty per orders
- Member receives MD pay rate
- Tax treatment per IRS

### When MD Pay Does Not Apply

For routine annual muster correspondence.

- May not require MD pay
- Different procedure than active muster duty
- Specific provisions per MCRAMM

## Failure to Respond to Muster

Per MCRAMM Chapter 8.

### Initial Non-Response

Member misses muster window.

- Additional contact attempts
- Extended response window
- Specific procedures

### Continued Non-Response

Member fails to respond after multiple attempts.

- Possible status review
- Possible transfer to other component
- Specific procedures per MCRSC

### Loss of Membership Points

Failure to complete muster.

- No 15 membership points for that anniversary year
- Affects retirement calculations
- Specific recovery procedures

### Possible Discharge

Long-term continued non-response.

- Specific discharge procedures
- Discharge characterization based on circumstances
- Coordinate with HQMC

## Member Reachability

Per MCRAMM, member must remain reachable.

### Address Maintenance

- Member's address current with MCRSC
- Address updates via MOL
- Annual verification

### Contact Information

- Phone, email, or other contact methods
- Specific contact preferences
- Updated as needed

### Specific Member Responsibility

Per 10 USC and MCRAMM.

- Member responsible for staying reachable
- Specific consequences for non-reachability
- Member's obligation continues during IRR

## Common Muster Issues

### Member Moved Without Address Update

Member moved but didn't update address.

- Mail returned to MCRSC
- Member non-responsive
- Specific procedures to locate

### Member Disregards Muster

Member ignores muster correspondence.

- Continued contact attempts
- Specific consequences
- May trigger status review

### Member Dispute About Muster

Member disagrees with muster requirement.

- Specific procedures for member concerns
- Member's obligation continues
- Coordinate with MCRSC

### Lost Member Contact Information

Member's information lost or outdated.

- Use multiple contact methods
- Specific search procedures
- Possible transfer to different status

## Coordination Touchpoints

Annual muster touches.

- **Marine Corps Reserve Support Command (MCRSC)**: Primary IRR muster administration
- **HQMC**: For specific muster policy
- **MOL**: For member self-service updates
- **VA**: For some address coordination

## Related Roles

Annual muster primarily affects IRR members.

- **Marines** in IRR respond to annual muster correspondence.
- **Leaders** at SMCR units rarely engage in IRR muster.
- **Commanders** at I&I support occasional IRR member contacts about muster.
