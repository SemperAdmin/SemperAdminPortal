## Role and responsibility

The Commander, Marine Corps Forces Command (COMMARFORCOM), the Commander, Marine Forces Reserve (COMMARFORRES), and the regional Marine Corps Installations (MCI) Commanding Generals appoint the Regional Victim Witness Liaison Officer (RVWLO) in writing per MCO 5800.16<Citation source="MCO 5800.16" /> Chapter 4 paragraph 040902(A)(1). The installation commander appoints the Installation Victim Witness Liaison Officer (IVWLO) per paragraph 040902(B)(2). The RVWLO and IVWLO source from the supporting Legal Services Support Section (LSSS) or Staff Judge Advocate (SJA) office per paragraphs 040903(A)(1) and 040903(B)(1). The RVWLO consolidates regional VWAP data and forwards to the VWAP Director, JMJ on a quarterly basis per paragraph 040903(A)(5). The IVWLO chairs the local Victim and Witness Assistance Council quarterly per paragraph 040903(B)(5).

Source documents flow from the appointment letters, the LSSS or SJA personnel roster, the regional IVWLO roster maintained by the RVWLO per paragraph 040903(A)(3), the unit VWAC roster maintained by the IVWLO per paragraph 040903(B)(3), the Victim and Witness Assistance Council meeting minutes, the regional VWLO meeting minutes, the quarterly DD Form 2701-2703 distribution data per paragraph 040903(A)(5), the training rosters per paragraphs 040903(A)(4) and 040903(B)(7), and the installation VWAP website per paragraph 040903(B)(6). Outputs include the appointed RVWLO and IVWLO, the established Victim and Witness Assistance Council, the quarterly Council meeting minutes, the semi-annual regional meeting minutes, the quarterly VWAP data report to JMJ, the maintained VWAC and IVWLO rosters, and the audit trail for IGMC inspection.

Per Chapter 4 paragraph 040902(B)(4), the installation commander establishes a local Victim and Witness Assistance Council chaired by the IVWLO. Per paragraph 040903(B)(5), the IVWLO chairs and conducts a quarterly installation-level Council. Per paragraph 040903(A)(7), the RVWLO chairs and conducts a semi-annual Regional VWLO meeting to discuss VWAP-related issues.

## Trigger events

- New regional command stand-up triggering RVWLO appointment.
- Installation commander change triggering IVWLO appointment refresh.
- Quarterly installation Council meeting cycle opens per paragraph 040903(B)(5).
- Semi-annual Regional VWLO meeting cycle opens per paragraph 040903(A)(7).
- Quarterly VWAP data submission cycle to JMJ per paragraph 040903(A)(5).
- New VWLO or VWAC appointment triggering basic VWAP training per paragraph 040903(A)(4).
- Annual VWAC training cycle per paragraph 040903(B)(7).
- Deploying unit triggers VWAP training per paragraph 040903(B)(11).
- New tenant command triggers VWAC appointment per paragraph 040903(B)(4).
- IGMC VWAP FA inspection covers Installation Commanders (Section 01.0), RVWLO (02.0), and IVWLO (03.0).

## Processing workflow

1. Appoint the RVWLO. Per Chapter 4 paragraph 040902(A)(1), the COMMARFORCOM, COMMARFORRES, and regional MCI CGs appoint the RVWLO in writing by name, title, duty address, and telephone number. Per paragraph 040903(A)(1), the RVWLO is an officer or civilian member of the commander's staff sourced from the supporting LSSS or SJA office.
2. Appoint the IVWLO. Per paragraph 040902(B)(2), the installation commander appoints the IVWLO in writing as an officer or civilian member of the Installation Staff. Per paragraph 040903(B)(1), the IVWLO sources from the supporting LSSS or SJA office.
3. Distribute VWLO contact information. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3), the installation commander ensures VWAP representatives aboard the installation receive the VWLO's name and phone number.
4. Establish the local Council. Per paragraph 040902(B)(4), the installation commander establishes a local Victim and Witness Assistance Council chaired by the IVWLO.
5. Appoint Confinement Facility VWAC Council representative. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(5), when a Marine Corps confinement facility is located aboard the installation, the installation commander appoints in writing a confinement facility representative as the VWAC and Council representative.
6. Construct the installation VWAP directory. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(6) and paragraph 040903(B)(6), with the local Marine and Family Programs Office, the installation constructs and maintains a directory of military and civilian programs, services, and crime victim compensation funds. The directory publishes on the installation's public website.
7. Maintain the regional IVWLO roster. Per paragraph 040903(A)(3), the RVWLO maintains a list of IVWLOs under the cognizance of the commanding general and provides copies to the VWAP Director, JMJ.
8. Maintain the installation VWAC roster. Per paragraph 040903(B)(3), the IVWLO maintains a list of VWACs, service providers, and SJAs aboard the installation. Copies provide to all VWACs and service providers.
9. Confirm VWAC appointment at every command. Per paragraph 040903(B)(4), the IVWLO ensures each organization (battalion/squadron level and above) including tenant commands appoints a VWAC in writing by name, title, duty address, and telephone number.
10. Chair quarterly installation Council. Per paragraph 040903(B)(5), the IVWLO chairs and conducts a quarterly installation-level Victim and Witness Assistance Council meeting.
11. Chair semi-annual Regional VWLO meeting. Per paragraph 040903(A)(7), the RVWLO chairs and conducts a semi-annual Regional VWLO meeting.
12. Provide basic VWAP training. Per paragraph 040903(A)(4), the RVWLO ensures basic VWAP training is available to all new VWLOs and VWACs. The RVWLO maintains a roster of trained personnel per paragraph 040903(A)(4).
13. Run IVWLO annual VWAC training. Per paragraph 040903(B)(7), the IVWLO obtains and distributes relevant information and provides annual training to all VWACs aboard the installation.
14. Coordinate deploying unit training. Per paragraph 040903(B)(11), the IVWLO ensures deploying units receive appropriate VWAP training and information prior to deployment.
15. Compile and forward quarterly VWAP data. Per paragraph 040903(A)(5), the RVWLO collects and maintains data on the number of victims and witnesses who received DD Forms 2701-2703 and who elected notification via DD Form 2704. Forwards to the VWAP Director, JMJ quarterly.

## Systems of record and forms

### Systems

- Installation VWAP Website. Per paragraph 040903(B)(6). Holds the directory of VWAP personnel and programs.
- Regional IVWLO Roster. Per paragraph 040903(A)(3). Maintained by the RVWLO.
- Installation VWAC Roster. Per paragraph 040903(B)(3). Maintained by the IVWLO.
- VWAP Council Meeting Records. Per paragraph 040902(B)(4) and 040903(B)(5).
- Electronic Military Justice Case Management System. Per paragraph 040904(E)(3). Holds VWAP data.

### Forms

- RVWLO Appointment Letter. Per paragraph 040902(A)(1).
- IVWLO Appointment Letter. Per paragraph 040902(B)(2).
- Confinement Facility Council Representative Appointment Letter. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(5).
- VWAP Council Meeting Minutes. Quarterly.
- Regional VWLO Meeting Minutes. Semi-annual.
- Installation VWAP Directory. Per paragraph 040902(B)(3)(6).
- Quarterly VWAP Data Report. Per paragraph 040903(A)(5).
- Basic VWAP Training Completion Roster. Per paragraph 040903(A)(4).

## Common pitfalls

- RVWLO appointment missing name, title, duty address, or telephone number. Per paragraph 040902(A)(1), all elements record.
- IVWLO not sourced from LSSS or SJA. Per paragraph 040903(B)(1), the source flows from the supporting legal services.
- Local Council not established. Per paragraph 040902(B)(4), the installation commander establishes the Council chaired by the IVWLO.
- Quarterly installation Council missed. Per paragraph 040903(B)(5), the cadence is quarterly.
- Semi-annual Regional VWLO meeting missed. Per paragraph 040903(A)(7), the cadence is semi-annual.
- VWAC roster not maintained or distributed. Per paragraph 040903(B)(3), the IVWLO maintains and distributes to VWACs and service providers.
- IVWLO list not provided to JMJ. Per paragraph 040903(A)(3), the RVWLO provides copies of the IVWLO list to JMJ.
- Basic VWAP training not available to new VWLOs and VWACs. Per paragraph 040903(A)(4), the RVWLO ensures availability.
- Quarterly VWAP data not forwarded to JMJ. Per paragraph 040903(A)(5), the RVWLO forwards quarterly.
- Installation VWAP website missing directory. Per paragraph 040903(B)(6), the directory publishes on the installation's public website.
- Deploying unit VWAP training skipped. Per paragraph 040903(B)(11), the IVWLO ensures deploying units receive training prior to deployment.

## Decision points

- RVWLO source (LSSS or SJA). Per paragraph 040903(A)(1), the source flows from the supporting legal services. The CG selects based on regional structure.
- Council chair (IVWLO). Per paragraph 040903(B)(5), the IVWLO chairs the installation Council. Per paragraph 040902(B)(4), the local Council establishes under installation commander direction.
- Tenant command VWAC scope. Per paragraph 040903(B)(4), tenant commands at battalion/squadron level and above appoint a VWAC.
- Training cadence. Basic VWAP training runs at appointment per paragraph 040903(A)(4). Annual refresher runs at the IVWLO level per paragraph 040903(B)(7).
