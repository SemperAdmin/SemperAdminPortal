## Reserve Incentive Programs Overview

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4, the Marine Corps offers incentive programs to attract and retain Reserve members in critical specialties. The Selected Reserve Incentive Program (SRIP) is the primary umbrella for these incentives.

Specific incentives change annually via MARADMIN. Always reference current MARADMIN.

## Selected Reserve Incentive Program (SRIP)

SRIP is the umbrella program for Reserve enlistment and reenlistment incentives.

### SRIP Components

- Enlistment bonuses
- Reenlistment bonuses
- Affiliation bonuses (PS members joining SMCR)
- Loan Repayment Program (LRP)
- Specific MOS-targeted incentives

### How SRIP Bonuses Pay

Bonuses generally pay in installments over the obligation period.

- Initial payment at contract signing
- Annual payments tied to qualifying year completion
- Final payment at obligation completion
- Specific structures per current MARADMIN

### Eligibility for SRIP

Member qualifies for specific SRIP based on.

- MOS or specialty
- Service category (NPS, PS, Officer)
- Specific contract requirements
- Continued satisfactory service

## Reserve Optional Enlistment Program (ROEP)

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.3, ROEP provides specific enlistment options for Reserve members.

### ROEP Structure

- Specific enlistment contract options
- Tailored to Reserve service patterns
- Specific length and obligation options
- Coordinated with critical MOS needs

### When ROEP Applies

- Standard SMCR enlistment contracts
- Specific accession periods
- Specific MOS targeting

## Incremental Initial Active Duty for Training (IIADT)

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.4, IIADT provides structured initial active duty for training.

### IIADT Structure

- Initial Boot Camp at MCRD
- MCT and MOS school as continuing IADT
- Total IADT period varies by MOS
- Member transitions to SMCR drilling after IADT

This is the standard NPS Marine path covered in NPS Enlisted Accessions.

## Education Assistance

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.5, specific education assistance programs.

### MGIB-SR

The Montgomery GI Bill Selected Reserve covered separately in education benefits.

### Tuition Assistance

For Reserve members on active duty, covered separately.

### Loan Repayment Program (LRP)

Per MCRAMM, LRP pays down qualifying student loans for eligible reservists.

- Critical MOS designation
- Specific contract obligation
- Annual payments based on qualifying years

## TRICARE Reserve Select (TRS)

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.6, TRS is incentive coverage for SMCR members covered separately.

## Affiliation Bonuses

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.7, affiliation bonuses for PS members joining SMCR.

### Eligibility

- Prior service in qualifying capacity
- Joining or returning to SMCR
- Specific MOS designation
- Time since prior service within window

### Bonus Amounts

Per current MARADMIN.

### Payment Structure

- Installment payments tied to qualifying years
- Specific obligations per contract

## Inactive Duty Training (IDT) Travel Reimbursement

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.8, specific travel reimbursement programs.

### Eligibility

- Drilling reservists with significant travel to drilling location
- Specific distance thresholds
- Coordinated with current MARADMIN provisions

### Reimbursement

- Per mile or specific allowance
- Capped at specific limits
- Tax treatment per IRS

## SMCR Retraining and Lateral Move Program

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 4.9, supports lateral moves into critical SMCR MOSs.

- Specific MOS designation
- Required retraining
- Coordinated with HQMC

## Reserve Affiliation Composite Score Points Incentive

Per MCRAMM, additional composite score points for SMCR drilling Marines.

- Promotion eligibility composite score
- Specific points for SMCR drilling participation
- Updated per current MARADMIN

## Performance Steps for Incentive Processing

### Step 1 - Verify Current SRIP Authorization

Before discussing incentives.

- Pull current SRIP MARADMIN
- Verify specific incentive currently authorized
- Verify member's MOS qualifies

### Step 2 - Counsel Member on Eligibility

Brief member on specific incentives applicable.

- Bonus amounts and structure
- Service obligation requirements
- Continuing eligibility requirements

### Step 3 - Coordinate Contract Documentation

Incentives are contractual.

- Bonus terms in enlistment or reenlistment contract
- Member acknowledges agreement
- Service obligation documented

### Step 4 - Process Initial Payment

Per contract.

- Initial bonus payment at contract signing
- Coordinated with PAC for processing
- Member receives initial payment

### Step 5 - Track Annual Payments

Each qualifying year.

- Verify member completed satisfactory year
- Submit annual bonus payment request
- Continue per contract structure

### Step 6 - Track Total Obligation

Until obligation completes.

- All payments tracked cumulatively
- Final payment at obligation end
- Documentation closed out

## Common Incentive Issues

### Wrong Bonus Quoted

SRIP changes annually. Outdated information.

### MOS No Longer Eligible

MOS might have come off SRIP since member's contract. Verify before promising.

### Annual Year Not Verified

Member must complete satisfactory year for each annual payment.

### Bonus Recoupment

If member fails obligation, bonus recoupment might apply.

- Specific recoupment procedures per DOD FMR
- Member owes back-payment of unearned bonuses
- Processed via PAC

## Documentation Required

For incentive processing.

- Original contract with bonus terms
- Annual qualifying year documentation
- Annual bonus payment requests
- Recoupment documentation if applicable

## Coordination Touchpoints

Incentive processing touches.

- **HQMC SRIP**: Program administration and current authorizations
- **DFAS**: Bonus payment processing
- **Member's loan servicer (for LRP)**: Loan payment coordination
- **Marine Corps Recruiting Command**: For initial enlistment incentives

## Related Roles

Incentives affect Reserve members from accession through obligation completion.

- **Marines** (Reserve members) qualify for incentives based on MOS and contract.
- **Leaders** counsel members on incentive options.
- **Commanders** at I&I support member benefit awareness.
