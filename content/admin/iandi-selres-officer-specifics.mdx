## Reserve Officer Service

Per MCRAMM Chapter 7 and Chapter 4 Section 4, Reserve officers face specific provisions different from enlisted SELRES.

## Officer Participation Requirements

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4.

### Same Drilling Standards

Reserve officers drill the same minimum periods as enlisted.

- 48 IDTs annually
- AT of 12-14 days
- Specific MOS qualification

### Additional Officer Responsibilities

Beyond drilling.

- Officer-specific training requirements
- Leadership duties at SMCR units
- Career schools per officer progression
- Specific reporting and coordination

### Minimum Officer Participation

Per MCRAMM, officers must demonstrate satisfactory participation.

- 50 points minimum for satisfactory year
- Continued MOS qualification
- Acceptable performance per evaluations

## Officer Tours

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 4.

### Office Tours

Reserve officers serve specific tours per assignments.

- Standard tour lengths per billet type
- Specific transitions between assignments
- Coordination with HQMC officer manpower

### Specific Tour Lengths

Vary by billet.

- Battalion XO or staff: 2-3 years typical
- Specific operational billets: 2-3 years
- Senior officer billets: Varies

### Officer Strength Limitations

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 4.3.

- Specific officer strength limits per grade
- Selection boards prioritize within limits
- Officers serve within authorized strength

## Officer Promotion Path

Reserve officers promote per MCO P1400.32<Citation source="MCO P1400.32" /> Volume 1.

### Selection Boards

Reserve officer promotions through selection boards.

- O-3 (Captain) through O-9 (Lieutenant General)
- Boards convened by HQMC
- Specific Reserve consideration

### Mandatory Removal Dates

Officers face mandatory removal dates per grade.

- Up-or-out structure
- Officers not promoted by specific dates removed from active reserve
- Specific provisions per grade

## Specific Officer Considerations

### Aviation Officers

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 4.7.

- Aviation Career Continuation Pay (ACCP)
- Specific flight currency requirements
- Aviation-specific career path

### Reserve Warrant Officer / Chief Warrant Officer

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 4.8.

- Specific warrant officer career path
- Different from commissioned officer progression
- Specific technical specialty focus

### Commanding Officer / Officer in Charge (CO/OIC)

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 4.6.

- Specific qualifications for CO/OIC duty
- Senior officer billets
- Significant responsibility

## Officer Pay

Reserve officers receive officer-specific pay.

- Officer drill pay rate (higher than enlisted)
- Officer BAS rate
- Officer-specific bonuses where applicable
- Continued pay during AT and active duty

## Officer-Specific I&I Tasks

For officer SELRES members.

### Step 1 - Officer Assignment Coordination

When officer arrives or transitions.

- Officer billet identification
- OQR coordination
- Specific officer counseling

### Step 2 - Officer Career Counseling

Periodic career counseling.

- Promotion timing
- Career schools
- Specialty considerations
- Mandatory removal dates

### Step 3 - Officer Promotion Coordination

For promotion eligible officers.

- Coordination with selection boards
- Documentation supporting promotion
- Promotion warrant processing if selected

### Step 4 - Officer Mobilization Coordination

For mobilizing officers.

- Specific officer mobilization processing
- Coordination with active duty receiving command
- Officer-specific deployment processing

## Common Officer Issues

### Mandatory Removal Date Approaching

Officer not promoted approaching MRD.

- Counseling on options
- Specific provisions per grade
- Possible separation processing

### Promotion Board Non-Selection

Officer not selected for promotion.

- Continuing eligibility per grade
- Specific subsequent board considerations
- Career counseling

### Officer Career Schools

Officer needing specific career school.

- Coordinate scheduling
- AT or ADT for school attendance
- Specific career progression

### Aviation Officer Currency

Aviation officer flight currency issues.

- Coordinate with aviation unit
- Specific currency requirements
- Possible duty status changes

## Officer Documentation Requirements

For officer SELRES.

- Officer Qualification Record (OQR) - not Service Record Book
- Fitness reports per cycle
- Officer-specific training documentation
- Specific officer career documentation

## Officer Evaluation

Per MCO P1400.32<Citation source="MCO P1400.32" />, Reserve officers receive fitness reports.

- Annual fitness reports
- Specific reporting officials
- Officer-specific evaluation criteria

## Coordination Touchpoints

Officer SELRES management touches.

- **HQMC Officer Manpower**: For policy and assignments
- **MARFORRES G-1**: For specific Reserve officer matters
- **Member's officer chain of command**: For unit-level officer actions
- **Selection boards**: For promotion processing

## Quality Control on Officer SELRES

Common errors.

1. **OQR vs SRB confusion.** Officers have OQR.
2. **Officer-specific counseling skipped.** Officers have specific counseling needs.
3. **Mandatory removal dates not tracked.** Officers face MRDs that affect career.
4. **Aviation currency not coordinated.** Aviation officers need ACIP and currency tracking.

## Related Roles

Reserve officer specifics affect commissioned and warrant officers.

- **Marines** (officers) maintain qualifications and serve in officer billets.
- **Leaders** (junior officers) lead drilling Marines.
- **Commanders** (senior officers) command Reserve units.
