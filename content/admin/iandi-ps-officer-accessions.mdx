## Who Is PS Officer

Per MCRAMM Chapter 2 Section 3.6, Prior Service (PS) Officer accessions include officers with previous commissioned service.

### Categories

- Marine Corps officers transitioning from active to Reserve
- Marine Corps officers returning from Standby or IRR
- Officers from other Services (Inter-Service Transfer)
- Specific direct commission cases

## PS Officer Eligibility

Eligibility requires.

- Previous commissioned service in qualifying capacity
- Honorable separation or current good standing
- Within specific time-since-separation window
- Available billet at qualifying SMCR location
- Meets specific MOS or specialty requirements
- Specific eligibility per current MARADMIN

## PS Officer Categories

### Active Marine Corps Officer to Reserve

Most common path.

- Officer completing active duty obligation
- Affiliating with SMCR
- Same MOS or different specialty
- Standard transition procedures

### Marine Corps Reserve Officer Reentry

Officer returning from IRR or Standby.

- Specific reentry procedures
- Might require refresher training
- Specific eligibility per time since drilling

### Inter-Service Transfer

Officer from other Service (Army, Navy, Air Force).

- Inter-Service Transfer procedures
- MOS conversion required typically
- Specific evaluation and acceptance

### Direct Commission for Specific Specialties

For specific medical, legal, or specialty officers.

- Direct commission without OCS or TBS for the specialty
- Specialty-specific qualifications
- Common for medical, legal, chaplain corps

## Performance Steps for PS Officer Receipt

When PS officer arrives at I&I.

### Step 1 - Verify Documentation

Pull officer's records.

- Prior service DD Form 214<Citation source="DD Form 214" /> or equivalent
- Prior officer record
- Discharge characterization
- Time since separation

### Step 2 - Verify Eligibility

Confirm member meets PS officer accession criteria.

- Honorable separation or current good standing
- Within time window for direct accession
- Meets physical and security requirements

### Step 3 - Coordinate MOS Verification

For the officer's specialty.

- Marine Corps MOS verified or assigned
- Inter-Service MOS conversion if applicable
- Specialty board for medical/legal/etc. if applicable

### Step 4 - Process Initial Drilling

For officers requiring no additional training.

- Direct to SMCR drilling status
- Initial officer briefing at unit
- Drill pay activation

### Step 5 - Coordinate Refresher Training

For officers requiring refresher.

- Specific refresher training per program
- Might include formal school or OJT
- Coordinate scheduling with HQMC

### Step 6 - Update OQR

- Prior officer documentation in OQR
- New SMCR contract in OQR
- All accession documents per UD/MIPS

## Direct Commission Programs

For specific specialties.

### Medical Corps

- Direct commission for physicians
- No OCS or TBS for medical officers
- Medical Corps officer designation
- Specific officer rank based on training

### Judge Advocate General Corps (JAG)

- Direct commission for attorneys
- Specific JAG officer training
- Legal services officer billets

### Chaplain Corps

- Direct commission for clergy
- Specific Chaplain Corps officer training
- Chaplain billets in units

### Other Specialties

Specific direct commission programs for other specialties per current MARADMIN.

## Inter-Service Transfer

Officers from other Services might transfer to Marine Corps Reserve.

### Eligibility Verification

- Honorable service in current Service
- Inter-Service Transfer authority
- Available billet

### MOS Conversion

- Officer's current Service MOS might not match Marine Corps MOS
- Conversion training required typically
- Specific conversion procedures

### Officer Status After Transfer

- Marine Corps officer rank confirmed
- Marine Corps SMCR drilling status
- Pay structure per Marine Corps officer

## PS Officer Documentation Required

For PS officer receipt.

- Prior service DD Form 214<Citation source="DD Form 214" /> or equivalent
- Prior officer record
- New SMCR officer contract
- MOS verification or conversion documentation
- Refresher training certificates if applicable
- DEERS verification
- SGLI election

All filed in OQR via UD/MIPS document storage.

## Officer-Specific I&I Tasks

When PS officer arrives.

### Welcome and Integration

- Welcome to unit officer leadership
- Brief on officer roles and expectations
- Connect with senior officers

### Documentation Coordination

- Establish OQR with prior service documents
- Verify all training and qualification credit
- Address any documentation gaps

### Pay System Setup

- Officer drill pay activation
- Officer BAS rate
- Specific specialty bonuses if applicable

### Career Planning

- Officer career counseling
- Promotion eligibility based on combined service
- Officer-specific opportunities

## Common PS Officer Issues

### Time Since Separation Long

Officer separated for many years returning to service.

- Significant retraining typically required
- Possible age and physical considerations
- Specific eligibility verification

### Inter-Service MOS Conversion

Officer from other Service requires Marine Corps MOS.

- Conversion training required
- Specific Marine Corps MOS school
- Coordinate scheduling

### Discharge Characterization Issues

Less than honorable discharge from prior service.

- Limits PS officer accession eligibility
- Specific waiver procedures
- Coordinate with HQMC

### Direct Commission Specialty Issues

For direct commission programs.

- Specific specialty board approval
- Marine Corps officer rank establishment
- Specific specialty training requirements

## Quality Control on PS Officer Receipt

Common errors.

1. **Officer rank verification missed.** Verify Marine Corps officer rank.
2. **OQR vs SRB confusion.** Officers have OQR.
3. **Specialty bonus not coordinated.** Some PS officers eligible for bonuses.
4. **Refresher training requirements unclear.** Coordinate with HQMC for specific requirements.

## Coordination Touchpoints

PS officer processing touches.

- **HQMC Officer Manpower**: For policy and decisions
- **Specialty boards**: For medical/legal/chaplain
- **MOS conversion schools**: For Inter-Service transfers
- **MCRSC**: For reentry from IRR

## Related Roles

PS officer accessions bring experienced officers to SMCR units.

- **Marines** (PS officers) bring experience to SMCR units.
- **Leaders** integrate PS officers based on experience.
- **Commanders** at SMCR units leverage PS officer skills.
