## Limited Duty (LIMDU)

Per MCRAMM Chapter 6 and MCO P6320.13<Citation source="MCO P6320.13" />, Limited Duty (LIMDU) is a status for members with temporary medical conditions limiting duty.

### When LIMDU Applies

Members enter LIMDU when.

- Medical condition limits ability to perform full duties
- Condition is temporary or has potential for recovery
- Treatment continues with goal of return to full duty
- Specific medical evaluation supports LIMDU status

### LIMDU Categories

LIMDU has different durations.

#### Initial LIMDU (8 months)

- Initial period for evaluation and treatment
- Member continues service with restrictions
- Treatment focused on full duty return

#### LIMDU Extension (8 additional months)

- If condition has not resolved
- Continued treatment
- Approaching disposition decision

#### LIMDU End

After 16 months total LIMDU, member's case must reach disposition.

- Full duty return
- Disability separation processing
- Specific other dispositions

### Reserve Member LIMDU

Reserve members in LIMDU have specific provisions.

- Drilling restrictions per medical limitations
- Pay continues for drilling performed
- LIMDU may affect deployment eligibility
- Coordinate with medical staff for specific accommodations

## Medical Hold

For members on active duty awaiting medical disposition.

### When Medical Hold Applies

Members enter Medical Hold when.

- On active duty (mobilization, AT, etc.)
- Medical condition prevents return to drilling status
- Treatment continues
- Disposition pending

### Medical Hold Status

- Member remains on active duty
- Active duty pay and benefits
- Treatment continues
- Disposition decision pending

### Medical Hold Outcomes

After Medical Hold completes.

- Return to drilling status (full duty)
- Continue on active duty for further treatment
- Disability separation processing
- Specific other dispositions

## Performance Steps for LIMDU/Medical Hold Processing

### Step 1 - Identify Medical Need

When member's medical condition affects duty.

- Medical evaluation by provider
- Specific recommendations
- Coordinate with member

### Step 2 - Process Status Action

For LIMDU.

- UD/MIPS LIMDU transaction
- Effective date of medical evaluation
- Specific category and duration

For Medical Hold.

- UD/MIPS Medical Hold transaction
- Active duty status continues
- Specific evaluation timeline

### Step 3 - Coordinate Treatment

With medical staff.

- Treatment plan
- Periodic evaluations
- Goal of return to full duty

### Step 4 - Track to Disposition

Through LIMDU or Medical Hold period.

- Periodic evaluations
- Coordinate disposition
- Action at end of LIMDU/Medical Hold period

### Step 5 - Process Disposition Action

Based on outcome.

- Return to full duty (UD/MIPS transaction)
- Disability separation (DES processing)
- Other disposition per evaluation

## Disability Evaluation System (DES) Coordination

If LIMDU does not result in full duty return, DES processing.

### DES Initiation

Triggered by.

- LIMDU period ending without recovery
- Medical board recommendation
- Specific medical evaluation findings

### DES Process

Detailed in its own page.

- Medical Evaluation Board (MEB)
- Physical Evaluation Board (PEB)
- Disposition recommendation

## Common LIMDU/Medical Hold Issues

### LIMDU Period Expiring

Member's LIMDU approaching 16 months total.

- Coordinate disposition
- DES referral if recovery uncertain
- Specific procedures per MCRAMM

### Member Recovers Earlier Than Expected

LIMDU member recovers before LIMDU period ends.

- Update medical evaluation
- Process return to full duty
- UD/MIPS transaction

### Member Worsens During LIMDU

Member's condition deteriorates.

- Coordinate with medical staff
- May accelerate disposition
- Specific procedures

### Reserve Member LIMDU Coordination

Reserve member in LIMDU during drilling status.

- Specific drilling accommodations
- Pay continues for drilling
- Coordinate with PAC for specific situations

## Medical Pay Considerations

During LIMDU and Medical Hold.

### LIMDU Pay

- Pay continues per regular schedule
- Drill pay for drilling performed
- Specific situations may affect pay

### Medical Hold Pay

- Active duty pay continues
- BAH and BAS at active duty rates
- Coordinate with PAC

### Incapacitation Pay

For specific Reserve members unable to perform civilian work.

- Per DODI 1241.01<Citation source="DODI 1241.01" />
- Coordinated with LOD finding
- Pay matches civilian income lost

## Documentation Required

For LIMDU and Medical Hold.

- Medical evaluation supporting status
- Specific category designation
- Treatment plan
- Periodic re-evaluations
- Disposition documentation

## Coordination Touchpoints

LIMDU and Medical Hold processing touches.

- **Local installation MTF**: For medical evaluation
- **Medical Board**: For specific evaluations
- **PEB**: For disability evaluation
- **Member's civilian providers**: For coordination of care
- **PAC**: For specific pay and status processing

## Quality Control on LIMDU/Medical Hold

Common errors.

1. **LIMDU period not tracked.** Member in LIMDU past 16 months without disposition.
2. **Disposition not coordinated.** End of LIMDU without clear next step.
3. **Reserve drilling not coordinated.** Reserve LIMDU member drilling without proper accommodations.
4. **Pay processing issues.** LIMDU or Medical Hold affecting pay incorrectly.

## Related Roles

LIMDU and Medical Hold affect members with medical conditions.

- **Marines** with medical conditions enter LIMDU and coordinate treatment.
- **Leaders** at units accommodate LIMDU members appropriately.
- **Commanders** at I&I coordinate LIMDU processing and medical care.
