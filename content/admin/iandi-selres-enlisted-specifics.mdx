## Reserve Enlisted Service

Per MCRAMM Chapter 7 and Chapter 4 Section 5, Reserve enlisted Marines have specific provisions for service.

## Enlisted Categories

### Junior Enlisted

E-1 through E-3.

- Initial Reserve service
- Standard drill requirements
- Specific career counseling

### Non-Commissioned Officers (NCOs)

E-4 (Corporal) through E-5 (Sergeant).

- Increased leadership responsibility
- Specific NCO duties at unit
- Career progression toward SNCO

### Staff Non-Commissioned Officers (SNCOs)

E-6 (Staff Sergeant) through E-9 (Sergeant Major).

- Senior leadership responsibility
- Specific senior leader duties
- Mentor and develop junior Marines
- Specific SNCO career considerations

## Enlisted Tours

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 5.

### NPS Tours

For Non-Prior Service enlisted.

- Initial 6-year SELRES (Selected Reserve, what most Marines call SMCR or the Reserves) contract typical
- Specific MOS service period
- Continued through obligation

### SNCO Tours

For Staff NCOs.

- Specific tour requirements per billet
- Increased leadership focus
- Specific time considerations

### Reserve Enlisted Opportunities

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4 Section 5.1.

- Various career opportunities for Reserve enlisted
- Specific MOS opportunities
- Career schools and qualifications

## Enlisted Promotion Path

Per MCO P1400.32<Citation source="MCO P1400.32" /> Volume 2.

### Quarterly Promotion Cycles

Reserve enlisted promote on quarterly cycles.

- Q1 (October), Q2 (January), Q3 (April), Q4 (July) effective dates
- Specific eligibility per grade
- Composite scores for E-2 through E-5

### Composite Score Cutting

E-2 through E-5 promote based on composite score.

- Score above cutting score earns promotion
- Cutting scores published quarterly
- Updated for each cycle

### SNCO Selection Boards

E-6 (Staff Sergeant) and above promote through selection boards.

- Annual or biennial boards depending on grade
- HQMC convenes
- Specific Reserve consideration

### Promotion Eligibility

Per MCO P1400.32<Citation source="MCO P1400.32" /> Volume 2.

- Time in grade requirements
- Time in service requirements
- MOS qualification
- Performance evaluations
- Required training completion

## Documentation Required for Enlisted SELRES

- Service Record Book (SRB) - not Officer Qualification Record
- Pro/Con marks per quarter
- Page 11 entries
- Promotion warrants
- School certificates
- Awards documentation

## Coordination Touchpoints

Enlisted SELRES management touches.

- **HQMC Enlisted Manpower**: For policy and SNCO board processing
- **MARFORRES G-1**: For specific Reserve enlisted matters
- **Member's enlisted chain of command**: For unit-level enlisted actions
- **Career schools**: For specific course attendance

## Quality Control on Enlisted SELRES

Common errors.

1. **SRB vs OQR confusion.** Enlisted have SRB.
2. **Quarterly promotions missed.** Verify eligibility each cycle.
3. **Pro/Con marks gaps.** Verify quarterly marks completion.
4. **Career counseling skipped.** Periodic counseling supports career progression.

## Related Roles

Reserve enlisted specifics affect every enlisted SELRES Marine.

- **Marines** progress through enlisted ranks toward SNCO and beyond.
- **Leaders** (NCOs and SNCOs) lead drilling Marines and mentor juniors.
- **Commanders** at I&I support enlisted career development.
