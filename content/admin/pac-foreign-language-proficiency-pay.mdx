## What FLPP Is

Per 37 USC 316<Citation source="37 USC 316" /> and DOD FMR Volume 7A<Citation source="DOD FMR Volume 7A" /> Chapter 19, Foreign Language Proficiency Pay (FLPP) compensates members proficient in foreign languages valuable to DOD. FLPP supports retention of language-skilled members and incentivizes language proficiency development.

FLPP can be substantial. The rate varies by language demand and proficiency level.

## Languages and Categories

DOD designates languages by strategic importance. Categories shift based on operational needs.

### Strategic Languages (Highest Demand)

Higher payment rates apply.

- Arabic (multiple dialects)
- Chinese (Mandarin and others)
- Dari, Pashto, Farsi
- Russian
- Korean
- Specific languages per current strategic priorities

### High-Demand Languages

Mid-tier payment rates.

- French (specific contexts)
- Spanish (specific contexts)
- Other languages per current designations

### Lower-Demand Languages

Lower or no FLPP for general proficiency. May apply for specific operational use.

DOD updates the designated language list periodically.

## DLPT - The Test

The Defense Language Proficiency Test (DLPT) is the standard measurement.

### DLPT Format

- Listening proficiency test
- Reading proficiency test
- Some languages also have speaking tests
- Members test once for each language they claim

### DLPT Levels

- Level 0: No proficiency
- Level 1: Elementary proficiency
- Level 2: Limited working proficiency
- Level 3: General professional proficiency
- Level 4: Advanced professional proficiency
- Level 5: Functionally native proficiency

Higher levels earn higher FLPP rates.

## FLPP Rate Structure

Per DOD FMR Volume 7A<Citation source="DOD FMR Volume 7A" /> Chapter 19, FLPP rates vary based on.

- Language category (high-demand vs lower-demand)
- DLPT scores in listening and reading
- Whether member is in language-required billet

### Example Rate Tiers

| Combined DLPT Score | Approximate Monthly Rate |
|---------------------|--------------------------|
| 2/2 in lower demand | Lower rate |
| 2/2 in high demand | Mid rate |
| 3/3 in high demand | Higher rate |
| 3/3 in strategic | Highest rate |

Specific amounts updated annually. Reference current DOD FMR.

## Eligibility Requirements

A Marine receives FLPP when meeting these.

- Active duty status
- Documented DLPT scores at minimum required levels
- Language is on current FLPP-eligible list
- DLPT scores are current (typically annually)
- Member uses or maintains the language

## Performance Steps for FLPP Processing

### Step 1 - Verify DLPT Qualification

Pull member's DLPT records.

- Verify recent DLPT scores in listening and reading
- Verify scores meet minimum thresholds
- Verify language is on FLPP-eligible list
- Note scores and levels

### Step 2 - Determine Rate

Per DOD FMR Volume 7A<Citation source="DOD FMR Volume 7A" /> Chapter 19.

- Look up rate for member's language category
- Apply rate based on combined DLPT scores
- Verify any specific assignment-based modifiers

### Step 3 - Process UD/MIPS Transaction

Apply FLPP via UD/MIPS.

- Specific FLPP transaction code per MCTFSCODESMAN
- Effective date of qualifying scores
- Monthly recurring pay activated

### Step 4 - Verify EDFR

Confirm processing within 5 business days.

- FLPP posts at correct rate
- Member sees FLPP on next LES

### Step 5 - Track Annual DLPT

For continued FLPP.

- Member must retest annually (typical cycle)
- Track each member's DLPT expiration date
- Coordinate retest scheduling
- Process FLPP changes based on new scores

## DLPT Annual Cycle

FLPP requires current DLPT.

- DLPT scores are current for typically 1 year
- Member retests annually to maintain pay
- Higher scores increase pay
- Lower scores decrease pay
- Failure to test stops FLPP

The annual retest is essential.

## When FLPP Stops or Changes

FLPP changes when.

- Member retests with different scores
- Annual currency expires without retest
- Language removed from FLPP-eligible list
- Member transfers from language-related duty (in some specific cases)
- Member separates or retires

## Common FLPP Errors

These hit during PAC processing.

1. **DLPT expired but FLPP continues.** Annual retest missed. Stop FLPP after currency expires until retested.
2. **Rate not updated after retest.** Member retested with higher scores but rate not increased. Process rate update.
3. **FLPP not started after qualifying.** Member achieved qualifying scores but FLPP never started. Process retroactive start.
4. **Wrong rate for language category.** Language category may have shifted. Verify current category and rate.
5. **FLPP applied to non-eligible language.** Language not on current FLPP list. Verify and correct.

## Multiple Language FLPP

A member may receive FLPP for multiple languages.

- Each language has its own rate
- Combined FLPP for multiple languages may apply
- Specific stacking rules per DOD FMR
- Total FLPP capped at maximum per DOD provisions

## FLPP and Other Pays

FLPP coordinates with other special pays.

- BAH applies independently
- BAS applies independently
- HFP applies if combat zone
- SDAP applies if member also in special duty
- Each pay is separate

## Audit Implications

FLPP is audit-relevant.

- FLPP without current DLPT creates findings
- Wrong rate creates compounding pay error
- FLPP for non-eligible language creates findings

PAC accuracy on FLPP supports unit audit posture.

## DLPT Resources

Members access DLPT through.

- Defense Language Institute (DLI) at Monterey, California
- DLPT-certified test centers worldwide
- Online DLPT scheduling
- Specific testing periods at member's installation

I&I admin coordinates testing for reservist members.

## Documentation Required

For FLPP actions, PAC needs.

- DLPT score reports (current within annual cycle)
- Member's qualifying language documentation
- Annual currency verification
- Coordination with command for language-required assignments

## Quality Control

Before submitting FLPP transactions.

- Verify DLPT scores current
- Verify language on FLPP-eligible list
- Verify rate per current DOD FMR
- Verify effective dates correct

## Coordination Touchpoints

FLPP processing touches.

- **DLI Foreign Language Center**: For DLPT testing
- **HQMC Manpower**: For language-required billet assignments
- **Member's chain of command**: For language use verification
- **Local installation testing center**: For DLPT scheduling

## Related Roles

FLPP affects language-qualified Marines.

- **Marines** maintain DLPT currency and verify FLPP on LES.
- **Leaders** support member language testing and currency.
- **Commanders** at language-required units track member FLPP eligibility.
