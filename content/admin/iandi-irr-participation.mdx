## IRR Participation Requirements

Per MCRAMM Chapter 8 and Chapter 4 Section 2.2, IRR members maintain specific participation requirements.

### Annual Muster

Per 10 USC 10206<Citation source="10 USC 10206" />.

- Annual screening contact with member
- Verify member status and contact information
- Update readiness data
- Earn membership points (15 per anniversary year)

### Current Address

- Member must maintain current address with HQMC/MCRSC
- Address updates via MOL
- Annual verification through muster

### DEERS Maintenance

- DEERS enrollment current
- Family member DEERS as applicable
- Specific dependent updates

### SGLI Election

- Current SGLI election
- Specific changes for life events
- Annual review

### Readiness Data

- Periodic readiness data submissions
- Mobilization availability information
- Specific reporting requirements

## IRR Membership Points

Per MCRAMM Chapter 4.

### 15 Points Per Anniversary Year

Members earn membership points for IRR status.

- Counts toward retirement satisfactory year
- Combined with active duty days for total points
- Specific calculation per anniversary year

### Earning Membership Points

Member earns points by maintaining IRR participation requirements.

- Annual muster completion
- Address maintenance
- DEERS and SGLI current

### Not Sufficient for Satisfactory Year

15 points alone is not enough for satisfactory year.

- 50 points minimum for satisfactory year
- Member must combine with other point sources for satisfactory year
- IRR alone might not produce satisfactory years

## Failure to Participate

Per MCRAMM, members failing to maintain IRR participation requirements might face consequences.

### Loss of Membership Points

Failure to complete annual muster.

- No 15 membership points for anniversary year
- Affects retirement calculations
- Specific recovery procedures

### Continued Mobilization Subject

Even failing IRR members remain subject to mobilization.

- Mobilization authority continues
- Member's failure to participate does not exempt
- Specific procedures during mobilization

### Possible Status Change

Long-term failure might trigger status change.

- Possible transfer to Standby Reserve
- Specific procedures per MCRSC
- Member's situation evaluated

### Possible Discharge

Severe non-participation might result in discharge.

- Specific discharge procedures
- Discharge characterization based on circumstances
- Coordinate with HQMC

## Common IRR Participation Issues

### Member Unaware of Requirements

IRR member doesn't know participation required.

- Brief member on requirements
- Provide written materials
- Connect with MCRSC

### Address Out of Date

Member's address not current.

- Update via MOL
- Contact MCRSC for assistance
- Verify update posted

### Annual Muster Not Completed

Member missed muster.

- Specific procedures with MCRSC
- Might affect membership points
- Coordinate completion

### Member Wants to Return to SMCR

IRR member desiring to return to drilling.

- Coordinate local SMCR unit join procedures
- Verify availability of billet
- Process unit assignment

## Coordination Touchpoints

IRR participation processing primarily touches.

- **Marine Corps Reserve Support Command (MCRSC)**: Primary IRR administration
- **HQMC RA**: For policy matters
- **MOL**: For self-service updates
- **Local SMCR units**: For SMCR return

## Related Roles

IRR participation primarily affects IRR members directly.

- **Marines** in IRR maintain participation requirements.
- **Leaders** at SMCR units rarely engage IRR participation.
- **Commanders** at I&I support occasional IRR member contacts.
