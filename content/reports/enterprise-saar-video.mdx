## What you learn

This walkthrough takes you through the System Authorization Access Request for Enterprise Reports. Watch it before you submit the form.

## When to use

- New Marine arriving at the S-1 shop with no Reports access.
- Returning Marine whose access lapsed and needs a fresh SAAR on file.
