## What This Page Will Cover

- Eligibility (Chapter 33 service requirements)
- Percentage tiers (40% to 100% based on aggregate service)
- 36 months of benefits
- Tuition and fees paid directly to school
- Monthly Housing Allowance (MHA) at E-5 with dependents BAH rate
- Books and supplies stipend ($1,000 annual)
- Yellow Ribbon Program for high-cost schools
- Transfer of Education Benefits (TEB) to spouse and children
- 4-year service obligation for TEB
- Activation timing at separation
- Coordination with TA during active service
