## Why You Read Your LES

Per MCO 5000.14D Enclosure 2, you own the accuracy of your pay information. The LES is the system of record for what you got paid. Errors that go unreported become harder to fix the longer they sit. Pay debts compound interest under the Statement of Service.

Pull your LES every month from MyPay (https://mypay.dfas.mil). Review it before you spend the deposit.

## Where to Get Your LES

- **MyPay**: Primary source. Login with CAC or username and password.
- **MCTFS via MOL**: Backup source. Shows the same data with different formatting.
- **Hard copy**: S-1 prints on request. Last resort.

## What to Check Each Month

Five sections matter most.

### Top Block - Personal Data

Verify these match your record.

- Name, SSN, rank, pay grade
- Years of service
- ETS or EAS date
- Pay entry base date (PEBD)

If any are wrong, your pay calculation is wrong. Report immediately.

### Entitlements (What You Earned)

Common lines you should see.

| Line | What It Is | Common Issue |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| BASE PAY | Your monthly base pay by grade and years | Wrong rank or YOS posted |
| BAH | Housing allowance | Wrong rate, dependent status, or zip code |
| BAS | Food allowance | Should not draw if on meal card |
| FSA | Family Separation Allowance ($250/month) | Should pay during deployment over 30 days |
| HFP/IDP | Hostile Fire / Imminent Danger Pay | Combat zone designation |
| SDA | Special Duty Assignment Pay | Drill instructor, recruiter, etc. |

### Deductions (What Was Taken Out)

| Line | What It Is | Watch For |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| FED TAXES | Federal income tax | Check your W-4 election |
| FICA | Social Security and Medicare | Should be 7.65% of taxable pay |
| STATE TAXES | State tax based on legal residence | Verify legal residence is correct |
| SGLI | Service Group Life Insurance | Should match your election (max $500K) |
| TSP | Thrift Savings Plan contribution | Should match your election |
| AFRH | Armed Forces Retirement Home ($0.50) | Standard, not optional |

### Leave Block

Your leave balance shows.

- Beginning balance
- Earned (2.5 days per month)
- Used
- Lost (over 60 days at end of FY, you lose it)
- Ending balance

Cap is 60 days at end of fiscal year. Special Leave Accrual lets you carry up to 120 days under specific conditions.

### Remarks Block

Important entries appear here.

- Pay debts and collection schedules
- BAH or BAS audit notes
- TSP catch-up contributions
- Allotment changes

## Common Pay Errors

These hit most often.

1. **BAH stopped after PCS or move.** New zip code or dependent status not updated. Submit NAVMC 10922 if dependent change. Update zip code via MOL or PCS orders.
2. **BAS taken away with meal card.** If you're on a meal card you do not draw BAS. If you turned in your meal card and BAS did not start, report to S-1.
3. **Special pay missing.** Jump pay, dive pay, or SDA missing after qualification or assignment. Submit jump records, school cert, or assignment orders to S-1.
4. **FSA not paid during deployment.** If deployed over 30 days you should draw $250 per month. Submit DD 1561.
5. **Wrong tax state.** State of legal residence drives state tax. Update via DD 2058 if you changed residence.

## How to Report Errors

Follow this escalation path.

1. **First**: Talk to your S-1 admin clerk. Most errors fix at this level.
2. **Second**: If S-1 cannot resolve, request the Personnel Officer (0170) review.
3. **Third**: If still unresolved, submit through your command to IPAC.
4. **Fourth**: For policy disputes, email MCAAT at ask_mcaat@usmc.mil.
5. **Last resort**: IG complaint or congressional inquiry.

## Documentation to Keep

Keep digital copies of these for at least 7 years.

- Every monthly LES
- All NAVMC 10922 submissions and approvals
- All special pay documentation (jump records, school certs)
- All travel orders and vouchers
- All TSP and allotment changes

This is your personal ready book. If S-1 says they "do not have your documents," you have them.

## Related Roles

This basic Marine task gets executed differently up the chain.

- **Leaders** verify their Marines are reviewing LES monthly and coach them on what to look for.
- **Admin** at S-1 and PAC process the corrections you submit.
- **Commanders** review the command payroll monthly per MCO 5000.14D Enclosure 3 and report discrepancies to IPAC.
