## Start Here If You Are New to DAP

DIRECT AFFILIATION PROGRAM (DAP) is the Active-to-Reserve transition tool that affiliates departing active duty Marines DIRECTLY with a Selected Marine Corps Reserve (SMCR) unit. Year-round submission window. Submit NET 365 days prior to EAS, NLT 90 days prior to EAS. Affiliation Bonus per MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" /> pays $20,000 to $50,000 depending on rank, MOS, and obligation period for eligible MOSs. SMCR unit billet must be available within commuting distance or relocation plan. Career Counselor on the active side coordinates with Reserve Affiliation Counselor (RAC) on the Reserve side. DAP does NOT require break in service. Marine transitions from active to Reserve status with continuous service. Per MCO 1040.31<Citation source="MCO 1040.31" />, MCO 1001R.1L<Citation source="MCO 1001R.1L" />, and MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" />.

This page walks you through DAP from your seat as a transitioning Marine. By the end you will know.

- The DAP framework and purpose
- Who is DAP eligible
- The submission window (year-round)
- The Affiliation Bonus per MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" />
- SMCR unit billet matching
- The Career Counselor and RAC partnership
- Active-to-Reserve transition logistics
- Continued benefits and obligations

## What DAP Is

DAP is the streamlined path for active duty Marines who plan to leave active service and want to continue serving in the Marine Corps Reserve. It transitions you DIRECTLY to a Selected Marine Corps Reserve (SMCR) unit billet without a break in service.

### DAP vs Other Transition Paths

Three patterns exist for active-to-Reserve transition. DAP is the most streamlined.

- DAP. Direct affiliation to SMCR unit. No break in service. Bonus eligible. Year-round submission.
- IRR (Individual Ready Reserve) and standard transition. Marine separates and joins Reserve later. Break in service. No DAP bonus.
- Civilian then Reserve. Full separation. Marine reaffiliates later through prior service Reserve recruiter.

DAP is the cleanest path for Marines who know they want to continue in the Reserve.

## Who Is DAP Eligible

Per MCO 1040.31<Citation source="MCO 1040.31" /> and MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" />.

### Basic Eligibility

- Active duty Marine within the submission window (NET 365 days, NLT 90 days from EAS)
- No derogatory information that would disqualify Reserve service
- Current PFT and CFT (passing)
- MOS qualified for the SMCR unit billet OR willing to lateral move
- Recommended by chain of command
- Medically and administratively cleared

### Bonus Eligibility

The Affiliation Bonus per MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" /> requires.

- MOS on the eligible MOS list (varies by FY)
- Available SMCR unit billet matching the MOS
- Obligation period commitment (typically 3 or 6 years in SMCR)
- Rank on the eligible rank list

Career Counselor and RAC confirm bonus eligibility before submission.

## The Submission Window

DAP submission is YEAR-ROUND. This is different from FTAP and STAP, which have specific FY windows.

### Submission Timing

- NET (No Earlier Than). 365 days prior to EAS.
- NLT (No Later Than). 90 days prior to EAS.
- Recommended. 6 to 9 months prior to EAS for best billet matching.

Earlier submission gives more billet options. Later submission risks missing preferred billet.

### Why The Window Matters

- Billet matching takes time. SMCR units have specific MOS and rank requirements.
- Bonus contract execution requires lead time.
- Marine may need to relocate. Relocation timing affects civilian employment, family, and housing.
- Active duty separation paperwork and Reserve enlistment paperwork must align.

## The Affiliation Bonus

Per MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" /> (Reserve Affiliation Bonus Program), eligible MOSs receive an Affiliation Bonus.

### Bonus Amounts

Bonus amounts range from $20,000 to $50,000 depending on.

- Rank at time of affiliation
- MOS on eligible list
- Obligation period (3 or 6 years in SMCR)
- Critical or hard-to-fill MOS designation

Specific amounts and tier structure are published in MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" /> and updated periodically. Career Counselor and RAC confirm current amount for your case.

### Bonus Payment

- Paid in installments tied to drill attendance and contract anniversaries
- Initial installment upon affiliation contract execution
- Subsequent installments at 12-month intervals or per MARADMIN guidance
- Recoupment applies if Marine fails to fulfill SMCR obligation

### Service Obligation

The bonus carries a service obligation in the SMCR.

- Typically 3 or 6 years of drilling status
- Drill attendance requirements (typically one weekend per month, two weeks per year)
- Mobilization availability
- Bonus recoupment if obligation not met

## SMCR Unit Billet Matching

Affiliation requires an AVAILABLE billet at an SMCR unit. The match depends on.

### Geographic Considerations

- Commuting distance from your home of record or future residence
- Relocation willingness
- Major SMCR concentrations (Reserve sites across CONUS)

### MOS Match

- Direct MOS match preferred
- Lateral move possible if MOS unavailable but you qualify for another MOS the unit needs
- LATMOV bonus may apply per current MARADMIN

### Rank Match

- Billet authorized rank must align with your current rank
- Some flexibility for senior or junior fits depending on unit needs

## The Career Counselor And RAC Partnership

DAP cases involve TWO counselors working together.

### The Active-Side Career Counselor

Your unit Career Counselor handles.

- Initial DAP discussion and eligibility screening
- NAVMC 11537<Citation source="NAVMC 11537" /> RELM preparation
- Active duty separation documentation alignment
- Coordination with RAC for billet matching
- Tracking through the active duty side of the transition

### The Reserve Affiliation Counselor (RAC)

The RAC is a Reserve-side specialist who handles.

- SMCR billet matching
- Affiliation contract preparation
- Reserve unit liaison
- Bonus contract execution
- Initial drill schedule setup
- Reserve-side onboarding

### How They Work Together

- Career Counselor refers you to the RAC after initial eligibility screening
- RAC searches for matching billets
- Career Counselor and RAC align documentation and timelines
- Active duty separation and Reserve enlistment execute on aligned dates

You should know both counselors by name during your DAP case.

## The Submission Process

### Step 1. Initial Discussion With Career Counselor

12 to 18 months from EAS, raise DAP intent with your Career Counselor. Career Counselor.

- Confirms basic eligibility
- Explains DAP framework
- Discusses bonus eligibility for your MOS and rank
- Refers you to the RAC

### Step 2. Connect With Reserve Affiliation Counselor

The RAC works your billet matching.

- You provide preferred geographic locations
- RAC searches SMCR billets
- RAC presents available options
- You and RAC discuss MOS and rank fit

### Step 3. Complete Documentation

Once a billet is identified.

- NAVMC 11537<Citation source="NAVMC 11537" /> RELM with DAP designation
- Affiliation contract documentation
- Bonus contract documentation (if eligible)
- Active duty separation paperwork alignment

### Step 4. Approval And Execution

- Active duty side approves separation
- Reserve side approves affiliation contract
- Marine separates from active duty and enlists in SMCR on aligned dates
- First drill weekend with new SMCR unit

## Continued Benefits And Obligations

### What Continues

- Service in the Marine Corps. You remain a Marine, just in Reserve status.
- Drill pay (per drill attended, plus annual training)
- Reserve healthcare options (TRICARE Reserve Select)
- GI Bill education benefits eligibility for drilling Reservists
- Continued eligibility for promotion in the Reserve component
- Time in service accrual

### What Changes

- You hold civilian employment as your primary occupation
- You drill one weekend per month and two weeks per year (typical)
- Your active duty pay and allowances stop (replaced by drill pay)
- Your active duty BAH stops
- Your TRICARE Prime ends (TRICARE Reserve Select option)

### Mobilization

As an SMCR Marine, you remain subject to mobilization. Per current Reserve component guidance, mobilization is possible based on operational needs.

## Common Questions

### Can I DAP With Any MOS

Eligibility depends on SMCR billet availability and current bonus MOS list. If your active MOS is not on the bonus list, DAP without bonus is still possible if a billet is available.

### Can I DAP To Any Location

Geographic options depend on SMCR unit locations. Major concentrations exist in CONUS. RAC presents options based on your preferences.

### Can I DAP And Then Go Back To Active Duty

Active component recall from Reserve is possible through specific programs but is not guaranteed. Treat DAP as a Reserve commitment.

### Does DAP Affect My GI Bill

Drilling Reservists retain GI Bill eligibility. Use of benefits during Reserve service follows current VA guidance.

### What If I Cannot Find A Matching Billet

If no matching billet exists in your preferred area, options include.

- Wait for billet availability (year-round window helps)
- Consider broader geographic options
- Lateral move to a different MOS
- Standard separation and later reaffiliation through Reserve recruiter

## Quick Reference

- Submission window. NET 365 days, NLT 90 days from EAS, year-round.
- Bonus. $20,000 to $50,000 per MARADMIN 023/26<Citation source="MARADMIN 023/26" />, MOS and rank dependent.
- Obligation. 3 or 6 years in SMCR typically.
- Counselors. Active-side Career Counselor plus Reserve-side RAC.
- Billet match. Available SMCR billet required, MOS and rank aligned.
- No break in service. Direct active-to-Reserve transition.
- Drilling. One weekend per month, two weeks per year (typical).
