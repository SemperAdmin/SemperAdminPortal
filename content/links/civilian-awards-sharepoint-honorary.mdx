## Honorary Awards Submission

This SharePoint application handles submission and tracking for all civilian honorary award nomination packages, including:

- Navy Superior Civilian Service Award
- Navy Distinguished Civilian Service Award
- All higher-level awards administered by the Department of Defense and the Department of the Navy

Effective per MARADMIN 280/26, dated 22 Jun 2026. This replaces all previous email-based submission procedures.

Point of contact: MPC-40 Labor and Employee Relations (SMB_CIVILIAN_AWARDS@usmc.mil)
