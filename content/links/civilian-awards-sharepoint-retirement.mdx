## Retirement and Career Service Awards Submission

This SharePoint application handles submission and tracking for:

- Secretary of the Navy (SECNAV) Length of Service Awards (40+ years of Federal service)
- Commandant of the Marine Corps (CMC) retirement letters (30+ years of Federal service)
- Secretary of the Navy retirement certificates (40+ years of Federal service)

Effective per MARADMIN 280/26, dated 22 Jun 2026. This replaces all previous email-based submission procedures.

Point of contact: MPC-40 Labor and Employee Relations (SMB_CIVILIAN_AWARDS@usmc.mil)
