## Order summary

This order governs the full separation lifecycle: voluntary release, EAS-driven separation, retirement, and involuntary actions.

- Mandatory TAP attendance window: not later than 365 days before EAS.
- Pre-separation counseling required at the 24, 12, and 3 month gates.
- DD-214 issuance procedure documented in chapter 6.

## Decision authority

Battalion commander signs all routine separations. General court-martial convening authority retains involuntary action.
