## When to submit

- On every PCS in or out of a unit.
- After any change in marital status, dependents, or beneficiaries.
- During the annual record review window.

## Common rejection reasons

Routing errors, missing signature blocks, and outdated SGLI elections drive 80 percent of rejections.
