## What changed

The Marine Corps updated three entitlement categories effective 1 January 2025.

- BAH rates rose 5.4 percent on average across the continental United States.
- BAS climbed to 465.77 dollars for enlisted, 320.78 dollars for officers.
- Hardship duty pay for designated billets adjusted upward by 50 dollars.

## Action for Marines

Verify your LES against the new rates within 30 days. Submit a NAVMC 11000<Citation source="NAVMC 11000" /> if your entries do not match.

## Action for admin specialists

Run a section-wide audit for hardship duty entries during the first pay period of the year.
